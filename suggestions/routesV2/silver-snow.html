Silver Snow — Route Plan V2

Route 3 of 4 · Black Eagles → Church. The Black Eagles students fighting for the Church without Edelgard. Difficulty: Maddening — we run the full optimal 5-skill set on every carry (R12) and route every unit through mastery classes deliberately.
House: Black Eagles (Church-aligned) · Difficulty: Maddening (R12: full optimal kit) · Roster: 15 (fills 12 deploy + 3 adjutants, R7). SS has the deepest pool in the game (Church units + Black Eagles defectors + cross-house), so the 14th–15th 🪑 come essentially free: Petra (sword/flying flex) and Dorothea (magic/dance flex) auto-join as Black Eagles defectors — run them as adjutant/insurance bodies.
Byleth (R3 — distinct): Falcon Knight (♀). Falcon Knight is FEMALE-ONLY, and Female Byleth is required for the Byleth × Seteth S-support (Seteth romances female Byleth only) — so Byleth flies a Lance/Flying chassis here rather than the male-only War Master. Distinct from R1 (Enlightened One), R2 (War Master), R4 (Wyvern Lord) — all four Byleth classes differ.

Bonus: other units' birthdays give a free, auto-accepted tea (no C-support gate) for cheap support on recruit/S-targets.

Roster composition (R9 — built as a team, Maddening discipline)

  • Damage split (balanced for Maddening). Magic: Lysithea (Gremory, premier nuke), Hanneman (Dark Knight, mounted second nuke), Linhardt (Warlock chip), Flayn (Bishop chip) handle armor / low-Res. Physical: Byleth (Falcon Knight, flying lance), Seteth (Wyvern Lord, Swift Strikes), Catherine (Swordmaster, Thunderbrand crit), Shamir (Bow Knight, 1–3 range), Cyril / Ferdinand / Ingrid (Wyvern Lords), Alois (War Master).
  • Healing / staff. Flayn (Bishop) + Manuela (Dancer who keeps Faith for emergency Physic) + Linhardt (Warlock retains some white magic). This is the thinnest area on Maddening → keep Flayn alive and parked.
  • Fliers / mobility. Byleth + Seteth + Cyril + Ferdinand + Ingrid give a deep air corps; Shamir / Hanneman (mounted) give Canto; Manuela's Dance is the tempo enabler. Excellent Maddening mobility.
  • Ranged / effective coverage. Shamir (Bow Knight, anti-flier, 1–3 range) + Lysithea / Hanneman / Linhardt magic (anti-armor) + Cyril / Ferdinand bow option. Strong coverage in every direction.
  • Gambits / Authority. Byleth + Catherine + Seteth carry the strongest battalions; gambits matter more here than R1/R2 — bring rattle / Stride. Manuela's Dance is the key action-economy lever for the Maddening spike.
  • Featuring notes. Ferdinand → Wyvern Lord (reuse-variant vs CF Paladin, adds a flier); Shamir → Bow Knight (reuse-variant vs VW Sniper); Hanneman → Dark Knight (reuse-variant vs CF Warlock, mounts the second nuke). Alois is featured ONLY here as War Master — community rates War Master his one genuinely good class, so we showcase him once rather than force a weaker reuse-variant on Verdant Wind.

💃 Dancer this route: Manuela

A minority-but-sound, community-backed pick (R4). Manuela has ~60% Speed and a Sword boon (Dancer's Sword Avo+20 makes her a fine dodge unit), and because this roster is otherwise healer-light, her ability to Physic-heal between refreshes is a real bonus the other dancers can't match. Converting her redundant combat slot into the refresher is the highest-value use of her seat. Distinct from R1/R2/R4 dancers (Dorothea / Petra / Yuri or Marianne).

🪙 Stealer this route: Catherine

Run one Thief-master to Steal rare items off enemies — Dark Seal, stat boosters, consumables — the only repeatable way to farm them. Catherine is ideal: Sword boon + high Speed Swordmaster (Steal is Speed-gated — only foes with lower Spd) → cheap Thief detour; master it so Steal stays equippable in her final class. Shamir is a fine backup. Full mechanics → facts: Thief.

Recruitment plan & timing (R11)

Recruit selection (R11.1) — identical procedure on every route: forced free core (house + 🔒 + Byleth) → compatible-gated recruits → too-good-to-miss exceptions (support-waiver) → fill to 15 from the free Lv15-only pool by role → combat-ready → lowest reuse → alphabetical → exclude off-build non-essentials. Target size 15 (Crimson Flower capped at 12–13).

🆓 Free / near-free recruits (Church route — the deepest pool). The Black Eagles who don't follow Edelgard auto-join for free — beyond the core, scoop up Caspar and Bernadetta as no-cost extra bodies/adjutants. The faculty & Knights (Shamir, Catherine, Alois, Manuela, Hanneman) join for just Byleth Lv15 + a talk (no skill gate). The only real gates are Lysithea (Mag 15 + Faith B — off-build but too good to miss; Faith is Byleth's budding talent) and Ingrid (Flying — which is on-build for Byleth's Falcon Knight here). Both are fine.

This run leans on Church/faculty units used almost nowhere in R1–R2:

  • Flayn 🔒 — recruit in Part 1 (story); stays.
  • Hanneman, Manuela — available from Ch.8 (faculty); recruit promptly.
  • Alois — joins ~Ch.11.
  • Seteth, Catherine, Cyril 🔒 — auto-join start of Ch.12 ("To War").
  • Lysithea (recruit) — cross-house mage; gate is Magic 15 + Faith B. R11 note: Faith is off-build for Byleth's Falcon Knight (Lancefaire build doesn't run white magic) — but Byleth has Faith as a budding talent, so the Faith B requirement can be raised cheaply via that budding line / support seminars and isn't a wasted off-build grind. Raise both early in Part 1.
  • Ferdinand, Ingrid, Linhardt (recruits) — student recruits to fill the bigger roster; recruit early in Part 1 so they level into the timeskip. (Ingrid / Linhardt are flex.)
