Route 1 — Azure Moon (Blue Lions) V2

House: Blue Lions · Difficulty: Normal · Byleth: ♀ · Birthday: early Harpstring Moon (M5), wk 1.
Run 1 of 4 — the warm-up. Blue Lions is the strongest house and the best place to learn the systems on Normal.
Female Byleth is required for the iconic Byleth × Dimitri S-support (Dimitri romances female Byleth only).
🎂 Birthday — set early Harpstring Moon (Month 5), on an exploration week. Dimitri mails the Blue Lion Brooch (Charm +2); because the date recurs after the timeskip you collect it in both Part 1 and Part 2 (two copies) — but only if the birthday falls on a week you're actually at the monastery, so avoid mission/locked weeks. Charm punches above its weight: it decides the White Heron Cup (your Dancer), eases Charm-gated recruits (Dorothea needs Charm 25), and raises gambit Hit & damage — so equip the two brooches on Dorothea (Dancer / Cup) and Byleth (recruiter). Bonus: on other units' birthdays a tea party with them is free & auto-accepted (no C-support gate) — spend recruit / S-support targets' birthdays to bank support cheaply.
Byleth class this run: Enlightened One (啟明之神) — auto-granted at the end of Ch.10, zero certification. Sword + Brawl boons, magic access, 6 Mov foot. Needs no Faculty Training spent on its own gate.
Roster size: 15 (10 core + 3 flex + 2 adjutant/insurance) — enough to fill the 12-unit max deployment + 3 adjutants (R7). The 14th–15th 🪑 are low-priority faculty recruits, bench/adjutant only with no real investment on Normal: Alois (War Master — spare axe body) and Manuela (Holy Knight — a backup healer, which R11.1 prefers over a redundant 3rd mage). R12: Normal = lean kit.

Roster composition (R9 — built as a team)

  • Damage split: physical-leaning house, deliberately balanced. Magic (Mercedes, Annette, + Byleth's magic access) punishes armor / low-Res; physical (Dimitri, Felix, Sylvain, Ingrid, Ashe, Dedue) carries the line. Two dedicated mages is plenty for Normal.
  • Healing / staff: Mercedes (Gremory, primary) carries Physic / Fortify / Warp; Annette (Warlock) backs her with Faith chip and the Rally suite.
  • Fliers / mobility: Ingrid (Falcon Knight) is the dedicated flier (a 2nd isn't needed on Normal); Sylvain (Paladin) + Dimitri (cavalry line) + Shamir (Bow Knight, Canto) provide mounted mobility. Annette's Rallies amplify the push.
  • Ranged / effective coverage: Ashe (Sniper, anti-flier bows) is the bow anchor; Shamir (free Bow Knight recruit) backs him up at 1–3 range; Mercedes / Annette magic covers anti-armor and 1–2 range.
  • Gambits / Authority: Byleth + Dimitri carry the best battalions; Annette's Rallies amplify. On Normal, gambits are insurance, not a crutch.
  • Composition picks flagged below: Felix → Swordmaster (not his community #1 War Master); Sylvain → Paladin (lower-investment than community-#1 Wyvern); Annette → Warlock (a pure-mage anti-armor seat over her community-#1 Wyvern Lord); plus the flex picks (Catherine, and Shamir — a free Lv15 recruit replacing the off-build Leonie).

Normal-mode lean kit (R12) — read this before the skill table. On Normal, enemy hit and enemy damage are both low, so the optimal equipped set is short: run just Faire + Death Blow (or Fiendish Blow / Darting Blow) + the unit's signature combat art, and fill the rest with whatever is already free. Keep Hit+20 only where it is a boon-cheap harvest (chiefly Ashe, who certs Bow for Archer anyway). Treat avoid / mitigation / enemy-phase skills (Alert Stance+, Battalion Wrath / Vantage, Avoid+10, White Magic Avo+20) as optional flair — nice for teaching the systems, never required to win.

Recruitment plan & timing (R11)

Recruit selection (R11.1) — identical procedure on every route: forced free core (house + 🔒 + Byleth) → compatible-gated recruits → too-good-to-miss exceptions (support-waiver) → fill to 15 from the free Lv15-only pool by role → combat-ready → lowest reuse → alphabetical → exclude off-build non-essentials. Target size 15 (Crimson Flower capped at 12–13).

Blue Lions lacks magic and fliers, so recruit to fill those gaps. Master rule for this run: Byleth raises skills only via combat + Faculty Training — never from lectures/instruction (those train students, not the teacher). So meet stat gates from free/passive sources first (gardening = Str, Choir = Byleth Authority, deploying = Authority/Charm) and build support to lower each gate (~20%/level). For a skill gate Byleth's build never uses (e.g. Lance for Leonie), the only way up is Faculty Training — Enlightened One needs no cert, so your FT is free to point there, but it's a slow, multi-month grind, so prefer support or deprioritize the recruit.

🆓 Free / near-free recruits worth grabbing (Blue Lions route). These join for just Byleth Level 15 + a talk — no stat or skill gate, so pick up any you like as free bodies, adjutants, or support partners: Catherine (Lv11 Swordmaster, combat-ready), Shamir (anti-flier Bow Knight, the flex), Alois & Manuela (the two adjutants above — Manuela edges out Hanneman by R11.1's role-first rule), and Hanneman / Anna 🧩 (DLC) as further free bodies if you want them. Dorothea is also effectively free here — her Authority gate is Byleth's boon. There's no reason not to scoop the free ones up.

⚠️ No tea parties before Ch.4. Tea unlocks only at Ch.4 via Ferdinand's "Tea for Two" quest (else it auto-unlocks Ch.13). Early support (Ch.1–3) runs on greetings, gifts, lost items, shared meals, and group tasks; add tea once it opens.
Choir gives NO motivation — only Faith EXP + Byleth Authority EXP + a small support bump.

Romance / pairings this route (R5)

Azure Moon is the first of the two easy routes, so per R5 it carries the one allowed diversity swap (Dedue × Mercedes). The paired-ending count is still maximized with real support chains; everything else is best-by-default.

  • Byleth S-support → Dimitri 🔒 — the iconic Azure Moon S-support; requires female Byleth.
  • Felix × Annette — best-by-default (confirmed in-house pairing). ✔ best
  • Sylvain × Ingrid — best-by-default (childhood-friend chain; confirmed). ✔ best
  • Ashe × Catherine — real support chain used to maximize the count (not a diversity swap). (If Catherine is benched, leave Ashe combat-only.)
  • Dedue × Mercedes — ⚠️ DIVERSITY pick — deliberately sub-optimal. Why it's a weak choice: it's neither unit's best partner. Dedue's strongest tie is Byleth (taken by Dimitri's S-support); Mercedes's best are Byleth / Sylvain (Sylvain is paired with Ingrid). The two also share little combat synergy — a foot wall + a frail backline healer rarely fight together. It's a real support chain used only to add variety and pad the paired-ending count, which is tolerable only because this is the Normal route. (Prefer to skip it? Leave Dedue unpaired — his best is unavailable anyway.)
  • Combat-only on this roster: Gilbert (closest tie is daughter Annette — not romantic), Dorothea (the dancer), Shamir (pair with Catherine if you field both — their signature chain; otherwise combat-only).

What a "diversity pairing" is, and why it's a bad choice (R5): it deliberately passes over a unit's strongest support for a weaker, different one — purely to show off variety across the four runs. You lose the best support's affinity/stat payoff and usually the combat synergy too, so it's a real cost. The plan only permits it on the easy routes (Azure Moon Normal first, then Crimson Flower Hard), never on Maddening. Here that swap is Dedue × Mercedes (both their best partners are already taken). Ashe × Catherine is not a diversity swap — it's a genuine best-available chain used to maximize the count.
