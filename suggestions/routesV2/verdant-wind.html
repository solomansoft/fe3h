Verdant Wind / 翠风之章 — Route Plan V2

House: Golden Deer · Difficulty: Maddening · Byleth: Female (required for the iconic Byleth × Claude S-support — Claude romances female Byleth only — and unlocks her Wyvern Lord path here).
Run 4 of 4: the finale and the hardest run, on full Maddening, and the most lore-complete route. Golden Deer is flier-light, so this plan closes the air gap with native Wyvern users and paths every unit through mastery classes on boon/neutral weapons while skipping bane-gated detours.
🎂 Birthday — set early Harpstring Moon (Month 5), on an exploration week. Claude mails the Golden Deer Bracelet (Charm +2), collected in both Parts (two copies) if the date lands on a monastery week (avoid mission/locked weeks). Charm decides the White Heron Cup (Dancer = Marianne, or Yuri on DLC), eases Charm-gated recruits, and raises gambit Hit/damage — equip the bracelets on your Dancer and Byleth. Female Byleth is required for the Byleth × Claude S-support. Bonus: other units' birthdays give a free, auto-accepted tea (no C-support gate) for cheap support.
Tags: 🔒 route-exclusive · ⚠️ tutor/special-care · 💃 dancer · ⭐ top carry · 🧩 DLC-optional (non-DLC alt given) · ⛏ HARVEST (mastery picked up via a boon/neutral cert detour).

The recommended default (you do not own the Cindered Shadows DLC) is the all-Golden-Deer non-DLC roster in the next section — it un-reserves Lysithea (native GD), uses Marianne as Dancer, and adds Mercedes as a dedicated healer. The DLC Ashen Wolves roster that follows is kept as an optional path for owners.

🔒 Route-exclusive highlight (verified)

Claude is playable only on Verdant Wind (NPC/enemy elsewhere, never controllable). Edelgard is again an enemy/major boss here.

Maddening tip: GD's three opening bow users (Claude, Leonie, Ignatz) give ranged safety from turn 1 — lean on it. The early Death Blow / Hit+20 harvests below sharpen exactly that opening.

V2 build basis

  • Design principle: route each unit's path through intermediate/advanced classes that certify on a boon or neutral weapon, and HARVEST (⛏) the masteries worth keeping along the way. Skip masteries gated behind a unit's BANE. Cert ranks used: Brigand = Axe C (→ Death Blow), Archer = Bow C (→ Hit+20), Mage = Reason C (→ Fiendish Blow), Pegasus Knight = Lance C + Flying D, FEMALE-ONLY (→ Darting Blow).
  • Gender locks enforced: Hero / Brawler / Grappler / War Master = MALE-ONLY; Falcon Knight / Pegasus Knight / Valkyrie / Dark Flier / Gremory = FEMALE-ONLY.
  • Byleth this run (R3 — distinct): Wyvern Lord (飛龍の長) — the strongest endgame physical chassis (Move 8 flying, Axefaire, Defiant Avo, Alert Stance+). Distinct from R1 Enlightened One, CF War Master, SS Falcon Knight.
  • Difficulty (R12): Maddening → full optimal kit for every unit.

🪙 Stealer this route: Leonie (non-DLC) / Yuri 🧩 (DLC)

Run one Thief-master to Steal rare items off enemies — Dark Seal, stat boosters, consumables — the only repeatable way to farm them (Steal is Speed-gated: only foes with lower Spd). Leonie's Spd 60 out-speeds almost any foe — detour her through Thief (Sword C, Lv10), master it, and Steal stays equippable in her Bow Knight build. With DLC, Yuri natively runs Thief → Trickster, the ideal built-in stealer. Full mechanics → facts: Thief.

Recruitment plan & timing (R11)

Recruit selection (R11.1) — identical procedure on every route: forced free core (house + 🔒 + Byleth) → compatible-gated recruits → too-good-to-miss exceptions (support-waiver) → fill to 15 from the free Lv15-only pool by role → combat-ready → lowest reuse → alphabetical → exclude off-build non-essentials. Target size 15 (Crimson Flower capped at 12–13).

🆓 Free / near-free recruits (Golden Deer route). GD's native pool is smaller, so lean on the Level-15-only recruits — Shamir, Catherine, Alois, Manuela, Hanneman each join for just Byleth Lv15 + a talk (no skill gate → fully Byleth-compatible). That's how the roster reaches 15: grab them as carries/adjutants. Anna 🧩 (DLC) is likewise free from Ch.3. Only Mercedes carries an off-build gate (see below).

  • Native GD (Claude, Lorenz, Leonie, Hilda, Marianne, Raphael, Ignatz, Lysithea) — no recruiting. Push Byleth + Hilda + Catherine toward Wyvern (Axe/Flying), Leonie toward Bow Knight, Lorenz toward Dark Knight, Lysithea toward Valkyrie. Start the Brigand/Archer/Mage harvest detours early so Death Blow / Hit+20 / Fiendish Blow land before the timeskip.
  • Non-DLC cross-house recruits — recruit early in Part 1: Shamir, Catherine, Alois (all Level-15-only, no skill gate → fully Byleth-compatible). Mercedes (the dedicated healer) is the exception: her gate Magic 15 + Bow C is OFF Byleth's Wyvern build, so recruit her via her own support (waive the gate) — not a Byleth Bow/Magic grind. She's kept as the one deliberate off-build recruit because she's too good to miss for healer-thin Golden Deer (R11).
  • Ashen Wolves DLC (Yuri, Balthus, Constance, Hapi) — recruitable into Part 1 only, each after the corresponding Cindered Shadows chapter (Constance Ch.2, Balthus Ch.4, Hapi Ch.5, Yuri Ch.6). They join ~Lv3 and become instant top-tier — recruit ASAP on Maddening.

⚠️ Tutor / special-care this run (full Maddening)

  • Lysithea ⚠️ (non-DLC) / Constance ⚠️ / Hapi ⚠️ (DLC) — frail nuke mages; body-block (Alois/Raphael), pair with the Dancer's refresh. Lysithea's Valkyrie Canto eases this.
  • Marianne ⚠️ — frail; stat boosters + dance from safety (or stay mounted as Holy Knight on DLC).
  • Mercedes ⚠️ — slow and squishy; keep behind the line, lean on Live to Serve + range; her job is healing, not the front.
  • Raphael ⚠️ — very low Spd; feed Speedwings, lean on Quick Riposte; never let mages reach him.
  • Hilda ⚠️ — lazy-base; concentrate her limited instruction on Axe/Flying only.
  • Wyvern grinders (Byleth, Catherine): Axe/Flying are neutral, so start Brigand → Wyvern training early and feed instruction tickets; the Death Blow harvest is on the path.
  • Late DLC joiners — recruit ASAP in Part 1 so they level into the timeskip.
  • General: rich-train ~6 carries (Byleth, Claude, Leonie, Hilda, Raphael, one mage); rotate stat boosters onto fliers and Byleth first.
