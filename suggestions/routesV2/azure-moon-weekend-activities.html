Azure Moon — Weekend Activity Priority (Blue Lions · Normal)

Direction-level guide for what to do on each free weekend: pick one of Exploration / Battle / Seminar / Rest. This is the Azure Moon (Blue Lions, Normal) companion to the route plan — not an exhaustive calendar, just where to point your effort. Mechanics reflect the corrected facts: Choir gives NO motivation (Faith + Byleth Authority + support only); Tea Parties unlock only at Ch.4 (do Ferdinand's "Tea for Two" quest).

Default priority

Exploration > Battle > Seminar > Rest. On Normal you'll spend most weekends exploring; battle when a paralogue/level need appears; seminar to rush a certification; rest almost never.

Direction by phase (Azure Moon)

  1. Ch.1–4 — Explore almost every weekend. Build recruit support early (Dorothea, Leonie); pump Byleth Authority via Choir (Dorothea's gate) and garden Str seeds (Leonie's gate) so you never have to grind Byleth's off-build skills. Recruit Catherine (just talk, ~Ch.4) and Dorothea (Ch.3–4). Clear "Tea for Two" at Ch.4 to switch tea on. Answer the monthly lecture question well (motivation + Professor EXP); accept every weekly Group Task.
  2. Ch.5–8 — Explore, with Battle on paralogue/level weekends. Recruit Leonie ~Ch.6 (support + gardened Str, not Faculty Training). Bank Renown → Saint Statues when they unlock (~Ch.8) — the EXP/support multipliers compound. Feed kills to weak early units (Ingrid).
  3. Ch.9–12 — finish builds. Win the White Heron Cup with Dorothea (decided by Charm — pre-load with meals/tea). Use Seminar to push multiple units through advanced certs; Battle remaining paralogues. Byleth's Enlightened One auto-unlocks (end Ch.10) — zero certification needed.

Monthly rhythm (rule of thumb)

  • First free day: Explore (fresh quests/items/dialogue).
  • Each week: answer the lecture question well; accept the Group Task; spend Instruct Points on priority units.
  • Last free day: Battle if a paralogue is open or anyone's under-levelled; otherwise Explore again.
  • Rest only as an emergency motivation reset.
