The TRAINING / ACTIVITY layer for THIS PROJECT'S Azure Moon roster (Run 1, Normal)

This file is the week-by-week tutoring + weekend-activity plan that builds toward the exact roster in ../routes/azure-moon.md. It sits on top of the month-by-month calendar/mechanics in blue-lions-day-by-day.md — read that for mission names, deadlines, paralogue windows and the Professor-Level / Explore / Battle / Seminar / Rest system. This file does not repeat those; it tells you WHO to tutor in WHICH skill each week, and WHAT to do on the weekend, to hit this roster's certifications and recruits.

1. Setup & priorities

1.1 The weekly activity budget (quick recap — full detail in day-by-day §0)

  • Weekday = Instruction. You may individually tutor a number of students equal to your Instruct Points (3 at Prof. Lv E → 7 at A). Each instruction costs the student 25 motivation; a fully-motivated student can be drilled up to 4× in one lecture day. A Great/Perfect result lets you Praise (refunds 25 motivation) — chase these on your priority units.
  • Set Goals at each unit's target-class skills (below). Accept every Group Task (Riding / Heavy Armor / Flying paired lessons) — free skill XP + support.
  • Weekend = one of Explore / Battle / Seminar / Rest. Default Explore; Battle for paralogues/levels; Seminar to rush a cert; Rest only when motivation is dead (also repairs Sword of the Creator).
  • Two activity systems this plan leans on hardest:
  • Faculty Training (1 AP, unlocks Ch.3): the way to raise Byleth's own ranks — this is how Byleth's off-type Reason gets to B+ for Mortal Savant.
  • Meals / Tea / Lost items / Gifts: raise support with Byleth, which lowers recruit thresholds (~20% per support level). This is the lever for Dorothea / Leonie.

2. Who needs the most attention (prioritized)

① Byleth ♀ (Mortal Savant) — the off-type Reason project

Mortal Savant needs Sword A + Reason B+. Sword trains itself (it's her boon and she's always deployed). Reason is the work item — Byleth has no Reason boon, so: - Faculty Training every week it's worth an AP from Ch.3 onward, targeting Reason (and Authority on the side). This is the single most-repeated instruction in this plan. - Self-study / Goals: when Byleth tutors others you can't add Byleth, but you can let Reason accrue via the Mage class you bank her in — certify Byleth Myrmidon → Mage early so she gains Reason class XP just by being in Mage, then keep Reason as a Faculty-Training focus. - Choir Practice also nudges Byleth's Authority. Push Authority → A for her battalion as a secondary. - Mastery detours: Brigand (Death Blow) and Archer (Hit+20) — slot these on Battle/Seminar-heavy stretches. - ⚠️ Mortal Savant requires Master Seal, Lv30+ — she will only certify late (≈ Ch.10–11). That's fine; the Enlightened One auto-unlock at end of Ch.10 is your zero-effort fallback if Reason B+ is somehow short, but the plan is to keep Mortal Savant distinct.

② Recruits — start the gating stat EARLY, recruit BEFORE the timeskip

  • Dorothea (Charm 25 + Authority B): Charm rises passively, but Authority B is the real gate. Start raising Byleth's Authority from Ch.1 (Choir + Authority Goal weeks) and build Dorothea support every Explore day (greet her, return her lost items, gift liked/loved items, one Tea Party, one Shared Meal). Target recruit: Ch.3–4, never later than Ch.6, so she's in your class with EXP before the White Heron Cup (Ch.9, 12/16). The Cup is decided by Charm — feed her meals/tea the weeks before.
  • Catherine (no gate): simply talk to her on an Explore day in Ch.4 and invite. Free combat-ready body. After joining, her tutoring job is Axe + Flying for the Wyvern Lord detour (she's Sword A already). On Normal you may even bench her — she's flex.
  • Leonie (Strength 15 + Lance C): Strength 15 on Byleth is the slow part — keep a Strength Goal / faculty Sword-Lance training ticking and garden Str seeds to push Byleth's Str via boosters if needed. Build her support too (she shares the Jeralt link with Byleth, so supports come naturally). Target recruit: Ch.6. After joining: Lance is her boon, tutor Flying (and Sword to C) toward Falcon Knight.

③ Ingrid ⚠️ (weak early → flying lance)

  • Bases are weak; she is a project, not an emergency. Feed her kills (last-hit fleeing/weakened enemies) in Ch.2–6 to level her.
  • Tutor Lance + Flying from Ch.1. Get her into Pegasus Knight (Lance C + Flying D) as soon as ranks allow (~Ch.5), then grind Flying B+ / Lance A / Sword C for Falcon Knight.
  • Accept every Flying Group Task — she's your anchor flier. Master Pegasus → Darting Blow and Brigand → Death Blow (both "vital" per her rec file).

④ Mercedes ⚠️ (frail healer → Gremory)

  • Never melee her; keep her behind the line. Her job is healing + chip.
  • Rush Faith — Physic is available as early as Ch.3; that one spell defines her early value. Then climb Faith A + Reason A for Gremory.
  • Feed her Spd boosters (garden Spd/Nordsalat seeds) so she stops getting doubled.
  • Build her A-support with Annette (mutual +3 Mt) — pair them in meals and adjacent in battle.

⑤ Annette (Reason/Authority → Gremory + rallies)

  • Tutor Reason A + Faith (Faith needed for Gremory's Faith A). Authority too — her Rally Strength personal makes Authority Rally-stacking valuable.
  • Master Mage → Fiendish Blow and Archer → Hit+20. Build alongside Mercedes for the +3 Mt.

⑥ Gilbert 🔒⚠️ (Ch.13 auto-join, under-leveled) — catch-up plan in §4

Nothing to do in Part 1 (he isn't present). Lowest investment priority. See §4.
