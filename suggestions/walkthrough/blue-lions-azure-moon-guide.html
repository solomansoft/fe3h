0. How the monthly / weekly system works (mechanics primer)

0.1 The calendar

  • Part 1 ("White Clouds") runs ~12 in-game months (Great Tree Moon → Lone Moon); each month ~4 weeks.
  • Most weekdays are fixed: one Instruction (lecture) day per week, plus story/quest days.
  • Each week ends in a weekend free day — pick exactly one of:
Choice What it does Pick it when…
Explore Spend Activity Points (AP) on meals, choir, fishing, gardening, faculty training, tea, tournaments, merchant/forge, lost items, and talking to recruit targets. Most professor exp/support/motivation/items. Default. Especially the first weekend of a new month (monastery refreshes with quests/items/dialogue).
Battle An Auxiliary battle (one free/week; extras cost Battle Points) and/or a Paralogue / Rare Monster. No motivation. A paralogue is up, units are underleveled, or you need gold/skill XP. Best on the last weekend after instruction.
Seminar Byleth + up to ~5 students gain a block of skill XP (+~50 motivation). Rushing several units toward a cert without micromanaging an explore day.
Rest Bulk motivation (~+50) and +5 durability to the Sword of the Creator (only repair method). No free meal/buff. Motivation is dead across the board, or to repair the Sword of the Creator. Lowest priority.

Default priority: Explore > Battle > Seminar > Rest.

0.3 Instruction (weekday tutoring)

  • Tutor a number of students = your Instruct Points (3 at E → 7 at A).
  • Each student has a motivation bar (max 100 = 4×25 steps). Each instruction costs 25 motivation → a fully-motivated student can be drilled up to 4× per lecture day.
  • A Great/Perfect result lets you Praise (refill 25 motivation); higher Byleth support → better odds/gains.
  • Goals: assign each student 1 skill (large) or 2 skills (moderate) at their target-class skills.
  • Group Tasks (团队任务) — always accept, every week. Assign two students to a weekly task (free — no Activity Point). The pair gains skill XP in that task's movement skill + mutual support, plus gold & Smithing Stones; a Perfect result (likelier if both have a strength in the skill and good support) gives more. Only three tasks exist:
  • Sky Watch → Flying · Stable Duty → Riding · Weeding → Heavy Armor. So group tasks only train Flying / Riding / Heavy Armor — assign them to units who need those: Sky Watch (Flying) for Ingrid / Leonie / Catherine, Stable Duty (Riding) for Dimitri / Sylvain, Weeding (Heavy Armor) for Dedue (Gilbert in Part 2). Pair two units who both want the skill so both gain (their support is a bonus). (Mage/sword supports like Mercedes×Annette and the romance couples are better built via meals + battle adjacency + adjutants — group tasks won't train their skills.) Never decline one.
  • Refill motivation before lecture day (meals / tea once unlocked Ch.4 / Rest — not choir) so you can tutor 4×.

0.4 Exploration — priority order for a typical explore day

  1. Free (0 AP), every time: Gardening (Plant → Cultivate → Harvest), Fishing, Return lost items, Talk to/greet students (recruitment + support).
  2. Cook a Meal (1 AP) once early each month — party-wide combat buff for that month's battles.
  3. Choir Practice (1 AP) — Faith (students) + Authority (Byleth) + bonds; no motivation. Top value for passively raising Byleth's Authority (Dorothea gate / battalions).
  4. Share a Meal (1 AP) — +support & motivation on two students (sets up max tutoring).
  5. Faculty Training (1 AP, unlocks Ch.3) — raise Byleth's own skill ranks.
  6. Tea Party (1 AP) — big support push for a key character/recruit. ⚠️ Unlocks only in Ch.4 (do Ferdinand's "Tea for Two" quest; else auto at Ch.13) — unavailable before then.
  7. Tournament (1 AP) — gold/items + biggest professor-exp spike.
  8. Browse Merchant/Blacksmith, then accept all new quests before leaving.

0.5 Gardening seed → stat booster (always Cultivate)

HP+1: Western Fódlan/Blue · Str+1: Mixed Herb/Angelica/Purple · Mag+1: Southern Fódlan/Yellow · Dex+1: Morfis-Plum/Green · Spd+1: Nordsalat/Pale-Blue · Lck+1: Boa-Fruit/Mixed Fruit. Plant 5, spend gold to Cultivate (guarantees the drop), harvest next visit.

0.6 Renown / Saint Statues (unlock Month 8 / Chapter 8)

Spend Renown at the 4 Cathedral statues. Priority: the EXP +5% / +10% tiers (faster army leveling), then 10,000-Renown stat-cap +5 tiers. Statue map: Cichol = Axe/Authority/Flying/Str&Spd cap · Cethleann = Lance/Faith/Class-Mastery/Lck&Cha cap · Macuil = Sword/Reason/Riding/Mag&Dex cap · Indech = Bow/Fighting/HeavyArmor/Def&Res cap.

0.7 Recruiting (Blue Lions specifics)

  • All 8 Blue Lions auto-join with the house — recruiting effort goes only to cross-house units.
  • Meet the student's stat + skill threshold, then invite on an explore day. Byleth support lowers the requirement (~20%/level) — build it via lost items, gifts (loved/liked), shared meals, group tasks, and tea parties (only after they unlock in Ch.4 — do Ferdinand's "Tea for Two" quest).
  • Run-1 recruits: Dorothea (patches weak magic + becomes the Dancer), Catherine (free combat-ready body), Leonie (second flier). See §1.

Battalions — what to buy & equip

Every deployed unit can carry one battalion for stat bonuses + a Gambit. Cross-ref: §1.3 note on pushing Byleth's Authority toward a battalion, and the §0.4 Choir habit (Choir trains Byleth's Authority).

Mechanics recap (one battalion per unit)

  • A battalion's "Lvl" = the minimum Authority rank the equipping unit needs (e.g. a Lvl B battalion needs Authority B), and the movement type must match (infantry / armored / cavalry / flying). So only Ingrid/Leonie/Catherine (fliers) can take flying battalions; Dimitri/Sylvain (mounted) can take cavalry ones; etc.
  • Using a Gambit trains the unit's Authority (like a weapon trains its skill), and levels the battalion up to Lv5 (better bonuses). So gambit-spam on a high-Authority carry compounds.
  • Endurance drops as the commanding unit takes damage; at 0 the battalion (and its gambit) drops out for that battle. Repair/replenish with gold at the Battalion Guild before each fight — budget gold for this.
  • Where they come from: hire at the Battalion Guild (marketplace, explore days / battle prep) once it's unlocked early in Part 1 (Jeralt's Bolstering Forces also gives 3 free battalions). The guild's stock is route-specific (Kingdom/Blue Lion battalions are hireable on this route) and is restocked at Chapter 8 and again at Chapter 14 (Azure Moon). Higher Professor Level → more Funds & Activity Points, i.e. the gold/time to buy and repair more battalions, even though the guild's roster is driven by route + the Ch.8/Ch.14 restocks, not by professor-rank milestones directly. [⚠️ verify] — exact per-battalion appearance weeks aren't cleanly documented; treat timings below as "available by/around."
  • Lord battalion is automatic: after the timeskip Dimitri is auto-granted the King of Lions Corps (Lvl C, gambit Wave Attack, best Str bonus + max endurance) — don't buy him one.
