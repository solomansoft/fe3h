Blue Lions / Azure Moon — Day-by-Day Walkthrough

Fire Emblem: Three Houses (Switch) — Run 1 of the 4-playthrough plan (Normal)

Scope: a calendar-based "what to do each teaching day / each weekend" guide for the Blue Lions house and its route Azure Moon (苍月之章). This is the Run 1 = Normal "learn the systems" playthrough from /suggestions/team.md. Facts policy: dates, deadlines and recruitment thresholds below are cross-checked across Serenes Forest, fe3h.com (Triangle Attack), Game8, GameWith, fedatamine.com and Fire Emblem Wiki. Anything not fully verified is tagged [UNVERIFIED]. Sources cached in /sources/fe3h-com/, /sources/serenes-forest/, /sources/game8/. Recruitment thresholds grounded in /character/blue-lions/.

0. How the monthly / weekly system works (mechanics primer)

0.1 The calendar

  • Part 1 (academy, "White Clouds") runs ~12 in-game months (Great Tree Moon → Lone Moon). Each month is ~4 weeks.
  • Most weekdays are fixed: one Instruction (lecture) day each week, plus story/quest days.
  • Each week ends in a weekend free day where you pick exactly one of four activities:
Choice What it does Pick it when…
Explore Walk the monastery, spend Activity Points (AP) on meals, choir, fishing, gardening, faculty training, tea, tournaments, the merchant/forge, returning lost items, and talking to recruit targets. Gives the most professor exp, support, motivation and items. Your default. Especially the first weekend of a new month (the monastery refreshes with new quests/items/dialogue).
Battle Fight an Auxiliary battle (one free per week; extra ones cost Battle Points) and/or an available Paralogue / Rare Monster Sighting. Gives no motivation. When a paralogue is up, when units are underleveled, or you need gold/skill XP. Best on the last weekend of the month (after instruction is done).
Seminar Byleth + up to ~5 students whose goals match the focus gain a big block of skill XP (and ~+50 motivation). When you want to rush several students toward a certification/master class and don't want to micromanage an explore day.
Rest Restores bulk motivation (~+50 to everyone) and +5 durability to the Sword of the Creator (the only way to repair it). There is no free meal or "rested buff." When motivation is low across the board and you don't want to spend AP, or to repair the Sword of the Creator. Lowest priority.

Default priority: Explore > Battle > Seminar > Rest.

Raise professor level fast (professor exp sources, biggest first): - Tournaments: 300 exp on a win (100 on loss) — the best single action. - Like+Like meals / choir: up to 200 each. - Fishing (0 AP): 10–100 per fish — best AP-free grind. - Gardening (0 AP): 100 × lowest seed star. - Answering lecture questions correctly, completing quests, certification exams, picking the preferred response when giving advice, faculty training, returning lost items, winning battles. - Bottom line: every explore day, do the free 0-AP actions (fish, garden, lost items, talk) plus one tournament/choir, and you climb steadily. Higher level = more AP, more battles, more students tutored, more adjutants, more gold.

0.3 Instruction (weekday tutoring)

  • You can individually tutor a number of students = your Instruct Points (3 at E → 7 at A).
  • Each student has a motivation bar (max 100, in 25-point steps = 4 bars). Each instruction costs 25 motivation, so a fully-motivated student can be drilled up to 4 times in one lecture day.
  • A Great/Perfect result lets you Praise (refills 25 motivation); a poor one lets you Console/Critique (chance to recover). Higher support with Byleth → better odds and bigger gains.
  • Goals: assign each student 1 skill (large boost) or 2 skills (moderate each). Aim Goals at the skills each unit needs for their target class (see roster below).
  • Group Tasks (Riding / Heavy Armor / Flying paired lessons): free skill XP + support + funds + items — accept them whenever offered.
  • Refill motivation before lecture day via meals/choir/tea/Rest so you can tutor 4×.

0.4 Exploration — priority order for a typical explore day

  1. Free (0 AP), do every time: Gardening (Plant → Cultivate → Harvest), Fishing, Return lost items, Talk to/greet students (recruitment + support).
  2. Cook a Meal (1 AP) once early each month — gives a party-wide combat buff for all battles that month.
  3. Choir Practice (1 AP) — motivation + Faith (students) + Authority (Byleth) + bonds; top value.
  4. Share a Meal (1 AP) — +support and motivation on two students (set up max tutoring).
  5. Faculty Training (1 AP, unlocks Ch3) — raise Byleth's own skill ranks.
  6. Tea Party (1 AP) — big support push for a key character/recruit.
  7. Tournament (1 AP) — gold/items + the biggest professor-exp spike.
  8. Browse the Merchant/Blacksmith, then accept all new quests before leaving.

0.5 Gardening seed → stat booster (always Cultivate)

HP+1: Western Fódlan/Blue Flower · Str+1: Mixed Herb/Angelica/Purple · Mag+1: Southern Fódlan/Yellow · Dex+1: Morfis-Plum/Green · Spd+1: Nordsalat/Pale-Blue · Lck+1: Boa-Fruit/Mixed Fruit. Plant 5, spend gold to Cultivate (guarantees the drop), harvest next visit.

0.6 Renown / Saint Statues (unlock Month 8 / Chapter 8)

Spend Renown (earned from battles/paralogues/quests) at the 4 Cathedral statues. Priority tiers: the EXP +5% / +10% tiers (faster leveling for the whole army) and the 10,000-Renown stat-cap +5 tiers. Statue skill mapping: Cichol = Axe/Authority/Flying/Str&Spd cap · Cethleann = Lance/Faith/Class-Mastery/Lck&Cha cap · Macuil = Sword/Reason/Riding/Mag&Dex cap · Indech = Bow/Fighting/HeavyArmor/Def&Res cap.

0.7 Recruiting (Blue Lions specifics)

  • Good news: all 8 Blue Lions members (Dimitri, Dedue, Felix, Sylvain, Ingrid, Annette, Mercedes, Ashe) auto-join when you pick the house — no recruitment needed. Your recruiting effort goes only into cross-house units.
  • How cross-house recruiting works: meet the student's stat + skill threshold (shown on their recruit screen), then invite them on an explore day. Raising support with Byleth lowers the requirement (~20% per support level). Build support outside battle via lost items, gifts (loved/liked), tea parties, shared meals.
  • Run-1 recruit target (from team.md): Dorothea — patches the Blue Lions' weak magic AND becomes your Dancer (win the White Heron Cup in Month 9 with her). Threshold: Charm 25 + Authority B. Optional second magic recruit: Lysithea (Magic 15 + Faith B) if you want a nuker, but team.md reserves her for Run 3 — skip for diversity unless you really want her.

PART 1 — White Clouds (academy phase), month by month

Numbering: walkthrough/calendar style, Chapter 1 = Great Tree Moon. Months map to real months (Great Tree=Apr … Lone=Mar). Mission deadline = the month's last weekend ("Mission" day).

Month 0 — Prologue (late Great Tree Moon): "An Inevitable Encounter"

  • Battle A Skirmish at Dawn (defeat Kostas). Tutorial; no calendar/deadline. Choose Blue Lions when prompted.

Chapter 1 — Great Tree Moon (April): "Three Houses"

  • Mission: Rivalry of the Houses (the 3-house mock battle, rout). Deadline 4/30.
  • Weekends: only 1 free day (4/27) → Explore. Do all free 0-AP actions, cook a meal, talk to everyone.
  • Instruction focus: start everyone's Goals toward their target class (see roster). Dimitri → Lance/Authority; Felix → Sword; Mercedes/Annette → Reason+Faith; Ashe → Bow; Ingrid → Lance/Flying; Sylvain → Lance/Riding; Dedue → Axe/Heavy Armor.
  • Recruitment: begin chatting up Dorothea every explore day from now on; start gifting her liked items to build support.
  • Paralogues: none yet.
  • Shopping: buy a few Iron weapons and Vulneraries; unlock the greenhouse seed quest (Dedue's) so gardening starts paying off.

Chapter 2 — Harpstring Moon (May): "Familiar Scenery"

  • Mission: Red Canyon Dominance (defeat Kostas). Deadline 5/31.
  • Weekends (3): 5/11, 5/18, 5/25. Plan: Explore → Explore → Battle (do one auxiliary battle to level the team before the mission, or Explore again if levels are fine).
  • Instruction focus: push Dimitri toward Soldier certification (it wants no specific rank — available early; it boosts him before Cavalier). Get Mercedes/Annette toward Monk → Mage/Priest. Begin Felix's Sword grind.
  • Recruitment: keep feeding Dorothea support (lost items + gifts + a tea party). Track her Authority (needs B) — assign Authority as a 2nd Goal if you recruit her into class early; otherwise just build support.
  • Paralogues: none yet.
  • Note: first real battle vs. the prologue bandits.

Chapter 3 — Garland Moon (June): "Mutiny in the Mist"

  • Mission: The Magdred Ambush (defeat Lonato; foggy map with Catherine). Deadline 6/30.
  • Weekends (4): 6/8, 6/15, 6/22, 6/29. Plan: Explore → Explore → Battle → Explore.
  • Faculty Training unlocks this month (quest "Learning from the Best"). Start raising Byleth's Authority/Sword/Reason ranks so Byleth can tutor those skills better and certify into Mercenary later.
  • Instruction focus: first certification window opens for many units. Certify into intermediate goals as ranks allow: Mercedes/Annette → keep Reason/Faith climbing; Felix → Sword C for Mercenary path; Ashe → Bow D→C for Archer.
  • Recruitment: Dorothea support should be climbing; keep gifting. If her Charm/Authority are close, you can already invite her.
  • Paralogues: none yet.

Chapter 4 — Blue Sea Moon (July): "The Goddess's Rite of Rebirth"

  • Mission: Assault at the Rite of Rebirth. Deadline 7/26.
  • Weekends (3): 7/6, 7/13, 7/20. Plan: Explore → Battle → Explore.
  • Story: the Death Knight first appears at the end — do not try to kill him this month; just survive/finish the objective.
  • Instruction focus: push certification exams as ranks hit C: Felix → Mercenary (Sword C); Ashe → Archer (Bow D); Dimitri → Cavalier (Lance C / Riding C); Mercedes → Priest/Mage; Annette → Mage.
  • Recruitment: finalize Dorothea if not already in. Keep her in class so she gets instruction/EXP before the Cup.

Chapter 5 — Verdant Rain Moon (August): "Tower of Black Winds"

  • Mission: The Gautier Inheritance (defeat Miklan, who transforms into a Black Beast). Deadline 8/31.
  • Weekends (3): 8/10, 8/17, 8/24. Plan: Explore → Explore → Battle.
  • Instruction focus: continue intermediate classes. Sylvain → Cavalier; Ingrid → Pegasus Knight (Lance C / Flying C, female-only — start the Flying Group Tasks now); Dedue → Armored/Brigand toward Fortress Knight.
  • Shopping: all gardening seed types become buyable around now — start farming stat boosters (prioritize Str seeds for Felix/Dimitri, Mag for Mercedes/Annette/Dorothea, Spd for Ingrid).
  • Paralogues: none on Azure Moon are open yet for your core units.

Chapter 6 — Horsebow Moon (September): "Rumors of a Reaper"

  • Mission: The Underground Chamber — the fightable Death Knight battle (rescue Flayn). Deadline 9/30.
  • Weekends (4): 9/7, 9/14, 9/21, 9/28. Plan: Explore → Battle → Explore → Battle.
  • Death Knight tip: on Normal you can try to kill him for EXP/loot, but it's optional and risky early — focus the objective.
  • Paralogue window OPENS: War for the Weak (Dedue) becomes available (Ch6 → Ch11). You can start it now; it's safe to clear early. This is the most important paralogue of the run (see Chapter 11). If you want, clear it now on a Battle weekend and stop worrying about it.
  • Instruction focus: keep certifying; start aiming Byleth toward Mercenary (Sword C) on the way to the unique Enlightened One.

Chapter 7 — Wyvern Moon (October): "Field of the Eagle and Lion"

  • Mission: Battle of the Eagle and Lion — the big Gronder Field mock war (rout). Deadline 10/27.
  • Weekends (4): 10/5, 10/12, 10/19, 10/26. Plan: Explore → Battle → Explore → Explore (this is a winnable showcase battle; come in a level or two ahead).
  • Paralogue windows OPEN: The Forgotten (Sylvain → Lance of Ruin) Ch7→Ch11; Rumored Nuptials (Dorothea OR Ingrid → Lúin) Ch7→Ch11; (Land of the Golden Deer Ch7→Ch11 only if you recruited Lorenz — skip). You may clear The Forgotten now for the Lance of Ruin (great on Sylvain), or wait to batch in Ch11.
  • Instruction focus: push advanced-rank skills. Felix Sword toward A (Swordmaster); Mercedes/Annette Reason+Faith toward Warlock/Bishop; Ingrid Lance/Flying toward Falcon Knight.

Chapter 8 — Red Wolf Moon (November): "The Flame in the Darkness"

  • Mission: The Remire Calamity (defeat Solon). Deadline 11/25.
  • Weekends (4): 11/2, 11/9, 11/16, 11/23. Plan: Explore → Battle → Explore → Battle.
  • Saint Statues UNLOCK this month. Spend Renown: grab EXP +5% on a couple of statues first, then keep banking for EXP +10% and stat-cap tiers.
  • Paralogue window OPENS: True Chivalry (Felix → Aegis Shield) Ch8→Ch11. Clear when convenient.
  • Instruction focus: keep advancing; start certifying anyone ready into advanced classes (Sniper, Warlock, Priest, Paladin).

Chapter 9 — Ethereal Moon (December): "The Cause of Sorrow"

  • Mission: Salvation at the Chapel (rout). Deadline 12/26.
  • Weekends (3): 12/7, 12/14, 12/21. Plan: Explore (prep for Cup) → Battle → Explore (Ball).
  • ★ White Heron Cup (12/16): a dance contest — enter Dorothea (highest Charm) and win to certify her as Dancer for free (a major support unit — extra turn for an ally). Build her Charm with meals/tea beforehand.
  • ★ Establishment Day Ball (12/25): story event; raises support with whoever you dance/spend time with (set up your S-support partner — Dimitri for Run 1).
  • Story: Jeralt is killed near the end of the chapter — unavoidable.
  • Paralogue windows OPEN: An Ocean View (Seteth & Flayn → Spear of Assal) Ch9→Ch11 (only if you recruited both — likely skip on a Blue Lions diversity run); Falling Short of Heaven (Ashe OR Catherine → Critical Ring, Shoes of the Wind) Ch9→Ch11 — worth doing (Critical Ring is great on Felix). Clear it now or in Ch11.

Chapter 10 — Guardian Moon (January): "Where the Goddess Dwells"

  • Mission: The Sealed Forest Snare (defeat Solon; avenge Jeralt). Deadline 1/31.
  • Weekends (4): 1/4, 1/11, 1/18, 1/25. Plan: Explore → Battle → Explore → Explore.
  • Story: Byleth gains Sothis's power (Sword of the Creator / Divine Pulse boost). Use Rest if the Sword of the Creator needs repair.
  • Instruction focus: final push of advanced ranks — you want everyone in a strong advanced class by the timeskip. Byleth toward the Enlightened One track.
  • Paralogues: finish off any of the Ch6–Ch11 windows you haven't cleared (see Chapter 11 — last chance is next month).

Chapter 11 — Pegasus Moon (February): "Throne of Knowledge" ★ LAST PRE-TIMESKIP EXPLORATION ★

  • Mission: Conflict in the Holy Tomb (defeat the Flame Emperor). Deadline 2/29.
  • Weekends (3): 2/8, 2/15, 2/22. Plan: clear ALL remaining Part 1 paralogues on Battle weekends, Explore the rest.
  • ★★ CRITICAL — clear all these paralogues NOW (they vanish at the timeskip):
  • War for the Weak (Dedue) — if you do nothing else, do this. Required so Dedue survives and returns in Chapter 16. Miss it → Dedue is gone forever.
  • The Forgotten (Sylvain) → Lance of Ruin (Hero's Relic).
  • Falling Short of Heaven (Ashe/Catherine) → Critical Ring, Shoes of the Wind.
  • True Chivalry (Felix) → Aegis Shield.
  • Rumored Nuptials (Dorothea/Ingrid) → Lúin.
  • (Optional, only if recruited those units: An Ocean View / Land of the Golden Deer.)
  • Story: the Holy Tomb — the Flame Emperor is revealed as Edelgard. The route locks to Azure Moon as a Blue Lions student.
  • Last call: finish all support/S-support groundwork (Dimitri), all shopping/forging, all certifications you want before the 5-year gap.

Chapter 12 — Lone Moon (March): "To War" (route finale)

  • Mission: the Battle of Garreg Mach (route-specific). Battle-only — no exploration. [UNVERIFIED exact deadline/free-day count — route-dependent.]
  • War begins; the 5-year timeskip follows and you enter Azure Moon (Part 2).
  • Dedue leaves at the end of this chapter (returns Ch16 only if War for the Weak was done).

Golden rules — quick reference (do these EVERY week)

  1. Default to Explore. Battle only for paralogues / underleveled units / gold; Seminar to rush certifications; Rest only when motivation is dead.
  2. First weekend of every month = Explore (the monastery refreshes).
  3. Every explore day, do the free 0-AP actions first: garden (Plant→Cultivate→Harvest), fish, return lost items, talk to/greet students.
  4. Cook one buff meal early each month (party-wide combat bonus for that month's battles).
  5. Keep motivation topped up before each lecture day (meals/choir/tea) so you can tutor each student 4×.
  6. Climb professor level relentlessly — tournaments + choir + fishing. More level = more AP, battles, instruction, adjutants, gold.
  7. Set Goals at each unit's class skills; accept every Group Task (free skill XP + support).
  8. Clear time-limited paralogues before they expire. Part 1 ones all die at the timeskip (do them by Chapter 11).
  9. ★ Never skip "War for the Weak" (Dedue) — clear it by Chapter 11 or lose Dedue forever.
  10. Chapter 9: win the White Heron Cup with Dorothea → free Dancer. Chapter 11 and Chapter 21 are your "last exploration" deadlines (pre- and post-timeskip) — finish all shopping/supports/paralogues then.
  11. Recruit Dorothea early (Charm 25 / Authority B; lower the bar with support via gifts + lost items + tea) — she fixes the Blue Lions' weak magic and becomes your Dancer.
  12. Spend Renown at the Saint Statues from Month 8 — EXP% tiers first, then stat-cap tiers.
