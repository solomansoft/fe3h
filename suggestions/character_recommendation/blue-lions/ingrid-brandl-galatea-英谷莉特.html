Ingrid Brandl Galatea / 英谷莉特·布兰德尔·加拉提亚 — Recommendation

Sword/Lance/Riding/Flying strengths; no weaknesses (rare — flexible to build). No budding talent. Minor Crest of Daphnel (10% Mt+5 on combat art). Personal: Lady Knight (gambits Mt+3, Hit+5). Best-in-house Speed 60% growth with high Lck/Res/Cha — a natural flier and dodge tank [GamerSky CN flier T2; note.com JP "B"; fe3h.com "one of the best dodge tanks"]. Recruitable (Dex 15 + Flying D). Owns relic Lúin (鲁纳) (lance).

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Soldier → Pegasus Knight → Falcon Knight — #1. "Unquestionably her best choice" — leverages her Flying + Lance strengths + Lúin; Falcon Knight's huge Spd/Res + 8-move flying + Canto turns her 60% Spd into a doubling, dodging menace [Game8, fe3h.com "easier to get to", GamerSky CN]. Female-only, fits. Grab Death Blow + Darting Blow on the way.
  2. Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord — #2. More Str and Def (consistent doubling on lower difficulty), still 8-move flying + Canto; "mostly down to personal preference" vs Falcon Knight [fe3h.com, Reddit, GameWith JP]. ⚠️ Needs Axe A (off her strengths) — slightly more investment than Falcon, and trades some of her signature speed.
  3. Soldier → Pegasus Knight → Falcon Knight (dodge-tank variant) — #3. Same class but built for evasion: Alert Stance+ + high avoid + Lúin's accuracy = enemies whiff entirely; one of the BL's best dodge tanks [fe3h.com].
  4. Mage → Warlock → Dark Flier / Valkyrie / Gremory — #4 niche. Her decent Mag growth (35%) supports a magic-flier; uses Uncanny Blow/Fiendish Blow [fe3h.com]. Fun but underuses her elite physical/flying kit.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Certify in, master the class bar for its permanent ability, then leave — these are NOT her final class. 1. Pegasus Knight → Darting Blow (Speed +6 when she initiates — "vital" for her; turns her best-in-house 60% Spd into guaranteed doubles; her #1 route already passes through Pegasus Knight). 2. Brigand → Death Blow (Strength +6 when she initiates — "vital" for her; offsets her one modest stat, Strength, so her doubles actually hit hard). 3. Soldier → Defense +2 (flat +2 Def, mastered early as her starting class on the Falcon Knight route). 4. Archer → Hit +20 (flat +20 Hit — optional accuracy insurance for the dodge-tank/flier build).

Training focus

  • Lance to S+ (Lancefaire; Frozen Lance Lance A, Hit and Run Lance C) — her primary + Lúin [fe3h.com].
  • Flying to A (Falcon/Wyvern + Alert Stance+ for dodge tank). Sword as a strong secondary (Hexblade at Sword A).
  • Master intermediates for Death Blow and Darting Blow — both "vital" for her [fe3h.com].
  • Authority — her Lady Knight personal buffs gambits (Mt+3/Hit+5), so invest in Authority to make her a strong gambit user too.
  • Special care: weak early base stats but scales excellently — stick with her. No weaknesses means low tutoring friction. Weight-3 (C Armor) early helps her keep speed [fe3h.com].

Supports / pairings

  • Gameplay: Felix & Sylvain (childhood-friend trio — Linked Attacks) [fe3h.com]; Dimitri, Ashe. Adjacent to a Dancer she abuses bonus turns well.
  • Romance (S-support): Byleth (M/F), Sylvain (also triggers his Philanderer), Felix, Dorothea (f/f).
  • Synergy: keep her near Felix/Sylvain for Linked Attack damage; her gambits (Lady Knight bonus) support the squad.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Lúin (relic lance, accurate + strong) + Killer/Silver Lance; a Sword (Killing Edge) for Hexblade/avoid; Javelin for 1–2 range.
  • Combat arts: Frozen Lance (Lance A), Hit and Run (Lance C, retreat after attack), Hexblade (Sword A — magic-defense damage) [fe3h.com].
  • Equipped skills (5): Lancefaire, Death Blow, Darting Blow, Alert Stance+ (dodge build) or Swordfaire, Hit+20. Lady Knight personal boosts her gambits passively.
  • Battalion/Gambit: a high-Hit flying battalion + an offensive gambit — her Lady Knight makes gambits hit harder/more accurately than most units'.
  • Key items: Strength boosters (her one modest stat at 35% Str); Speed she already has in spades.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Falcon Knight — #1, near-unanimous best; speed/avoid/Lance fit + Lúin [Game8 "unquestionably", fe3h.com, GamerSky CN].
  2. Wyvern Lord — #2, more Str/Def, less Spd; ⚠️ Axe A off-strength [fe3h.com, Reddit, GameWith JP].
  3. Falcon Knight dodge-tank variant — #3, evasion specialist [fe3h.com].
  4. Magic flier (Dark Flier/Valkyrie/Gremory) — #4 niche [fe3h.com]. - Disagreement is mild: Falcon vs Wyvern is "personal preference" [fe3h.com]. Verdict: Falcon Knight to maximize her elite Speed and dodge identity (and it's easier via her boons); Wyvern Lord only if you specifically want more raw Strength.
