Sylvain Jose Gautier / 希尔凡·荷塞·戈迪耶 — Recommendation

Lance/Axe/Riding strengths; Bow weakness. Budding talent: Reason → Black Magic Avoid +20. Minor Crest of Gautier (40% Mt+5 on combat art). Personal: Philanderer (+2 dmg / -2 taken near a female ally). Well-rounded growths (55% HP / 45% Str / 50% Spd / 40% Def) make him a durable mounted/flying bruiser [note.com JP "A"; GamerSky CN cavalry T1; Reddit rates him over Petra]. Auto-joins as Blue Lions; F!Byleth recruits him free from any house (Ch.2). Owns the relic Lance of Ruin (破裂の槍).

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Brigand(master) → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord — #1. "Highly appealing" — 8-move flying + Canto, Axefaire, top physical class; his Lance/Axe/Riding kit certifies it easily and Swift Strikes + Lance of Ruin end-phase combo deletes enemies [fe3h.com, Reddit "Sylvain- wyvern lord", note.com JP, GamerSky CN]. Best ceiling.
  2. Cavalier → Paladin — #2. Lower investment: 8 move + Canto + Lancefaire straight off his strengths; the JP-recommended "straightforward route" [watasonhakase JP, GameWith JP, GamerSky CN cavalry T1]. Slightly less damage/mobility than Wyvern but no banes to fix.
  3. Cavalier → Paladin(master) → Great Knight — #3. [Game8] pairs Lance of Ruin's Lancefaire with armored bulk; ⚠️ Heavy Armor A is steep and Great Knight has high skill demands for marginal gain [pegasusknight JP]. Tanky lance cavalry.
  4. Reason budding → Mage → Dark Knight — #4 niche. His best magical class (mobility + Lancefaire-adjacent), uses his Reason budding [fe3h.com, watasonhakase JP]. Fun hybrid but underuses his strong physical kit.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Certify in, master the class bar for its permanent ability, then leave — these are NOT his final class. 1. Brigand → Death Blow (Strength +6 when he initiates — "non-negotiable"; the kill combo with Swift Strikes + Lance of Ruin; his #1 route already masters Brigand). 2. Archer → Hit +20 (flat +20 Hit — fixes Lance of Ruin's poor accuracy; explicitly recommended in his training notes). 3. Cavalier → Desperation (follow-up before the foe counters when below 50% HP — optional aggressive detour from his Cavalier/Paladin line).

Training focus

  • Lance to S/S+ (Lancefaire; Swift Strikes at Lance A) — his core and signature [watasonhakase JP].
  • Master Brigand → Death Blow / 鬼神の一撃 ("non-negotiable", Str+6 on initiate) and Archer → Hit+20 to fix his accuracy with Lance of Ruin [watasonhakase JP, fe3h.com].
  • Riding to A/B for Cavalier/Paladin; Flying if going Wyvern. Axe is a strong secondary.
  • Budding talent: Reason → only needed for the Dark Knight build; skip otherwise.
  • Special care: Bow bane — never build him as an archer. Keep a female ally adjacent to trigger Philanderer (+2/-2).

Supports / pairings

  • Gameplay: Felix & Ingrid (Linked Attack — childhood-friend trio) [fe3h.com]; Mercedes, Annette, Dorothea. His Philanderer rewards positioning beside any female unit.
  • Romance (S-support): F!Byleth, Mercedes, Ingrid, Dorothea (all in-game romances and combat-adjacent for Philanderer).
  • Synergy: keep him beside Ingrid/Mercedes for both Linked Attacks and the Philanderer bonus simultaneously.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Lance of Ruin (his relic, huge Mt) + Killer/Silver Lance; forged Axe for Wyvern Axefaire; a Javelin/Spear for 1–2 range.
  • Combat arts: Swift Strikes / 連撃 (Lance A — among the best arts in the game), Frozen Lance, Tempest Lance (strong early scaling [Reddit]) [fe3h.com, watasonhakase JP].
  • Equipped skills (5): Lancefaire, Death Blow, Hit+20, Darting Blow/Death Blow stack, Crit+10 or Axefaire (Wyvern). Swift Strikes + Death Blow + Lance of Ruin is the kill combo.
  • Battalion/Gambit: a high-Mt cavalry/flying battalion; Stride or Retribution. Lady Knight-style gambit boosts aren't his, so use battalions for stats + a utility gambit.
  • Key items: Hit/Accuracy boosters (offsets Lance of Ruin's accuracy) and Str boosters.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Wyvern Lord — #1, mobility + Swift Strikes ceiling [fe3h.com, Reddit, note.com JP, GamerSky CN].
  2. Paladin — #2, lowest-effort strong build, no banes [watasonhakase JP, GameWith JP, GamerSky CN].
  3. Great Knight — #3, [Game8] Lancefaire+armor; ⚠️ Heavy Armor A cost.
  4. Dark Knight — #4 niche magic hybrid [fe3h.com]. - Disagreement: Game8 = Great Knight; everyone else = Wyvern Lord/Paladin. Verdict: Wyvern Lord if you'll tutor Flying, Paladin if you want it easy — both beat Great Knight's investment cost.
