Annette Fantine Dominic / 安妮特·芳汀·多米尼克 — Recommendation

Axe/Reason/Authority strengths; Bow/Heavy Armor weaknesses. No budding talent. Minor Crest of Dominic (10% conserve attack-magic use). Personal: Perseverance (Rally Strength — adjacent ally Str+4). Magic growths (50% Mag / 50% Dex) with a usable 30% Str and her Axe strength make her a unique hybrid mage/axe unit. The Blue Lions' magic carry [GamerSky CN "magic担当"; note.com JP "C"]. Recruitable (Magic 10 + Faith B). Owns relic Crusher (大锤) on Azure Moon.

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Monk(master) → Mage(master) → Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord — #1 (the "flying Annette" build). The standout: 8-move flying + Canto lets her dart in, swing Lightning Axe (one-shots many Maddening enemies), then retreat; Axefaire + her Axe strength + Crusher/Bolt Axe make her a mobile nuke [fe3h.com, search consensus]. Master Monk (Mag+2) and Mage (Fiendish Blow) first for the magic-axe damage. ⚠️ Requires Flying A (not a strength), but her Axe strength makes the axe ranks easy.
  2. Monk → Mage → Warlock → Gremory — #2. Pure-mage route: highest Magic, doubles spell uses, Authority for Rally utility; the GamerSky/JP default for female mages [GamerSky CN "Gremory", GameWith JP, Game8 "Warlock/Dark Knight"]. Female-only fits. Lower mobility than #1 but easiest and very strong.
  3. DLC Dark Flier (Dark Pegasus) — #3 (if DLC owned). Keeps her full spell list AND gives 7-move flying + Canto + Transmute; the best of both worlds for a flying mage [fe3h.com]. Ranked below Wyvern only because it needs DLC and slightly less bulk.
  4. Mage → Warlock → Dark Knight — #4. Mounted mage with mobility + Reason; [Game8] alt. Solid but Gremory out-magics it and Wyvern out-mobilizes it.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Certify in, master the class bar for its permanent ability, then leave — these are NOT her final class. 1. Mage → Fiendish Blow (Magic +6 when she initiates combat — the core magic-damage spike for her spells and Lightning/Bolt Axe; already a default stop on every route). 2. Brigand → Death Blow (Strength +6 when she initiates — fuels her physical Axe damage on the Wyvern build; the path already routes through Brigand). 3. Monk → Magic +2 (flat +2 Magic, mastered early on the way to her caster line).

Training focus

  • Reason to A (Warlock/Gremory + her spell list incl. Bolganone) and Faith for staff/support utility — Faith B is also her recruit requirement.
  • Axe to C+/A for Lightning Axe and Bolt Axe/Crusher — the key to her hybrid identity [fe3h.com].
  • Authority — her Perseverance/Rally Strength personal makes Authority Rally-stacking valuable; raise it for more Rally uses and battalions [fe3h.com].
  • Flying to A only if going Wyvern (#1).
  • Special care: No budding talent, so no hidden unlocks to chase. Avoid Bow/Heavy Armor (banes). Crusher has low accuracy — give her an Accuracy Ring [fe3h.com].

Supports / pairings

  • Gameplay: Mercedes — A-support = mutual +3 Might (build them together) [fe3h.com]; Felix (popular pairing), Sylvain, Dimitri, Ingrid. Her Rally Strength buffs whoever she stands by.
  • Romance (S-support): Byleth (M/F), Felix, Sylvain.
  • Synergy: position beside a physical attacker to feed Rally Strength (+4 Str) + the Mercedes +3 Mt buff; she enables others as well as nukes.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Crusher (relic, big Mt — use its Dust art) + Bolt Axe+ (ranged magic axe) + a forged Iron Axe for reliable Lightning Axe; Reason tomes (Bolganone, Fimbulvetr).
  • Combat arts: Lightning Axe (Axe — one-shots many enemies), Dust (Crusher), plus her offensive spells [fe3h.com].
  • Equipped skills (5): Fiendish Blow (Mage mastery, big magic-damage spike), Death Blow (if Brigand mastered), Axefaire (Wyvern) / Reason prowess, Magic +2, Hit+20 (helps Crusher accuracy).
  • Battalion/Gambit: a Magic battalion + a Hit/Rally-supporting gambit; her Authority feeds gambit reliability.
  • Key items: Accuracy Ring (for Crusher), Magic boosters, Speed Carrot.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Wyvern Lord (flying axe-mage) — #1, mobile Lightning-Axe nuke [fe3h.com, search].
  2. Gremory — #2, max magic + spell doubling, lowest investment [GamerSky CN, GameWith JP, Game8].
  3. Dark Flier (DLC) — #3, flying caster keeping spell list [fe3h.com].
  4. Dark Knight — #4, mounted mage alt [Game8]. - Disagreement: Chinese/JP wikis default her to Gremory (pure mage); English optimization sites (fe3h.com) prefer Wyvern Lord to exploit her Axe strength + Lightning Axe + Canto. Verdict: Wyvern Lord is the higher-skill, higher-payoff Maddening build; Gremory is the reliable, low-effort magic pick.
