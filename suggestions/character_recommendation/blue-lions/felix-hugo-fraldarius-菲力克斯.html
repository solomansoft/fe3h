Felix Hugo Fraldarius / 菲力克斯·雨果·弗拉尔达留斯 — Recommendation

Sword/Bow/Brawling strengths; Faith weakness. Budding talent: Reason → Black Magic Crit +10. Major Crest of Fraldarius (40% Mt+5). Personal: Lone Wolf (+5 dmg with no battalion). Elite combat growths: 55% Str / 55% Spd — one of the best player-phase damage units in the game [note.com JP "S"; GamerSky CN "best kitchen-knife"; GameWith JP physical ace]. Recruitable from other houses (Spd 15 + Sword B+).

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Myrmidon → Mercenary → Brigand(master)/Archer(master) → War Master — #1. Top Str growth class, Crit+20 works with any weapon, Quick Riposte mastery; his Brawling strength means War Master's brawling + axe both land. "Excellent synergy" + gauntlets exploit his 40% Crest [fe3h.com, mark-lxiii JP, Reddit "Felix- war master"]. Male-only — fine for Felix.
  2. Myrmidon → Mercenary → Swordmaster — #2. His natural class: Sword Aptitude + Sword Crit+10 stack with his Crest for relentless crits; lower investment, keeps Swords [mark-lxiii JP, Game8, GameWith JP]. Slightly lower ceiling than War Master's gauntlet quads but the easiest strong build.
  3. Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord — #3. 8-move flying + Canto; "best physical class" in general and great on Felix, but ⚠️ requires Axe to A and Flying A (he has neither strength) — high tutoring cost. Best for Maddening mobility [fe3h.com, Reddit, Sylvain-tier flier meta].
  4. Archer → Sniper → Bow Knight — #4. Hunter's Volley + ranged safety; [Reddit "either a Bow Knight or a War Master"]. Trades his elite melee for safety; solid but underuses his Str.
  5. Mortal Savant — #5 / avoid. [GamerSky CN] calls him a top Mortal Savant via his Reason budding, but it tanks his Spd and the class is widely panned ("utter garbage" [Reddit]; "rarely justifies returns" [pegasusknight JP]). Only for a magic-sword novelty build.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Certify in, master the class bar for its permanent ability, then leave — these are NOT his final class. 1. Brigand → Death Blow (Strength +6 when he initiates — "easy and core to every build"; maximizes his elite player-phase damage). 2. Archer → Hit +20 (flat +20 Hit — "easy and core to every build"; secures crit-fishing accuracy with his Crest). 3. War Master → Quick Riposte (guaranteed enemy-phase follow-up at high HP — strong on his Brawling routes even if not staying War Master). 4. Myrmidon → Speed +2 (flat +2 Spd, mastered early as his starting class; helps keep his doubling lead on Maddening).

Training focus

  • Sword to S (Swordfaire) for #2, or Axe + Brawling to A for #1 War Master. Bow as a strong secondary (he excels at it) for Hunter's Volley.
  • Master Brigand (Death Blow) and Archer (Hit+20) early — both easy and core to every build [fe3h.com].
  • Budding talent: Reason → Black Magic Crit+10 — only relevant for a Mortal Savant/magic novelty; skip for mainline builds.
  • Special care: Authority bane — keep him on cheap battalions (Jeralt's Mercenaries) or lean on Lone Wolf (battalion-less +5 dmg). Faith bane: never build him as a healer.

Supports / pairings

  • Gameplay: Sylvain & Ingrid (Linked Attack damage stacking) [fe3h.com]; Dimitri, Byleth.
  • Romance (S-support): Byleth (M/F), Annette (popular in-house pairing), Ingrid.
  • Synergy: keep him near Sylvain/Ingrid so Linked Attacks boost his already-huge player-phase damage.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Aegis Shield + a forged Silver/Killer Sword; Brave Sword for quads; gauntlets (Killer Knuckles) if War Master. Add a Bow for chip/Hunter's Volley.
  • Combat arts: Finesse Blade (Sword A), Sunder (Sword C+), Hunter's Volley (Bow A — broken), War Master's Strike / Mystic Blow (Brawl), Heavy Draw (Bow) [fe3h.com].
  • Equipped skills (5): Swordfaire/Fistfaire, Crit+20 (War Master), Death Blow, Hit+20, Sword Avoid+20 (tournament reward) or Quick Riposte [mark-lxiii JP].
  • Battalion/Gambit: cheap battalion (Authority bane) or none (Lone Wolf); take a utility gambit like Stride.
  • Key items: Speed Carrots / Seraph Robe; he wants to keep doubling on Maddening.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. War Master — #1, highest ceiling, gauntlet quads + Crit+20 [fe3h.com, Reddit, mark-lxiii JP].
  2. Swordmaster — #2, lowest-effort strong build, natural sword crits [Game8, GameWith JP, mark-lxiii JP].
  3. Wyvern Lord — #3, mobility king but ⚠️ Axe/Flying tutoring [fe3h.com, Reddit].
  4. Sniper / Bow Knight — #4, safe ranged [Reddit].
  5. Mortal Savant — avoid except novelty [GamerSky CN pro vs Reddit/pegasusknight con]. Verdict: the GamerSky Mortal Savant pick is contrarian; community consensus strongly favors War Master/Swordmaster.
