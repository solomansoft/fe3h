Dimitri Alexandre Blaiddyd / 蒂米特利·亚历山大·布雷达德 — Recommendation

House leader (Blue Lions). Lance/Sword + Authority strengths; Axe/Reason weaknesses. Budding talent: Riding → Seal Movement. Crest of Blaiddyd (Combat Art crit-double). Personal: Royal Lineage (+20% EXP; +20 Avoid at full HP in Part 2). Locked unit on Azure Moon — you keep him the whole game, so he is worth heavy investment. With 60% Str / 50% Spd / 55% HP growth he is widely rated the single strongest unit in the game [note.com JP "SSS"; GamerSky CN "infantry T1"].

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Soldier → (Cavalier/Brigand) → Paladin → Great Lord (unique) — #1. Great Lord is his signature class with top stat bonuses and access to both lance and Authority; Paladin gives Canto + 8 move on the way. "Really amps up his stats making him a powerhouse." [Game8] Great Lord is auto-granted at Ch.16 (no cert/exam); consider staying Paladin/Wyvern in maps needing mobility (Great Lord has no Canto) [fe3h.com].
  2. Soldier → Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord — #2. 8-move flying + Canto, huge offense. Best raw combat/mobility but ⚠️ requires Axe to A (Dimitri's Axe weakness must be tutored to certify Wyvern Lord's Axe A) and Flying training he lacks; high investment.
  3. Soldier → Mercenary → Hero/War Master — #3. [GameWith JP suggests Mercenary detour for the Wait/recovery ability]; [Reddit] "war master ideally." War Master needs Axe A + Brawl A — ⚠️ heavy retraining vs his Axe bane; viable but slower than #1.
  4. Great Lord with Dancer-style evasion — #4 niche. High avoid + Royal Lineage+ for single-digit enemy hit rates [fe3h.com]; fun but other units dodge-tank better.

⚠️ Wyvern Lord and War Master both demand Axe A — Dimitri's bane. Tutor Axe early if you want them, or take the Great Lord/Paladin lance route which plays to his strengths.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Certify in, master the class bar for its permanent ability, then leave — these are NOT his final class. 1. Brigand → Death Blow (Strength +6 when he initiates — "master Brigand for Death Blow unless going pure enemy-phase"; core to his player-phase lance damage). 2. Archer → Hit +20 (flat +20 Hit — fixes accuracy for Killer-weapon and combat-art crit fishing on the Wrath/Vantage build). 3. Soldier → Defense +2 (flat +2 Def, mastered early as his first class on the Great Lord route). 4. Mercenary → Vantage (attack first when struck below 50% HP — synergizes with the Battalion Wrath/Vantage enemy-phase combo; the Hero/War Master detour also passes through Mercenary).

Training focus

  • Lance to S+ (Lancefaire) — his primary weapon and best-in-class lance arts. Sword as backup (Swordfaire feasible too) [fe3h.com].
  • Authority to B/A — boosts gambits and unlocks better battalions; Authority is a strength.
  • Budding talent: Riding → unlocks Seal Movement, and Riding eases Cavalier/Paladin certification — train it.
  • Master Brigand for Death Blow (Str+6 on initiate) unless going pure enemy-phase [fe3h.com].
  • Special care: do NOT chase Axe/Reason classes unless committed to tutoring; his banes make Wyvern/War Master slow.

Supports / pairings

  • Gameplay: Dedue (Linked Attack, story-paired tank front line), Byleth, Felix, Ingrid, Sylvain.
  • Romance (S-support): Byleth (F!Byleth → Dimitri is the most-cited fan pick [Reddit]); also Mercedes, Annette, Ingrid for in-house pairings.
  • Synergy: pair with a Dancer (Annette/Ingrid) for an extra turn — Dimitri makes the most of bonus actions.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Areadbhar (his relic lance, Part 2) is the endgame pick; Killer Lance / Killer weapons for the Wrath/Vantage build; forged Silver Lance otherwise. Tempest Lance is a strong early scaling art [Reddit].
  • Combat arts: Glowing Ember (Lance A), Monster Piercer (Lance C+), Sunder/Windsweep (Sword) [fe3h.com].
  • Equipped skills (5): Battalion Wrath, Battalion Vantage, Death Blow, Lancefaire, Crit Avo+10 (or Hit+20). This is the "broken / game-breaking" enemy-phase combo [Reddit].
  • Battalion/Gambit: any high-Crest battalion + Retribution (best gambit, enables Battalion Wrath/Vantage loop ~Ch.8) [Reddit].
  • Key items: feed him Spd/Str boosters and the Crest items; he is the best investment target pre-timeskip [Reddit].

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Great Lord (lance) — #1, plays to strengths, no banes to fix [Game8, GamerSky CN].
  2. Wyvern Lord — #2, higher ceiling but ⚠️ Axe-bane tutoring [Reddit, note.com JP].
  3. War Master — #3, [Reddit] favorite, but Axe A + Brawl A retrain vs bane.
  4. Paladin (terminal) — #4, easy/Canto, slightly lower stats than Great Lord. - All sources agree Dimitri is top-tier regardless of class; the meta win condition is the Battalion Wrath + Battalion Vantage + Retribution enemy-phase combo [Reddit, GamerSky CN].
