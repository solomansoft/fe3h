Dedue Molinaro / 杜笃·莫利纳洛 — Recommendation

Lance/Axe/Brawling/Heavy Armor strengths; Faith/Riding/Flying weaknesses. No Crest, no budding talent. Personal: Staunch Shield (+4 Def on Wait). Growth profile is a pure tank: 60% HP / 50% Str / 50% Def, very low 20% Spd. Locked Blue Lions unit (Dimitri's retainer); note he leaves and returns in Part 2 on Azure Moon. Rated a defensive specialist [GameWith JP tank list; note.com JP "C / Duscur"].

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Commoner → Soldier → Armored Knight → Fortress Knight — #1. Plays directly to his Def strengths; Fortress Knight is "perfect," leveraging his extremely high defense to wall physical threats [Game8, GameWith JP]. Pick up Soldier's Reposition + Def+2 and Commoner's HP+5 on the way [fe3h.com].
  2. Fighter → Brigand → (Warrior) — #2. [GameWith JP] recommends switching to Warrior late because Fortress Knight's low movement is a liability; trades some bulk for mobility + Death Blow offense.
  3. Brawler → Grappler → War Master — #3 offensive build. Uses his Brawling strength for multi-hit damage; War Master Crit+20 + Quick Riposte make him a counter-attacking bruiser [fe3h.com]. ⚠️ Slower than the tank route and gives up his defensive identity.
  4. Great Knight — #4. Adds Riding mobility to an armored frame, but ⚠️ Riding is a weakness (must tutor to Riding B+) and Heavy Armor A is steep; marginal over Fortress [pegasusknight JP: "high skill demands for marginal improvement"].

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Certify in, master the class bar for its permanent ability, then leave — these are NOT his final class. 1. Soldier → Defense +2 (flat +2 Def — stacks his tank identity; he masters Soldier on the way to the Fortress Knight line anyway). 2. War Master → Quick Riposte (guaranteed follow-up on enemy phase at high HP — turns his counters into doubles; "great even if not staying" per his loadout, and on his Brawling offensive route). 3. Brigand → Death Blow (Strength +6 when he initiates — only for the Warrior/War Master offensive build; skip for the pure tank).

Training focus

  • Heavy Armor + Axe/Lance to certify the armored line; Brawling to B for Healing Focus and toward Grappler if going offensive.
  • Authority to B to command the unique Duscur Heavy Soldiers battalion (+10 protection) — strongly recommended [fe3h.com].
  • Death Blow "not necessary" for the tank build [fe3h.com]; grab it only for the Warrior/War Master offensive path.
  • Special care: do NOT invest in Faith/Riding/Flying (all banes). Keep him on foot armored unless deliberately going Warrior/War Master. His low Spd means he rarely doubles — lean into enemy-phase tanking + counters, not player-phase chasing.

Supports / pairings

  • Gameplay: Dimitri (story-paired, Linked Attack — keep them adjacent on the front line), Ashe, Mercedes, Ingrid.
  • Romance (S-support): Byleth (M or F). His chains are mostly story-flavor; no strong combat-romance synergy.
  • Synergy: park him next to Dimitri/squishy mages as a wall; Staunch Shield + Wait makes him an immovable guard tile.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: forged Steel/Silver Lance and Axe; Killer Axe for crit counters; a Brave weapon if going War Master; carry a Javelin/Hand Axe for 1–2 range chip on enemy phase.
  • Combat arts: Armored Strike (Axe A), Monster Breaker (Axe C+), Vengeance (Lance C+), Mighty Blow / One-Two Punch (Brawl) [fe3h.com].
  • Equipped skills (5): Defiant Def / Weight-3, Quick Riposte (War Master mastery — great even if not staying), Lance/Axefaire, Death Blow (offensive) or Armored Effect skills, plus his Staunch Shield is always-on.
  • Battalion/Gambit: Duscur Heavy Soldiers (his signature, +protection) with a strong defensive gambit; Retribution if available for enemy-phase punishment.
  • Key items: Spd boosters help most (his one weak stat); a Defense Ring stacks his identity.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Fortress Knight (tank) — #1, the consensus identity [Game8, GameWith JP, fe3h.com].
  2. Warrior — #2, [GameWith JP] mobility fix for Fortress's slow movement.
  3. War Master / Grappler — #3, [fe3h.com] offensive alt using Brawling.
  4. Great Knight — #4, ⚠️ Riding bane; marginal [pegasusknight JP]. - Disagreement: GameWith leans Warrior (mobility) vs Game8 pure Fortress (max defense). Verdict: Fortress for a defensive team, Warrior/War Master if you need him to keep pace and contribute offense.
