Mercedes von Martritz / 梅尔赛德司·冯·马尔特利兹 — Recommendation

Reason/Faith strengths; Sword/Lance/Axe/Heavy Armor weaknesses. Budding talent: Bow → Waning Shot. Minor Crest of Lamine (conserves healing uses). Personal: Live to Serve (self-heals equal to HP healed on allies) — one of the best healer personals in the game. Magic-focused growths (50% Mag / 45% Res / 45% Dex), low Spd doubling. The Blue Lions' designated healer and a strong magic support [GameWith JP healer; GamerSky CN heal/Gremory; note.com JP "A"]. Recruitable (Magic 15 + Bow C).

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Monk → Priest → Bishop → Gremory — #1. Gremory doubles spell/staff uses (huge for Live to Serve healing and her Reason offense) and is her highest magical ceiling; female-only, which fits [GamerSky CN, note.com JP, GameWith JP "Bishop → Gremory"]. Requires Reason A + Faith A — both are her strengths, so easy.
  2. Monk → Priest → Bishop (terminal) — #2. Best pure healer: learns Fortify/long-range Physic, the dedicated healer class [Game8, fe3h.com]. Lower offense than Gremory but cheaper (Faith A only) and the standard pick if you just want a medic.
  3. Monk → Mage → Warlock → Gremory — #3. Reason-leaning offensive caster route to the same Gremory endpoint; emphasizes nuking (she has good Reason). Slightly less healing-forward than #1 but same terminal class.
  4. Archer → Sniper (Bow build) — #4 niche. Uses her Bow budding (Waning Shot) and good Dex for a physic-bow hybrid; underuses her Magic and is generally a novelty [fe3h.com mentions Waning Shot].

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Certify in, master the class bar for its permanent ability, then leave — these are NOT her final class. 1. Mage → Fiendish Blow (Magic +6 when she initiates — boosts her Reason offense; already listed in her loadout and a stop on the offensive Warlock route). 2. Monk → Magic +2 (flat +2 Magic — listed in her loadout; mastered early as her opening caster class). 3. Priest → White Magic Heal +5 (+5 healing output — amplifies her Live to Serve self-sustain; she passes through Priest on the way to Bishop/Gremory). 4. Archer → Hit +20 (flat +20 Hit — only for the niche Waning Shot bow-hybrid build).

Training focus

  • Faith to A — rush Physic ASAP (earliest Ch.3; manage Ch.2 with Heal) and toward Fortify/Warp; Faith A also gates Bishop/Gremory [fe3h.com].
  • Reason to A for Gremory + offensive spells (she gets Fimbulvetr/strong Reason list).
  • Budding talent: Bow → Waning Shot — optional, only for a bow hybrid; her Faith/Reason come first.
  • Authority for healing/support battalions.
  • Special care: very low Spd and physical-weapon banes — never melee her. Keep her behind the line. Live to Serve means healing allies keeps HER topped off, so she sustains herself.

Supports / pairings

  • Gameplay: Annette — A-support grants mutual +3 Might, a top combat-support pairing; build them together [fe3h.com]. Also Dimitri, Dedue, Ashe, Sylvain.
  • Romance (S-support): Byleth (M/F), Sylvain (also triggers his Philanderer), Dedue.
  • Synergy: pair-position with Annette for the +3 Mt buff; healing a frontliner via Live to Serve doubles as self-sustain.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: healing staves (Physic, Fortify, eventually Warp) + offensive Reason tomes (Fimbulvetr, Bolganone); Caduceus-tier staff if available. Bow only for the niche build.
  • Combat arts: Waning Shot (Bow, niche) — her value is spells/staves, not arts.
  • Equipped skills (5): Live to Serve (always-on personal), Faith Prowess, Reason Prowess, Magic +2 (Monk mastery), Fiendish Blow (Mage mastery) or Defiant Res; Gremory's effect doubles staff/spell uses.
  • Battalion/Gambit: a Magic/Res battalion; Restore/heal-supporting gambit or a Hit-boosting one for her offensive spells.
  • Key items: feed her Spd boosters (her one weakness) and any Magic boosters; a Speed Carrot helps her avoid getting doubled.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Gremory — #1, doubles spell/staff uses; best ceiling for heal + nuke [GamerSky CN, GameWith JP, note.com JP].
  2. Bishop — #2, dedicated healer, cheaper [Game8, fe3h.com].
  3. Warlock→Gremory (offensive route) — #3, same endpoint, nuke-leaning.
  4. Sniper (bow hybrid) — #4 niche [fe3h.com]. - Consensus: she is a healer first; Gremory vs Bishop is the only real debate. Verdict: Gremory if you train both Reason+Faith to A (more value), Bishop if you want her purely as a low-investment medic.
