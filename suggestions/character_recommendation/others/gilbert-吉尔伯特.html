Gilbert Pronislav (Gustave) / 吉尔伯特 — Gameplay Recommendations

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Knights of Seiros. Azure Moon (Blue Lions) ONLY; auto-joins Ch.13 (War Phase) at ~Lv26 Fortress Knight. No academy-phase access, no other route. Sources: EN (Game8, GameFAQs, Reddit, Neoseeker, Fandom), JP (note.com/Altema), CN (GamerSky context).

Snapshot

The weakest of the church/faculty roster by reputation. Joins very late (Ch.13) as a Lv26 Fortress Knight with high Def/HP but low Spd/Res, mediocre Mag, and no Crest. Note: he uses a weaker Fortress Knight variant carried over from his Ch.5 NPC appearance. He is best treated as an emergency wall, not a damage carry. [note.com JP ranks him "Unusable / 戦力外" — the only roster member so rated.]

Class-change path (stack-ranked, to Master tier)

  1. #1 — Great Knight (大骑士) [Reddit, GameFAQs EN]. - Cert: Axe A / Heavy Armor A / Riding A (Master Seal). He has Lance/Axe/Riding strengths but Heavy Armor is a weakness for him — ⚠️ Heavy Armor A will need tutoring/grinding. - Rationale: keeps his armored-tank identity but fixes the Fortress Knight's 4-move problem with mounted mobility. "Great Knight can make good use of this." Suggested build: Axe Prowess, Lance Prowess, Death Blow, Aegis, Pavise. [Reddit]
  2. #2 — Fortress Knight (重装骑士) [Game8 EN]. - Cert: Heavy Armor A (Advanced). "High defenses synergize with the Fortress Knight; gives you a good tank." [Game8] Downside: 4 move and weak Res. Cheapest tank option; he's already armored on join.
  3. #3 — Paladin / Wyvern Lord — Paladin (Lance/Riding, no armor needed) is arguably the most efficient given his Heavy Armor weakness; Wyvern Lord wants Axe/Flying A (he lacks Flying). Both viable detours if you'd rather skip his armor weakness, but his low Spd hurts.

⚠️ DO give up on magic entirely: "arguably the worst playable character to use magic with." [Reddit]

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into ONLY to fill the class-mastery bar and learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave — these are NOT part of the final class path above. - Fortress Knight → Pavise (chance to halve physical damage) — core tank skill to carry into Great Knight; he joins already armored so it's cheap. - Paladin → Aegis (chance to halve ranged/magic damage) — the other half of the Aegis/Pavise wall combo; Paladin also sidesteps his Heavy Armor weakness. - Brigand → Death Blow (+6 Str when this unit initiates combat) — gives his weak offense some bite. - Soldier → Defense +2 — cheap early permanent Def bump that suits his wall role.

Training focus

  • Lance / Axe → A, Riding → A (Great Knight) or just Heavy Armor → A (Fortress).
  • Authority (his strength) → high for strong battalions — his real value is as a battalion-buffed wall; personal Veteran Knight gives -2 damage while a battalion is equipped.
  • Brigand dip for Death Blow to give his attacks some bite.
  • Skip Reason/Faith.
  • Special-care: joins at Lv26 but with low Spd he gets doubled by fast enemies — keep him as a gambit/tank/Aegis-Pavise unit rather than an attacker.

Supports / pairings

  • Annette (his daughter — story-significant support and the canonical pairing) and Byleth, Dimitri, Mercedes.
  • Park him beside fragile units as a damage soak; pair with a healer.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapon: Silver Lance+ / Killer Lance, Silver Axe; a Javelin/Hand Axe for chip at range.
  • Combat arts: lance/axe smash arts for chip damage.
  • 5 equipped skills (suggested): Lance Prowess 5 / Axe Prowess 5, Death Blow, Aegis, Pavise, Authority/Battalion-defense skill (or Quick Riposte if reachable). [Reddit build]
  • Battalion/Gambit: a high-defense / high-Endurance battalion to maximize Veteran Knight; Retribution so even a slow tank punishes attackers.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Great Knight — [Reddit EN], [GameFAQs EN] best master class for him (mobility + Aegis/Pavise tank).
  2. Fortress Knight — [Game8 EN] simplest, no reclass, leans into his Def; but immobile.
  3. Paladin — efficient pick that sidesteps his Heavy Armor weakness (not in the cited best-class lists but mechanically sound). - Disagreement on usability: [note.com JP] = "unusable / 戦力外" (worst on the roster — too late, too slow, AM-only). EN sources don't call him unusable but agree he's a backup tank, "still suboptimal vs Wyvern Lord/War Master units." [GameFAQs EN] - Verdict on investment: Lowest priority of all 9. Use him as a stopgap wall in Azure Moon Part 2 if you need a body; don't pour resources in. If you must build him, Great Knight + Aegis/Pavise + a defense battalion is the play.
