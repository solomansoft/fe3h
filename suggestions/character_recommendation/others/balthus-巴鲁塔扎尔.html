Balthus von Albrecht 巴鲁塔扎尔·冯·阿尔布雷希特 — Build Recommendation

Ashen Wolves (Cindered Shadows DLC). Recruitable into Part 1 after clearing Cindered Shadows Ch.4; appears at ~Lv3, instantly a top early unit on Maddening. Strengths: Sword, Axe, Brawling, Faith, Heavy Armor. Personal King of Grappling (+6 Str/+6 Def when HP ≤ 50%). High HP/Str/Def growths, low Dex/Spd/Lck.

Grounded in: others/ashen-wolves-dlc/balthus-巴鲁塔扎尔.md, classes/ (War Master, Grappler, War Monk context).

Class-Change Path (stack-ranked, to Master tier)

#1 — War Master (战神) — BEST endgame (male Master)

  • Why: Plays straight to his Brawl + Axe + Str. Axefaire + Fistfaire + Crit +20 + mastery Quick Riposte. "Choose War Master for Balthus to use high-strength skills and grappling." [Game8 EN], [GameWith JP cache]. King of Grappling makes him hit HARDER as he drops low — pairs naturally with a tanky front-line bruiser.
  • Path: Brawler → Grappler → War Master (Master Seal). ⚠️ Brawl A + Axe A — both strengths, cheap to reach. Male-only (fine).

#2 — Grappler (综合格斗家) — argued best by some

  • Why: "Balthus' overall best class is Grappler. Tripling combat art (Fierce Iron Fist = 3 hits) and Grappler not having movement penalties > +20 crit." [Reddit cache]. 40% HP growth, +3 Spd/+3 Dex helps his weak Spd; the 3-hit combat art can out-damage War Master's crit on tough single targets, and no Mov penalty. Advanced class but a legit endgame pick for him.
  • ⚠️ Brawl A only — easiest path of all.

#3 — War Monk (战僧, DLC) — his canon class, brawl + self-heal

  • Why: Gauntlets + Axe + Faith — uses his Faith strength to self-heal mid-brawl (one of only 3 brawl+magic classes). Fistfaire; great sustain for a front-liner who wants to dip low (synergy with King of Grappling). Lower ceiling than War Master damage-wise but more self-sufficient. [Game8 EN: "War Monk is his original class."]
  • ⚠️ Must certify Priest first; uses Abyssian Exam Pass. Brawl + Faith both strengths.

Alternatives

  • Fortress Knight / Great Knight: leans Heavy Armor strength for a pure wall; works with King of Grappling but wastes his offense. Niche.
  • Wyvern Lord: Flying is a weakness — avoid.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into ONLY to fill the class-mastery bar and learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave — these are NOT part of the final class path above. - Brigand → Death Blow (+6 Str when this unit initiates combat) — top priority; huge on his already high Str for every brawl/axe build. - Brawler → Unarmed Combat (retained as an equippable gauntlet-combat skill) — cheap, since Brawl is his core strength and he passes through it toward Grappler/War Master anyway. - Fighter → Strength +2 — early, low-cost permanent stat bump while he's still in beginner classes. - Archer → Hit +20 (+20 Hit) — fixes his low Dex/Spd accuracy on the brawl builds.

Training Focus

  • Brawling to A is mandatory and free (his core strength) — unlocks Grappler/War Master/War Monk.
  • Axe to A for War Master; Faith to B+ if going War Monk (self-heal).
  • His budding talent Reason → Black Magic Crit +10 is mostly a curiosity; skip unless you want a gimmick — focus Brawl.
  • Mastery skills to grab: Death Blow (+6 Str on initiate, huge for him), Fierce Iron Fist (Grappler combat art), Quick Riposte (War Master), Healing Focus gambit synergy.
  • His low Spd/Dex means he wants Hit +20 and accuracy support; King of Grappling wants him intentionally a bit low on HP.

Supports / Pairings

  • Strong supports with Yuri, Constance, Hapi (Ashen Wolves), Hilda (childhood ties), Holst-adjacent units, and Byleth.
  • Synergy: keep a healer near him so he can be brought to ~50% HP safely to trigger King of Grappling, then he tanks + hits hard. Pair with a Dancer to re-act after a big Fierce Iron Fist.

Weapon / Item / Battalion / Skill Loadout

  • War Master build (best): Aura Knuckles / Killer Knuckles + a Brave/Killer Axe.
  • 5 skills: Fistfaire, Axefaire, Death Blow, Crit +20, Quick Riposte (mastery).
  • Battalion: high-Atk / armored battalion; offensive or Retribution gambit.
  • Grappler build: Aura Knuckles; skills Fistfaire, Death Blow, Fierce Iron Fist (combat art), Hit +20, Quick Riposte.
  • War Monk build: gauntlets + Faith tome (Heal/Recover); skills Fistfaire, Death Blow, Healing-focused, Hit +20, Brawl Avo +20.
  • Key items: Knuckle weapons (gauntlets); his strengths make the brawling-relic-free but Aura Knuckles / forged Killer Knuckles shine.

Ranked Options & Disagreements

  1. War Master — Crit +20 + dual faire + Quick Riposte; Game8/JP top pick. [Game8] [GameWith JP]
  2. Grappler — 3-hit Fierce Iron Fist + no Mov penalty; Reddit argues it edges War Master vs tough targets. [Reddit]
  3. War Monk (DLC) — canon class, brawl + Faith self-heal, most self-sufficient. [Game8] - Disagreement: Game8/JP rank War Master #1 for crit/damage; a vocal Reddit camp prefers Grappler for the tripling combat art and mobility. Both agree Brawl is his identity; avoid Flying (weakness).
