Hapi 哈匹 — Build Recommendation

Ashen Wolves (Cindered Shadows DLC). Recruitable into Part 1 after clearing Cindered Shadows Ch.5. Strengths: Reason, Riding, Flying. Personal Monstrous Appeal (her attacks are effective vs monsters; monsters more likely to target her). Solid Mag (45%) and Dex/Spd; very low Def/Lck. Praised by community as having "probably the best spell list in the game." [Reddit cache]

Grounded in: others/ashen-wolves-dlc/hapi-哈匹.md, classes/ (Dark Knight, Gremory, Warlock, Valkyrie/Dark Flier context).

Class-Change Path (stack-ranked, to Master tier)

#1 — Dark Knight (暗骑士) — BEST endgame

  • Why: Mounted (7 Mov) + Canto + Dark Tomefaire (the ONLY player class with Dark Tomefaire — crucial since her best spells, e.g. Banshee Θ / Death Θ, are dark magic), high Res, +Mag growth. "You can opt for a Dark Knight class... abuse her Canto ability" for hit-and-retreat. [Game8 EN]. Riding is one of her strengths, so the Riding A req is cheap.
  • Path: Mage → Warlock (grow Mag) → Dark Knight (Master Seal: Lance C / Reason B+ / Riding A).
  • ⚠️ Lance C is off-profile (minor) but Riding A is a strength → overall easy for her.

#2 — Valkyrie (圣魔导, DLC, mounted) — range + accuracy

  • Why: "Hapi works best as the Abyssian class Valkyrie." [Game8 EN]. Mounted caster, +1 magic range + Canto + mastery Uncanny Blow (Hit +30 on initiate → fixes long-range tome accuracy). Great for safe artillery + mobility. Trade-off: no Dark Tomefaire, so it slightly weakens her signature dark spells vs Dark Knight.

#3 — Gremory (格莫瑞, female-only) — best raw casting / sustain

  • Why: "I would say Gremory... +1 mvm & double dark spells > +10 heal" over Bishop. [Reddit cache]. +5 Mag, +Mag growth, and all spell uses x2 (doubles her excellent dark + reason list and any healing). Foot/slow (5 Mov) loses the Canto hit-and-run, but maximizes spell economy.
  • ⚠️ Reason A + Faith A (Faith off-profile → some tutoring).

Alternatives

  • Dark Flier (暗天马, DLC): Flying is a strength, BUT Dark Flier has NO Dark Tomefaire — this specifically weakens Hapi since her best spells are dark magic. [Fandom cache]. Use only if you want pure flying mobility over damage.
  • Bishop: healing utility (she has Crest of Timotheos = conserves recovery uses), but cache says her offense (double dark spells) is worth more than +10 heal. Niche.
  • Avoid Brawling/Authority/Heavy Armor (weaknesses).

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into ONLY to fill the class-mastery bar and learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave — these are NOT part of the final class path above. - Mage → Fiendish Blow (+6 Mag when this unit initiates combat) — top priority; the core offensive skill for her dark/black spell list, and Mage is her natural beginner step. - Valkyrie (DLC) → Uncanny Blow (Hit boost when initiating) — fixes long-range tome accuracy; grab it even if you finish as Dark Knight (it's also a valid final class for her). - Gremory → Defiant Mag (big Mag spike when HP is low) — if you pass through Gremory, take it as a low-HP damage spike (Gremory is also a valid final class). - Monk → Magic +2 — cheap early permanent Mag bump in the beginner tier.

Training Focus

  • Reason to A is the core (her dark + black spell list is elite). Riding to A (a strength) for Dark Knight.
  • For Valkyrie/Gremory bring some Faith up.
  • Build Mag growth in Warlock/Gremory before settling into a mounted shell (Dark Knight does add Mag growth, so it's fine to finish there).
  • Mastery skills: Fiendish Blow (+6 Mag on initiate), Seal Resistance (Dark Knight mastery — debuffs enemy Res), Uncanny Blow (Valkyrie — accuracy), Defiant Mag (Gremory).
  • Note Monstrous Appeal: she draws monster aggro AND is effective vs them → deliberately use her as the anti-monster specialist, but keep her healed (low Def).

Supports / Pairings

  • Strong supports with Constance (paired backstory), Yuri, Balthus (Ashen Wolves), Byleth, Linhardt.
  • Synergy: pair near a healer/tank — she's frail (low Def/Lck). Use Canto (Dark Knight/Valkyrie) to strike a Res-weak target and pull back. Deploy her on maps with monsters to exploit Monstrous Appeal, with a body-blocker controlling the aggro.

Weapon / Item / Battalion / Skill Loadout

  • Dark Knight build (best): dark/reason tomes — Banshee Θ, Death Θ, Mire Β, plus a Levin Sword for safe melee chip.
  • 5 skills: Dark Tomefaire, Black Tomefaire, Fiendish Blow, Hit +20, Seal Resistance (mastery).
  • Battalion: magic-attack battalion; Gambit: an offensive/Res-debuff gambit.
  • Valkyrie build: same tomes; skills Black Tomefaire, Fiendish Blow, Uncanny Blow (mastery), Hit +20, Avo +10. Equip a magic-range item (Thyrsus) for absurd Bolting/Banshee range.
  • Gremory build: doubled spell uses; skills Black Tomefaire, Fiendish Blow, Defiant Mag, Hit +20, Bowbreaker.
  • Key items: Thyrsus (magic-range relic) is excellent on her; stat boosters to shore up Def/Lck.
