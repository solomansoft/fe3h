Constance von Nuvelle 康丝坦兹·冯·努维尔 — Build Recommendation

Ashen Wolves (Cindered Shadows DLC). Recruitable into Part 1 after clearing Cindered Shadows Ch.2. Strengths: Sword, Reason, Authority, Flying. Personal Circadian Beat (+3 Str/+3 Mag indoors; +3 Def/+3 Res outdoors). Massive Mag growth (60%) but very frail (low HP/Str/Def/Lck).

Grounded in: others/ashen-wolves-dlc/constance-康丝坦兹.md, classes/ (Gremory, Warlock, Dark Flier/Valkyrie context).

Class-Change Path (stack-ranked, to Master tier)

#1 — Dark Flier (暗天马, DLC, female-only) — BEST endgame

  • Why: "Constance actually is best as a Dark Flier." [Reddit cache], [Game8 EN]. Flying (one of her strengths!) + Canto + 8 Mov turns the fragile glass-cannon into a mobile nuke that hits Res-weak targets and retreats. Her 60% Mag carries the damage even without a magic-growth class.
  • ⚠️ Critical caveat (cross-source): Dark Flier has NO Tomefaire (no +5 magic damage) and NO Magic-growth bonus (it gives Spd/Res growth instead). So level her up in Warlock/Bishop/Gremory first for the +10% Mag growth, THEN switch to Dark Flier late for mobility. [segmentnext / Fandom cache].
  • Path: Mage → Warlock (grow Mag) → Dark Flier (Abyssian Master Seal, Reason B+ / Flying C, Lv20+).

#2 — Gremory (格莫瑞, female-only) — best raw magic output

  • Why: +5 Mag modifier, +10% Mag growth, and all spell uses x2 → double Bolting / double Meteor / double Agnea's Arrow. "Unless you want to make her a Gremory for double Bolting purposes." [Reddit cache]. Foot (5 Mov) is the trade-off vs Dark Flier mobility. Best if you want her as a back-line artillery nuker.
  • ⚠️ Reason A + Faith A. Faith is NOT a strength for her → some tutoring needed, but doable.

#3 — Valkyrie (圣魔导, DLC, mounted) — range + accuracy fix

  • Why: Mounted caster with +1 magic range (stacks with relics) and Canto; mastery Uncanny Blow "greatly helps Constance's accuracy issues when she initiates combat, in particular with Bolting." [WebSearch / Game8]. Solves her shaky long-range hit rates while keeping mobility.
  • ⚠️ Reason B+ + Faith C + Riding (Valkyrie reqs); some Riding/Faith tutoring.

Alternatives

  • Warlock (术师): good intermediate growth class (Black Magic x2, Bowbreaker) — use as a stepping stone to grow Mag, not a final class.
  • Bishop: if you want Faith utility, but she's an offensive mage at heart. Avoid Axe/Heavy Armor (weaknesses).

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into ONLY to fill the class-mastery bar and learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave — these are NOT part of the final class path above. - Mage → Fiendish Blow (+6 Mag when this unit initiates combat) — top priority; the cornerstone offensive skill for her 60% Mag glass-cannon, and Mage is her natural beginner step. - Valkyrie (DLC) → Uncanny Blow (Hit boost when initiating) — fixes her shaky long-range Bolting accuracy even if you finish as Dark Flier/Gremory; certify, master, then move on. - Gremory → Defiant Mag (big Mag spike when HP is low) — if you pass through Gremory, grab it for a damage panic-button (and Gremory is also a valid final class for her). - Monk → Magic +2 — cheap early permanent Mag bump while she's still in beginner classes.

Training Focus

  • Reason to A (her damage core) and Faith up if going Gremory/Valkyrie (her accuracy/utility).
  • Build Mag growth in Warlock/Gremory BEFORE finalizing Dark Flier (Dark Flier gives no Mag growth).
  • Flying to C+ for Dark Flier (it's a strength — cheap).
  • Budding talent Brawling → Mystic Blow (reduces magic combat-art cost) — minor; only if you experiment with magic combat arts.
  • Exploit Circadian Beat: she gets +3 Mag indoors → she's strongest on indoor maps; outdoors she gains Def/Res (survival) instead.

Supports / Pairings

  • Strong supports with Yuri, Balthus, Hapi (Ashen Wolves), Hapi especially (paired backstory), Ferdinand, Byleth.
  • Synergy: she is FRAGILE — pair with a front-line bruiser (Balthus) to body-block, and a Dancer to re-cast Bolting. Keep her behind the line; use Dark Flier/Valkyrie Canto to hit-and-retreat. Avoid putting her in enemy bow/archer range (flier = bow-weak).

Weapon / Item / Battalion / Skill Loadout

  • Dark Flier build (best): Reason tomes — Bolting (long range), Agnea's Arrow, Fimbulvetr; keep a Levin Sword for safe 1–2 chip.
  • 5 skills: Black Tomefaire (carry it over from Warlock/Mortal-Savant access since Dark Flier lacks it), Fiendish Blow (+6 Mag on initiate), Uncanny Blow (from Valkyrie mastery — fixes hit), Hit +20, Avo +10 / Alert Stance+.
  • Battalion: magic-attack battalion; Gambit: offensive (e.g. a magic gambit) for the +Mt and the gambit-boost zone.
  • Gremory build: same tomes but doubled uses; skills Black Tomefaire, Fiendish Blow, Defiant Mag (mastery), Hit +20, Bowbreaker.
  • Key items: Caduceus Staff equivalent / magic range items; Thyrsus (magic-range relic) is GREAT on her if available — pushes Bolting range absurdly far.
