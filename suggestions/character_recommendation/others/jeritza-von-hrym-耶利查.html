Jeritza von Hrym 耶利查·冯·赫里姆 — Build Recommendation

⚠️ PLAYABLE ONLY ON CRIMSON FLOWER (红花线). On every other route he is the enemy boss "Death Knight (死亡骑士)" and cannot be recruited. Requires Expansion Pass (Wave 3 DLC). Joins automatically ~Ch.13 at high level in his unique Death Knight class.

Grounded in: others/special-recruits/jeritza-von-hrym-耶利查.md, classes/ (Wyvern Lord, War Master, Death Knight context).

Class-Change Path (stack-ranked, to Master tier)

#1 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主) — BEST endgame

  • Why: Jeritza joins with monstrous Str (32 @ Lv27) and the game's near-highest Spd growth (60%). Wyvern Lord (8 Mov, Flying, Canto, Avo +10, +4 Str/+4 Spd/+3 Def) turns that into a flying one-man-army. His budding talent is Flying → Darting Blow, so the Flying requirement is the natural route. "Wyvern Lord is arguably the best class in the game in terms of balanced offense, defense, avoid, and movement." [Reddit cache], [pegasusknight.com JP cache].
  • Path: unlock Flying budding talent → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord.
  • ⚠️ Tutoring: needs Lance C / Axe A / Flying A. He has Lance + Sword + Brawl strengths but NOT Axe, so Axe is the main grind. Mastermind (his class skill / his 2x class-EXP situation) and high level make mastery fast despite the late join.
  • Lighter option: even just Wyvern Rider "is a perfectly fine endgame class especially in CF and requires very little investment after you unlock his budding talent." [Reddit cache]

#2 — War Master (战神) — top raw damage, foot

  • Why: Plays to Str + the Sword/Lance/Brawl strengths; Axefaire + Fistfaire + Crit +20 + Quick Riposte. With his Spd he secures doubles and crits everything. Foot (6 Mov) loses Wyvern's mobility/avoid. [GameWith JP cache], [Game8 EN].
  • ⚠️ Tutoring: Axe A + Brawl A (Brawl already a strength). Male-only — fine for Jeritza.

#3 — Death Knight (死亡骑士) — his unique join class, zero-effort

  • Why: Mounted (Mov), strong bases, can use the Scythe of Sariel relic and lances. No training cost — keep him in it if you want him deployable immediately on a short CF run. Lacks the avoid/Crit of the above.

Alternatives / niche

  • Mortal Savant / Dark Knight: he has Sword/Lance but no Reason; not worth it (Faith is a weakness). Skip.
  • Paladin / Great Knight: serviceable mounted physical, but Wyvern Lord strictly better for him.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into ONLY to fill the class-mastery bar and learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave — these are NOT part of the final class path above. - Brigand → Death Blow (+6 Str when this unit initiates combat) — top priority; stacks onto his already monstrous Str for one-round kills. - Pegasus Knight / Wyvern Rider → Darting Blow (+5 Spd when initiating) — compounds his game-high Spd growth for guaranteed doubles (lines up with his Flying budding talent). - War Master → Quick Riposte (HP-high: always follow up on enemy phase) — only if you take the War Master path; otherwise grab via that build. - Archer → Hit +20 (+20 Hit) — accuracy insurance, optional.

Training Focus

  • Push Axe to A for Wyvern Lord/War Master (his one missing physical skill); Sword/Lance/Brawl come free.
  • Unlock Flying budding talent early → Darting Blow (+Spd on initiate) compounds his already great Spd.
  • A few Faith levels (despite being a weakness) to learn Restore can help on long CF maps. [GameFAQs cache]
  • Skills to grab via mastery: Death Blow, Darting Blow, Quick Riposte (War Master), Defiant Crit (Wyvern).

Supports / Pairings

  • Jeritza has a limited support list (CF only). Notable: Byleth (S-support available — his romance is CF-exclusive and well-regarded), Mercedes (his sister; strong story support), Bernadetta, Constance, Hubert.
  • Gameplay: pair him near Mercedes/a healer to offset his Faith weakness; his Murderous Intent (+20 Hit on initiate) makes him a reliable player-phase deleter — position him to start fights.

Weapon / Item / Battalion / Skill Loadout

  • Wyvern Lord build: Scythe of Sariel (his relic, lance-type) or a forged Brave Lance/Axe; Killer weapons to exploit his Crit potential.
  • 5 skills: Lancefaire/Axefaire, Death Blow, Darting Blow, Hit +20 (or Quick Riposte), Avo +10 / Alert Stance+.
  • Battalion: high-Atk, decent-Authority battalion; Gambit: Retribution (enemy-phase) or an offensive gambit. [Reddit cache]
  • War Master build: Brave Axe + gauntlets; skills Axefaire, Fistfaire, Crit +20, Death Blow, Quick Riposte.
  • Key item: Scythe of Sariel (auto with Death Knight class). Crest of Lamine occasionally conserves Faith uses if you run Restore.

Ranked Options & Disagreements

  1. Wyvern Lord — best mobility + offense + avoid; matches his Flying budding talent. [Reddit] [pegasusknight.com JP]
  2. War Master — highest raw damage/crit, foot-fragile. [GameWith JP] [Game8]
  3. Wyvern Rider / Death Knight — low-investment endgame for short CF runs. [Reddit] - Disagreement: some prefer keeping him Death Knight for zero setup on a fast CF clear; meta sources push Wyvern Lord once Flying is unlocked. His Mastermind mitigates the late-join mastery problem either way. [GameFAQs cache]
