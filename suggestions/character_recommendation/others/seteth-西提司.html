Seteth / 西提司 — Gameplay Recommendations

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Church of Seiros (auto-joins ~Lv23 Wyvern Rider on SS / AM / VW; enemy on Crimson Flower) Sources span EN (fe3h.com, Game8, GameFAQs, Reddit), JP (note.com/Altema personal tier, pegasusknight), CN (GamerSky).

Snapshot

Late auto-recruit but joins as a Level 23 Wyvern Rider with boons in essentially every skill above E (Sword/Lance/Axe/Authority/Riding), a Major Crest, and 8-move flying. He is one of the few late joiners who is immediately strong and clearly worth investing in — community verdict is near-unanimous.

Class-change path (stack-ranked, to Master tier)

  1. #1 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主) [Game8, fe3h.com, pegasusknight JP]. - Cert: Lance C / Axe A / Flying A (Master Seal). He starts B Axe, B Lance, C Flying — only Flying and a bit of Axe need topping up. No tutoring strictly required, but tutor Lance/Flying to speed him up. - Rationale: he literally joins in the wyvern line; staying the course wastes none of the Part-1 skill-building time he missed. Wyvern Lord is the game's best physical class (no offense/defense gaps; master skill Defiant Crit +50% crit below 25% HP). Flying + Canto turns him into a hit-and-run double-tapper. [pegasusknight JP: "Wyvern Lord — no gaps in offense or defense, best physical damage class."]
  2. #2 — Wyvern Rider → (detour) Brigand for Death Blow → back to Wyvern Lord. Optional dip into Brigand (Axe D) to master Death Blow (+6 Str on player-phase initiation) before promoting. Best if you have spare time; otherwise skip.
  3. #3 — Paladin / Holy Knight (mounted, magic-flex) — only if you specifically want a magic-capable mounted unit (he has 35% Mag growth and a B-rank Authority). Weaker than Wyvern Lord for him; not recommended.

⚠️ Certification note: Flying A is the gating requirement; tutor Flying if your professor rank/time is short. No gender lock issues.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into ONLY to fill the class-mastery bar and learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave — these are NOT part of the final class path above. - Brigand → Death Blow (+6 Str when this unit initiates combat) — top priority; +6 Str on Swift Strikes is enormous (called out as the key min-max dip for him). - Pegasus Knight / Wyvern Rider → Darting Blow (+5 Spd when initiating) — patches his mediocre Spd toward more reliable doubles. - Archer → Hit +20 (+20 Hit) — accuracy insurance for his lance/axe arts. - Soldier → Defense +2 — cheap early permanent bump if you route him through beginner classes.

Training focus

  • Lance → A for his signature combat art Swift Strikes (the single biggest reason to use him). It is player-phase 1–2 hits that bypass his mediocre Speed, scales off his high Str, and with the Major Crest of Cichol has a 75% chance to deny enemy counters. [fe3h.com]
  • Flying → A to certify Wyvern Lord.
  • Axe → A (he's already B) — keeps Wyvern Lord and gives axe combat arts.
  • Authority already B at recruit; push to A/A+ for strong battalions and Gambits — he's an above-average gambit user.
  • Don't bother with his magic.

Supports / pairings

  • Flayn — story-mandated and mechanically excellent: equip Flayn as his attack adjutant for up to +6 damage on Swift Strikes. [fe3h.com]
  • Byleth (S-support available, M or F), Manuela, Catherine, Alois.
  • His personal Guardian (+3 damage to adjacent female allies) makes him a good escort for female damage dealers (Catherine, Shamir, Flayn).

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapon: Steel/Silver Lance + Killer Axe; Spear of Assal or a Brave Lance late.
  • Combat art: Swift Strikes (primary), plus an axe art for chip.
  • 5 equipped skills (suggested): Lancefaire / Axefaire (whichever weapon you lead with), Death Blow, Darting Blow (or Defiant Crit), Lance Prowess 5, Swift Strikes is a combat art not a skill so pair with Hit +20 or Battalion Wrath.
  • Battalion/Gambit: a high-Phys-attack flying-compatible battalion; Retribution gambit synergizes with Battalion Wrath/Vantage enemy-phase tech.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Wyvern Lord — [Game8 EN], [fe3h.com EN], [pegasusknight JP] all agree; best all-round physical flier.
  2. Stay Wyvern Rider longer / Brigand detour for Death Blow — [GameFAQs/Reddit EN] min-max polish.
  3. Magic/mounted flex (Holy Knight) — fringe, only [note.com JP] hints at his magic stat being usable; widely seen as inferior. - Verdict on investment: Worth it. [note.com JP] places him A-tier despite late join ("strong stats"); [fe3h.com EN] "highly worth investing in ... viable immediately and all game." Among church/faculty units he and Shamir/Catherine are the safest investments.
