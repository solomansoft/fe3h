Anna 安娜 — Build Recommendation

Special recruit (Wave 3 DLC / Expansion Pass). Recruitable into any house/route as early as Ch.3, no requirements. High Luck (personal skill Business Prosperity = +5 Lck) and high Speed. Anna CANNOT build supports with anyone — plan her as a self-sufficient combat unit, not a pair-up piece.

Grounded in: others/special-recruits/anna-安娜.md, classes/ (Wyvern Lord, Sniper, Assassin, Trickster context).

Class-Change Path (stack-ranked, to Master tier)

#1 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主) — BEST overall

  • Why: "Wyvern Lord patches up her only stat weakness, which is her strength." [Reddit cache]. 8 Mov + Flying + Canto + Avo +10 + the +4 Str bonus fixes her mediocre Str (35% growth) while her high Spd (55%) and Luck make her dodge-y and double-happy.
  • Path: Myrmidon → Brigand (Axe + Death Blow) → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord.
  • ⚠️ Tutoring: Lance C / Axe A / Flying A. Anna has Axe + Sword + Bow strengths (Axe is free!), so only Flying needs grinding. Reason & Authority are weaknesses — avoid magic/command builds.

#2 — Sniper (狙击手) — leans on her Bow strength + crit

  • Why: "Sniper helps you build off her already great crit rate" (high Dex + Luck + crit). [Reddit cache]. Hunter's Volley combat art is overpowered, and Bow is one of her strengths. Foot, low Mov, but safe ranged damage with Bowfaire + Hit +20.
  • Path: Archer → (Brigand for Death Blow) → Sniper. ⚠️ Bow C→A tutoring.

#3 — Trickster (诡术师, DLC) — thematic, leverages her Luck

  • Why: "Trickster is the obvious class she was intended for." [Reddit cache], [Game8 EN]. Sword strength + huge Luck synergizes with Lucky Seven (+5 random stat/turn); Foul Play swap + Locktouch utility. Best on the DLC where the Abyssian Master Seal is available.
  • ⚠️ Tutoring: Sword B + Faith C. Sword is a strength; Faith is a strength too — easy reqs for her.

#4 — Assassin (刺客) — speed/utility

  • Why: Sword strength + top Speed; Locktouch/Stealth + Lethality mastery. [Game8 EN]. Good chest/door utility unit. Outclassed offensively by Wyvern Lord but cheaper to reach.
  • ⚠️ Sword B + Bow C (both in her wheelhouse).

Alternatives

  • Bow Knight (弓骑士): mounted 1–3 range; cited as viable but cache rates the class's growths weak ("struggles to secure follow-ups on high difficulty" [pegasusknight.com JP]). Fine for utility, not top-tier.
  • Avoid magic classes (Reason weakness) and Authority/command roles.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into ONLY to fill the class-mastery bar and learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave — these are NOT part of the final class path above. - Brigand → Death Blow (+6 Str when this unit initiates combat) — top priority; compensates her mediocre Str for every physical build (Wyvern Lord / Sniper). - Archer → Hit +20 (+20 Hit) — accuracy for the Sniper/Bow builds (Archer is also a step toward Sniper anyway). - Pegasus Knight / Wyvern Rider → Darting Blow (+5 Spd when initiating) — pushes her already-high Spd lead. - Trickster (DLC) → Duelist's Blow (Avo +20 when initiating) — only on the DLC Trickster route; turns her into a dodge-tank. (Foul Play is the class's repositioning combat art.)

Training Focus

  • Axe to A is the single most valuable grind (unlocks Wyvern Lord and fixes Str). Sword/Bow are already strong.
  • Flying to A if going Wyvern; otherwise Bow to A for Sniper.
  • Grab Death Blow (Brigand) to compensate low Str, and Darting Blow to push her Spd lead.
  • Use her Faith strength for a few Heal levels — she has no support partners, so self-utility matters.
  • Personal +5 Luck → favors crit builds (Killer weapons, Sniper) and dodge.

Supports / Pairings

  • None possible. Anna cannot build supports. Do NOT rely on Linked-Attack/pair-up bonuses with her.
  • Compensate with self-sustaining tools: Canto (Wyvern/Bow Knight), Stealth (Assassin/Trickster), high Avo, and ranged safety (Sniper). Keep her able to act independently.

Weapon / Item / Battalion / Skill Loadout

  • Wyvern Lord build: Brave Axe / Killer Axe (crit) + a backup Sword/Bow.
  • 5 skills: Axefaire, Death Blow, Darting Blow, Hit +20, Avo +10 / Alert Stance+.
  • Battalion: high-Atk battalion; offensive gambit. (No support partner, so battalion offense matters more.)
  • Sniper build: Killer Bow + a forged Longbow; skills Bowfaire, Hunter's Volley (combat art), Hit +20, Death Blow, Crit +10. Pair with Battalion Vantage for ranged enemy-phase.
  • Trickster build (DLC): Levin Sword / Killer Sword; skills Swordfaire, Lucky Seven, Duelist's Blow (mastery), Hit +20, Death Blow. Use Foul Play to reposition allies.
  • Key items: stat boosters / Goddess Ring — good investment since she's a flexible self-reliant unit.
