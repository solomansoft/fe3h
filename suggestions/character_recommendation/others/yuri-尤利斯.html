Yuri Leclerc 尤利斯·勒克莱尔 — Build Recommendation

Ashen Wolves (Cindered Shadows DLC). Recruitable into the main story (Part 1 only) after clearing Cindered Shadows Ch.6; appears at ~Lv3, instantly one of your strongest units on Maddening. Strengths: Sword, Reason, Faith, Authority. Personal Honorable Spirit (+3 Atk when no allies adjacent). Top-tier Spd growth (65%) and Luck (45%).

Grounded in: others/ashen-wolves-dlc/yuri-尤利斯.md, classes/ (Assassin, Swordmaster, Trickster/Dancer context).

Class-Change Path (stack-ranked, to Master tier)

#1 — Trickster (诡术师, DLC) — BEST / thematic

  • Why: "Yuri is perfect as a Trickster because of its combat art (Foul Play), which is boosted by Fetters of Dromi, which adds to his movement range." [Game8 EN]. Sword + Faith strengths match the class exactly; Foul Play (swap with ally 1–5) + huge Mov from his relic = unmatched battlefield repositioning, while Duelist's Blow (Avo +20 on initiate) + his Spd make him a dodge-tank. He can also self-heal (Faith).
  • Path: Myrmidon → Thief (Sword + utility) → Trickster (Abyssian Master Seal). ⚠️ needs Sword B + Faith C — both strengths, very cheap.

#2 — Assassin (刺客) — speed/crit infiltrator

  • Why: "Besides Sniper... you can consider assassin/swordmaster." [Reddit cache]. +5 Spd/+2 Mov, Swordfaire, Locktouch/Stealth, Lethality. His Spd makes Lethality and doubling reliable; great for solo-flanking (synergy with Honorable Spirit's +3 Atk when alone).
  • ⚠️ Sword B + Bow C (Bow is his budding talent → Deadeye, so this dovetails).

#3 — Swordmaster (剑圣) — pure crit duelist

  • Why: Sword strength + 65% Spd = Astra Storm / Crit machine, Sword Crit +10. Foot, fragile defensively but his Avo carries. [Reddit cache].
  • ⚠️ Sword A.

#4 — Dancer (舞者) — best Dancer candidate

  • Why: "Arguably best dancer in the game." [Reddit cache]. High Cha (50%) + Sword for the Sword Avo dance + his own mobility; if you need a refresher, Yuri is the top pick. Won the white-heron-cup-equivalent role well. (Utility role, not a damage build.)

Alternatives

  • Bow build (Sniper): his budding talent Bow → Deadeye is excellent; viable Sniper if you tutor Bow. [Reddit: "Sniper... best class for basically everyone."]
  • Avoid Lance/Axe/Riding (his weaknesses).

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into ONLY to fill the class-mastery bar and learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave — these are NOT part of the final class path above. - Brigand → Death Blow (+6 Str when this unit initiates combat) — top priority; player-phase damage for his sword builds. - Mercenary → Vantage (attack first when foe initiates and HP < 50%) — pairs with his Spd/Avo for an enemy-phase dodge-and-counter setup. - Pegasus Knight / (Brigand area) → Darting Blow (+5 Spd when initiating) — compounds his game-high 65% Spd growth. - Assassin → Lethality (chance to instantly kill) — if you pass through Assassin, grab it (Assassin is also a valid final class for him).

Training Focus

  • Sword to A and Faith to C+ for Trickster (both strengths). Authority is a strength too → good battalion access.
  • Unlock Bow budding talent → Deadeye (lets him snipe from safety; pairs with Honorable Spirit).
  • Get Fetters of Dromi (his relic, from the DLC) ASAP — the movement/Canto-like boost defines his best builds.
  • Mastery skills: Death Blow / Darting Blow, Duelist's Blow (Trickster), Lethality (Assassin).

Supports / Pairings

  • Strong supports with the other Ashen Wolves (Balthus, Constance, Hapi) and Byleth (S-support available, well-liked). Also Bernadetta, Constance, Linhardt.
  • Synergy: his Foul Play (Trickster) repositions fragile allies (e.g. swap a mage out of danger); pair near a mounted ally to ferry. Honorable Spirit rewards being ALONE, so for max Atk keep him slightly detached — balance against support/linked-attack bonuses.

Weapon / Item / Battalion / Skill Loadout

  • Trickster build (best): Levin Sword / Killer Sword + a Faith tome for Heal/Nosferatu.
  • 5 skills: Swordfaire, Death Blow, Duelist's Blow (mastery), Hit +20, Sword Avo +20 / Alert Stance+.
  • Equip Fetters of Dromi. Battalion: high-Avo or high-Atk; Dance/utility-oriented gambit.
  • Assassin build: Killer Sword + Bow; skills Swordfaire, Death Blow, Darting Blow, Lethality, Hit +20.
  • Key item: Fetters of Dromi (relic — movement boost is core to every build).

Ranked Options & Disagreements

  2. Assassin — speedy crit/Lethality flanker. [Reddit]
  3. Swordmaster — pure crit duelist. [Reddit]
  4. Dancer — best dance candidate if you need a refresher. [Reddit] - Disagreement: Reddit floats Sniper ("best class for everyone") and Dancer; Game8 firmly favors Trickster for the relic synergy. Consensus: lean into Sword + mobility; avoid his Lance/Axe/Riding weaknesses.
