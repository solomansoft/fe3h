Hilda Valentine Goneril / 希尔妲·瓦伦丁·哥纳利尔 — Gameplay Recommendation

Strengths: Lance, Axe. Weaknesses: Faith, Heavy Armor. Budding Talent: Heavy Armor → Seal Speed (and turns Heavy Armor into a boon). Minor Crest of Goneril (chance to nullify enemy counter on combat arts). Growths: HP 50 / Str 45 / Spd 50 / Cha 50 — a strong physical attacker. Advocate gives adjacent males Atk+3.

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Noble → Fighter → Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord ⭐ best - Rationale: Axe strength + good Str/Spd + flying = the game's best physical class. Wyvern Lord gives Mov 8, Canto, Axefaire, Avo+10. Her Crest of Goneril (nullify counter) + combat arts let her one-shot safely. Master Brigand for Death Blow. [Game8 "absurd strength… dive ranks and KO"; GameWith JP; GamerSky CN; note.com JP — universal pick.] - ⚠️ Wyvern needs Flying A and Lance C — Flying isn't her listed boon but trains fine; Lance is a boon. No hard weakness blocks it.
  2. Noble → Fighter → Brigand → Warrior - Rationale: Foot axe powerhouse — Warrior has high Str modifier and bow access. [GameWith JP / GamerSky CN list Warrior as her alt.] Pick if you don't want a flier or lack Flying training; lower mobility than Wyvern Lord.
  3. Noble → Soldier → Cavalier → Paladin → Great Knight - Rationale: Lance-cavalry route using her Lance boon; Great Knight is a mounted axe/lance bruiser. Viable but lower ceiling than flying — Wyvern Lord beats it on mobility and Avo.

Alternative: Pegasus → Falcon Knight (flying lance dodge) if you'd rather a lance flier; Wyvern Lord generally preferred for her Axe. Budding Heavy Armor → Seal Speed is a nice optional debuff and converts Heavy Armor to a boon, but isn't needed for her main routes. - ⚠️ Faith is a weakness (irrelevant to physical builds). Heavy Armor starts as a weakness but the budding talent fixes it if you ever want Fortress/Great Knight armor routes.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into only until the class-mastery bar fills, learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave. Not part of her final class path. - Brigand → Death Blow (Str +6 when she initiates combat) — core damage skill, already on her #1 path; master it before moving on. Lets her one-shot on initiation with Crest of Goneril counter-nullification. - Pegasus Knight → Darting Blow (Spd +6 when she initiates combat) — to double the fastest foes on player phase; equip alongside Death Blow on her flying frame. - (Optional) Heavy Armor budding → Seal Speed is a debuff from her budding talent, not a class-mastery detour — pop it only if you want a tankier variant.

Training focus

  • Prioritize Axe → A and Flying → A (Wyvern Lord cert); keep Lance (boon) for combat arts and the Lance C gate.
  • Master Brigand for Death Blow (+6 Str player phase) — core damage skill.
  • Optional: tutor Heavy Armor to pop Seal Speed (and make armor a boon) if you want a tankier variant.
  • Her infamous low motivation: she trains slowly, so focus tutoring on Axe/Flying only.

Supports / pairings

  • Best gameplay supports: Claude (both fliers/aggressive), Marianne (close friends, strong A-support), and place her next to male allies to share Advocate (Atk+3).
  • Romance (S-support): Byleth (either); Claude is the marquee pairing.
  • Synergy: Advocate buffs adjacent males — pair her flying frame next to Claude/Raphael on shared turns. Crest of Goneril makes her combat arts counter-proof.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Axes — Killer Axe (crit), Brave Axe (player-phase doubles), Hand Axe / Short Axe (1-2 range); a Lance backup for Tempest Lance.
  • Combat arts: Smash / Wild Abandon (Axe), Helm Splitter, Diamond Axe; Lance arts (Tempest Lance/Knightkneeler) via her Lance boon.
  • Equipped skills (5): Axefaire, Death Blow, Avo+10 (Wyvern Lord) / Alert Stance+, Darting Blow (Pegasus mastery, to double fast foes), and Defiant Crit / Seal Speed (budding).
  • Battalion/gambit: a high-Authority flying or axe battalion (Goneril/Holst fits thematically); Retribution gambit for enemy-phase value.
  • Key items: Speedwing (push her doubling threshold), Energy Drop optional; she's a top stat-booster recipient as a carry flier.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Wyvern Lord — best physical class, flying + Axefaire + Avo; unanimous pick [Game8, GameWith JP, GamerSky CN, note.com JP].
  2. Warrior — foot axe alt with bow access [GameWith JP, GamerSky CN]; pick without Flying training.
  3. Great Knight / Paladin — lance-cavalry bruiser using her Lance boon; lower ceiling. - Little disagreement: every source funnels Hilda to Wyvern Lord; the only branch is Warrior (no-fly alternative). [note.com JP] rates her B tier — strong but slow to train due to low motivation, so concentrate her tutoring.
