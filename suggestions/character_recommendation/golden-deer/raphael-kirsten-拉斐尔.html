Raphael Kirsten / 拉斐尔·基尔斯滕 — Gameplay Recommendation

Strengths: Axe, Brawling, Heavy Armor. Weaknesses: Bow, Riding. No Crest, no budding talent. Growths: HP 65 / Str 50 / Def 45 — a tanky bruiser. Low Mag 15 / Spd 15 / Res 10 (will rarely double).

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Commoner → Fighter → Brigand → Grappler → War Master ⭐ best - Rationale: Axe + Brawling are both strengths and War Master (male-only) is the game's premier brawler — Str+5 modifier, Crit+20, and the Quick Riposte mastery gives the enemy-phase follow-up he otherwise lacks due to low Speed. Master Brigand first for Death Blow (+6 Str on player phase). [Game8, GameWith JP, Reddit all converge on War Master.]
  2. Commoner → Fighter → Brigand → Grappler (stay Grappler) - Rationale: Grappler's Fierce Iron Fist hits three times for big burst, plus Fistfaire. Slightly higher Speed modifier than War Master; great until you want Quick Riposte. Essentially the penultimate stop of #1.
  3. Commoner → Armored Knight → Fortress Knight (then → Warrior late) - Rationale: Pure wall build leveraging Heavy Armor strength + huge Def. [GameWith JP] notes Fortress mobility (Mov 4) is too low, so switch to Warrior late for axe damage + mobility. Defensive niche only — lower offense than the brawler line.

Alternatives: Warrior as a final axe class if you skip brawling (Bow weakness makes Warrior's bow access wasted). - ⚠️ No remedial tutoring needed for the brawler line (Brawling/Axe are boons). Avoid Bow/Riding-dependent classes (both weaknesses).

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into only until the class-mastery bar fills, learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave. Not part of his final class path. - Brigand → Death Blow (Str +6 when he initiates combat) — core damage skill, already on his #1 path; master it before Grappler. Turns his huge Str into massive player-phase burst. - (Note) Quick Riposte (his enemy-phase fix) is the War Master mastery and Fierce Iron Fist / Tomebreaker come from Grappler — these are his final/penultimate classes, not detours. Avoid the Archer detour: Bow is a weakness, so the cert is a slow grind for little gain.

Training focus

  • Prioritize Brawling → A and Axe → A (War Master cert) — both strengths, so fast.
  • Heavy Armor to D/C only if you want the Armored intermediate; not required for the brawler route.
  • Don't bother with Bow/Riding (weaknesses). His Goody Basket personal scales with Luck — modest self-heal.

Supports / pairings

  • Best gameplay supports: Ignatz (childhood friend, A-support combat bonus), Leonie, Lysithea (he tanks while she nukes).
  • Romance (S-support): Byleth (either); thematic supports with Ignatz/Maya (story).
  • Synergy: front-line wall — put him adjacent to squishy mages (Lysithea/Marianne) to body-block. Quick Riposte + high HP/Def makes him a self-sufficient enemy-phase bruiser.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Gauntlets (Killer Knuckles for crit, Aura Knuckles for range); a Killer Axe / Hand Axe as backup ranged option.
  • Combat arts: War Master's Strike (3-hit gauntlet, from War Master), Fierce Iron Fist (Grappler), Smash / Wild Abandon (Axe).
  • Equipped skills (5): Fistfaire, Death Blow, Quick Riposte, Crit+20 (class-innate in War Master) / Battalion Wrath, and Hit+20 or Defiant Str.
  • Battalion/gambit: a high-Hit/Crit infantry battalion to fix his accuracy; Battalion Wrath + Vantage combo is strong on his high-HP frame. Retribution gambit for enemy-phase.
  • Key items: Speedwing (his biggest weakness — every point helps him double); Energy Drop optional; Goddess Icon for Hit/Avo.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. War Master — best final; Quick Riposte fixes his enemy-phase, top Str modifier [Game8, GameWith JP, Reddit].
  2. Grappler — Fierce Iron Fist burst + slightly more Speed; great if no Master Seal yet.
  3. Fortress Knight → Warrior — defensive wall route [GameWith JP, Reddit "Warrior or Fortress" thread]; lower offense. - Disagreement: offense (War Master/Grappler) vs defense (Fortress). Consensus = go offensive brawler; his Str/HP are wasted as a passive wall, and Death Blow + War Master's Strike makes him a real damage dealer. [GamerSky CN] lists him as a Wyvern/Warrior near-melee option — ignore the bow weakness.
