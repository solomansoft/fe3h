Claude von Riegan / 克劳德·冯·里刚 — Gameplay Recommendation

Golden Deer house leader (locked unit, Verdant Wind only). Strengths: Sword, Bow, Authority, Flying. Weaknesses: Lance, Faith. Budding Talent: Axe → Diamond Axe. Minor Crest of Riegan (combat-art HP drain). Growths skew Dex 60 / Spd 55 / Cha 55 / Str 40.

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Noble → Archer → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord ⭐ best - Rationale: His Bow + Flying strengths and good Str/Spd make a flying bow-platform the strongest endgame; Wyvern Lord brings Mov 8, Canto, Avo+10, Axefaire. Pairs perfectly with his Axe budding talent (Diamond Axe) so the Axe A certification requirement is on-strength once budded. - ⚠️ Needs the Axe budding talent unlocked (instruct/tutor Axe) to reach Axe A comfortably and to fix Axe as a boon; Lance C requirement is a weakness — give a little Lance tutoring or use his Authority/level to grind it.
  2. Noble → Archer → Sniper (then optionally → Bow Knight) - Rationale: Pure bow ace. Sniper gives Bowrange+1 and the broken Hunter's Volley combat art (two hits, +Crit). Foot Mov 5 is the only downside; Bow Knight upgrades to Mov 8 + Bowrange+2 but has poor growths. [GamerSky CN] calls him "the best archer in the game."
  3. Noble → Archer → Wyvern Rider → Barbarossa (Part 2, his unique class) - Rationale: Barbarossa is a flying bow class tailor-made for him [Game8]. Slightly different stat spread than Wyvern Lord; choose for flavor/bow focus. Effectively ties #1 — Wyvern Lord edges it for raw combat, Barbarossa for keeping bow range.

Alternatives: Bow Knight (mounted bow, Mov 8) if you prefer ranged kiting over flying.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into only until the class-mastery bar fills, learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave. Not part of his final class path. - Brigand → Death Blow (Str +6 when he initiates combat) — top priority; flat player-phase damage on every bow/axe initiation. His Axe budding makes the Brigand cert on-strength. - Archer → Hit +20 (Hit +20) — already a stop on every route, so master it for the accuracy passive; keeps his bow attacks landing on Maddening. - Pegasus Knight → Darting Blow (Spd +6 when he initiates combat) — helps him double on player phase before he settles into Wyvern Lord.

Training focus

  • Prioritize Bow → A and Flying → A (both strengths). Authority is also a boon — keep it climbing for stronger battalions/gambits and Authority abilities.
  • Tutor Axe to pop the budding talent early (unlocks Diamond Axe and enables the Wyvern Lord Axe A cert without fighting a weakness).
  • Dab in Lance to D/C only to clear Wyvern Rider/Lord lance gate.
  • His +20% EXP personal (Leicester Lineage) means he levels fast — exploit it by deploying him constantly early.

Supports / pairings

  • Best gameplay support: Hilda (both flying/aggressive, strong A-support combat boosts) and Lysithea.
  • Romance (S-support): widely praised story-wise [Reddit]; Byleth (either gender) and Hilda are the marquee pairings. No build downside.
  • Synergy: keep him near Authority-buffed allies; his Crest HP-drain rewards combat-art use, so a flier diving with bow arts self-sustains.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Brave Bow / Killer Bow (crit) for enemy-phase and player-phase; Magic Bow situationally; as a flier carry a Steel/Killer Axe for Diamond Axe.
  • Combat arts: Hunter's Volley (if Sniper), Curved Shot / Encloser (Bow), Diamond Axe (Axe budding).
  • Equipped skills (5): Bowfaire, Death Blow (from Brigand mastery), Hit+20 (Archer mastery), Avo+10 (Wyvern Lord), Defiant Crit or Alert Stance+ (Flying mastery).
  • Battalion/gambit: a high-Authority flying or ranged battalion (e.g. Holst's/Goneril fliers, or Leicester Knights); Retribution gambit is excellent for enemy-phase.
  • Key items: funnel a few Spirit Dust / Energy Drops are optional; he is a strong stat-booster recipient only if you want him to solo.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Wyvern Lord (flying bow/axe) — most combat ceiling; near-universal JP pick [GameWith JP: "Archer → Wyvern Lord → Barbarossa"].
  2. Barbarossa — his unique flying-bow class; Game8's headline pick [Game8].
  3. Sniper — best if you want pure bow + Hunter's Volley on foot [GamerSky CN, Reddit].
  4. Bow Knight — mobile ranged but weak growths; situational. - Sources broadly agree Claude is a top-tier flying bow unit (SS tier [note.com JP]); the only real debate is Wyvern Lord vs Barbarossa vs Sniper, all viable.
