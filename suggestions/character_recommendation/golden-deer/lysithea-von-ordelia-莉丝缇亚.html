Lysithea von Ordelia / 莉丝缇亚·冯·科迪利亚 — Gameplay Recommendation

Strengths: Reason, Faith, Authority. Weaknesses: Sword, Lance, Axe, Heavy Armor. Budding Talent: Sword → Soulblade. Two Crests (Major Gloucester + Minor Charon, both add Mt). Growths: Mag 60 / Dex 60 / Spd 50 — glass-cannon nuker. Low HP 20 / Def 10. Mastermind doubles skill EXP (trains fast).

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Noble → Mage → Warlock → Gremory ⭐ best - Rationale: The consensus best mage in the game. Gremory (female-only) gives Mag+5 modifier and doubles all spell uses (her biggest limiter is spell quantity — she has Warp/Dark Spikes T/Luna/Meteor). Major Crest of Gloucester procs bonus Mt on her magic. Highest raw magic ceiling. [GamerSky CN: "strongest mage"; GameWith JP; Reddit consensus "Gremory best overall."]
  2. Noble → Mage → Dark Mage → Dark Knight - Rationale: Adds Mov 8 + Canto and the only Dark Tomefaire (she has dark magic via Crest access in some builds) — fixes her low mobility/fragility by letting her hit-and-run. Reddit's "personal fav" alternative. Slightly less burst than Gremory but far better positioning/survivability.
  3. Noble → Mage → Warlock → Valkyrie (Note: confirm Valkyrie availability — it is a magic cavalry option some sources cite) - Rationale: Magic cavalry mobility if you want Canto without dark magic. Reddit lists it as a good option; treat as below Dark Knight for her since it lacks a tomefaire.

Alternative: Dancer [Game8] — if you win the dance-off, +2 Mov and refresh allies; her nuke value is too high to bench, so usually keep her casting. Dark Flier/Dark Pegasus is weaker — it comes with Black Tomefaire only and is redundant [Reddit]. - ⚠️ Her weapon weaknesses don't matter (she's a pure caster). Sword budding (Soulblade) is optional flavor — not needed for any magic route, so no remedial tutoring required.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into only until the class-mastery bar fills, learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave. Not part of her final class path. - Mage → Fiendish Blow (Mag +6 when she initiates combat) — the single most important mage detour; already a stop on every route, so master it. (Death Blow does NOT apply — she's a caster; Fiendish Blow is the magic equivalent.) Her Mastermind personal doubles skill EXP, so she fills the mastery bar fastest in the roster. - (Note) She has no physical detours worth taking — her Sword/Lance/Axe weaknesses make Brigand/Archer/Pegasus certs slow and pointless for a pure nuker.

Training focus

  • Prioritize Reason → A then Faith → A (Gremory cert needs both A). Her Mastermind personal doubles skill EXP, so she certifies fastest in the roster — exploit it.
  • Authority (boon) for battalions/gambits.
  • Optional: tutor Sword to unlock Soulblade (a magic-scaling sword art for melee self-defense), but it's a luxury.

Supports / pairings

  • Best gameplay supports: Claude, Hilda, a front-line body-guard (Raphael/Hilda) to protect her low HP/Def.
  • Romance (S-support): Byleth (either); Lysithea S-supports are well-liked story-wise.
  • Synergy: keep her behind a tank; her Crest-boosted Dark Spikes T / Luna obliterate armored & cavalry. Pair with a Dancer (or be the Dancer) to fire twice.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Reason — Dark Spikes T (anti-cavalry), Luna (Res-ignoring), Banshee Θ; Faith — Warp (massive utility), Physic. Levin Sword / Soulblade if melee'd.
  • Combat arts: Soulblade (if Sword budded) for emergency melee.
  • Equipped skills (5): Black Tomefaire (Warlock) or Fiendish Blow (Brigand-adjacent? — actually Death Blow N/A; use Fiendish Blow from Mage/Warlock training for +6 Mag on player phase), Black Magic Uses x2 (innate Gremory), Warp utility, Movement+1, and Defiant Mag / Bowbreaker (Warlock mastery).
  • Practical set: Fiendish Blow, Black Tomefaire, Movement+1, Warp Range/utility passive, Defiant Mag.
  • Battalion/gambit: a magic-attack-boosting battalion (e.g. ranged/mage battalion); Stride to extend her reach; gambits also let her act through her fragility safely.
  • Key items: Spirit Dust (Mag — top priority recipient), Seraph Robe (raise her dangerously low HP), Speedwing if needed.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Gremory — highest magic ceiling, doubled spell uses, best overall [GamerSky CN, GameWith JP, Reddit].
  2. Dark Knight — mobility + Dark Tomefaire fixes her fragility; many players' favorite [Reddit].
  3. Valkyrie — magic cavalry alternative if you skip dark magic.
  4. Dancer — utility pivot [Game8], but only if you'd rather refresh than nuke. - Disagreement: Gremory (max damage) vs Dark Knight (max safety/mobility). Reddit notes the Mag-growth gap between them is "1-2 points" — so pick Dark Knight if her squishiness gets her killed, Gremory if you can protect her. Dark Flier is the agreed worst caster option for her (redundant tomefaire). [note.com JP] ranks her SS tier overall.
