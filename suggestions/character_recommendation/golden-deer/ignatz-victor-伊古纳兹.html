Ignatz Victor / 伊古纳兹·维克托 — Gameplay Recommendation

Strengths: Sword, Bow, Reason, Authority. Weakness: Riding. Budding Talent: Reason → Seal Strength. No Crest. Growths: Dex 50 / Spd 50 / Lck 55 / Mag 30 / Str 35 — accurate, evasive, leans support/debuff.

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Commoner → Fighter → Archer → Sniper (stay Sniper) ⭐ best - Rationale: His top-tier Dex/Luck make him a precise bow user; Sniper gives Bowrange+1 and the elite Hunter's Volley combat art (two hits, +Crit). Master Archer first for Hit+20, stacking with his Watchful Eye personal (Hit+20) for near-100% accuracy. With Break Shot (-5 Def) + budding Seal Strength (-6 Str) he becomes one of the best debuffers in the game. [GameFAQs/Fire Emblem Wiki, GamerSky CN]
  2. Commoner → Fighter → Archer → Sniper → Bow Knight - Rationale: Same kit but Mov 8 + Bowrange+2 for mobile kiting/debuffing. Trade-off: Bow Knight has poor growths and needs Lance C + Riding A (Riding is his weakness ⚠️ — requires Riding tutoring to certify). Pick if you want mobility over Sniper's range.
  3. Commoner → Monk → Mage → Warlock → (Gremory N/A male) Dark Knight (magic build) - Rationale: His Reason budding talent unlocks a real spell list (high-crit Blizzard/Fimbulvetr boosted by his Hit). A viable hybrid mage that the other physical units can't replicate. Lower ceiling than his bow build but a strong off-meta option. Note: male, so no Gremory — Warlock or Dark Knight cap it.

Alternative: Assassin (Sword+Bow, Mov 6, evasion) — Game8's pick; uses both his weapon boons and his high Spd/Lck for a dodgy infiltrator. Solid #2.5. - ⚠️ Bow Knight / any mounted route needs Riding tutoring (his only weakness). Sniper route needs none.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into only until the class-mastery bar fills, learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave. Not part of his final class path. - Archer → Hit +20 (Hit +20) — already on his path; master it. Stacks with his Watchful Eye personal (Hit +20) for near-100% accuracy. - Brigand → Death Blow (Str +6 when he initiates combat) — flat player-phase damage to compensate for his modest Str on the bow build. - (Optional) Mage → Fiendish Blow (Mag +6 when he initiates combat) — only if you pursue his off-meta Reason/magic build; skip on the pure-bow Sniper route. - (Note) Seal Strength comes from his Reason budding talent, not a class-mastery detour.

Training focus

  • Prioritize Bow → A (Sniper/Bow Knight) and keep Sword as boon for melee combat arts (Assassin option).
  • Tutor Reason to pop the budding talent → Seal Strength (a must-have debuff even on a bow build, equipped passively).
  • Authority (boon) for battalions/gambits to amplify his support role.
  • Avoid Riding unless committing to Bow Knight.

Supports / pairings

  • Best gameplay supports: Raphael (childhood friend, A-support combat boost), Leonie, Marianne (he sets up kills she/others finish).
  • Romance (S-support): Byleth (either).
  • Synergy: a pure setup unit — Seal Strength/Break Shot soften a target, then high-Str allies (Raphael, Hilda, Leonie) finish. Keep him behind the front.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Killer Bow (crit, synergizes with his Hit), Brave Bow (player-phase doubles), Magic Bow situational; a Sword/Levin Sword backup.
  • Combat arts: Hunter's Volley (Sniper), Break Shot (-5 Def debuff), Curved Shot/Encloser (Bow), Seal-skill use.
  • Equipped skills (5): Bowfaire, Hit+20 (Archer mastery — stacks with personal), Seal Strength (budding), Death Blow (Brigand mastery) or Hunter's Volley access, and Bowrange+1 / Alert Stance.
  • Battalion/gambit: an Authority-boosted ranged/archer battalion; gambits like Onslaught/Stride for utility; use gambits to apply rattled status alongside his debuffs.
  • Key items: Energy Drop / Spirit Dust (his offense is modest — boost whichever build you pick); Goddess Icon for evasion.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Sniper (debuff bow) — best overall; Hunter's Volley + Hit+20 + Seal Strength/Break Shot = premier debuffer [Fire Emblem Wiki, GamerSky].
  2. Bow Knight — mobile version; needs Riding tutoring, weaker growths.
  3. Assassin — Game8's pick; sword+bow evasion infiltrator, good but less impactful than the debuff bow.
  4. Magic (Warlock/Dark Knight via Reason budding) — unique hybrid, fun off-meta, lower ceiling. - Disagreement: Game8 favors Assassin; the Wiki/Maddening community favors Sniper debuffer. [note.com JP] rates Ignatz surprisingly high (A tier) for his accuracy/utility despite no Crest. The debuff-Sniper interpretation is the strongest competitive read.
