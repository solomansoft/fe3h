Marianne von Edmund / 玛莉安奴·冯·艾德蒙 — Gameplay Recommendation

Strengths: Sword, Faith, Riding, Flying. Weaknesses: Brawling, Heavy Armor. Budding Talent: Lance → Frozen Lance. Minor Crest of the Beast (chance +Mt on weapon attacks). Growths: Mag 50 / Res 45 / Dex 40 / Spd 40. Low Str 20 / Def 15. Has the Hero's Relic sword Blutgang. Animal Friend personal heals 20% near cavalry/flier.

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Noble → Monk → Priest → Bishop → Holy Knight ⭐ best - Rationale: Her best stats are Mag + Res + Faith, and Riding is a strength. Holy Knight is magic cavalry (Mov 8 + Canto) that lets her heal/Physic and nuke then retreat. Animal Friend self-heal is moot once she's the mounted unit, but the mobility + Faith offense (Aura, Nosferatu) makes her a premier mobile healer-attacker. [Game8, GameFAQs/Neoseeker: "most obvious Dark Knight/Holy Knight candidate."]
  2. Noble → Monk → Mage → Warlock → Gremory - Rationale: Pure caster — Gremory doubles all spell uses (huge for a healer who also nukes) and Mag+5. Best if you want her as a back-line battery rather than mobile. Loses Canto/mobility vs Holy Knight.
  3. Noble → Mage → Dark Mage → Dark Knight - Rationale: Magic cavalry like #1 but with Reason/dark magic + more Mag/Def modifier (Holy Knight gives more HP/Res). [Neoseeker: differences "trivial — let her Faith decide."] Since Faith is her boon and Reason is not, Holy Knight edges it; pick Dark Knight only if you've trained her Reason.

Alternative: Falcon Knight (flying, her Flying boon + high Res/Spd dodge) — a strong flying-dodge healer if you'd rather fly than ride. Solid #2.5. Master Pegasus Knight for Darting Blow regardless. - ⚠️ No remedial tutoring needed for Faith/Riding routes (both boons). For a physical Lance build you'd lean on the Lance budding (Frozen Lance scales off Magic, sidestepping her weak Str).

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into only until the class-mastery bar fills, learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave. Not part of her final class path. - Mage → Fiendish Blow (Mag +6 when she initiates combat) — top priority for her offensive Faith/Reason casting (Aura, Nosferatu, Frozen Lance); boosts every magic initiation. - Pegasus Knight → Darting Blow (Spd +6 when she initiates combat) — master it regardless of build; enables doubling and is essential if you take the Falcon Knight dodge-healer route. - (Note) Frozen Lance comes from her Lance budding talent, not a class-mastery detour.

Training focus

  • Prioritize Faith → A (Bishop/Holy Knight, healing + offensive Faith) and Riding → A (Holy/Dark Knight cert).
  • Tutor Lance to unlock Frozen Lance (a magic-scaling lance art — turns her high Mag into melee damage despite low Str). Excellent on any mounted build.
  • Keep Reason only if going Dark Knight.

Supports / pairings

  • Best gameplay supports: Hilda (close friends, strong A-support), Claude, any cavalry/flier ally to trigger her Animal Friend self-heal when she's on foot.
  • Romance (S-support): Byleth (either) is the famous "saves Marianne" pairing; thematically beloved.
  • Synergy: keep her adjacent to mounted/flying allies if on foot for the 20% turn heal; as Holy Knight she becomes the mobile pocket-healer who escorts the front line.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Faith — Physic (ranged heal), Recover, Aura/Nosferatu (offensive Faith); Blutgang (her Hero's Relic sword, strong on her), Levin Sword (magic-scaling), or Frozen Lance via lance.
  • Combat arts: Frozen Lance (Lance budding, magic-scaling), Soulblade-style is N/A (that's a sword art — she can use sword arts with her Sword boon).
  • Equipped skills (5): Faith abilities, Fiendish Blow (if casting) or Lancefaire, Movement+1, Defiant Mag, and Avo+10 / Renewal. Healer-focused set: White Magic Avo+, Fiendish Blow, Movement+1, Canto (innate), Defiant Mag.
  • Battalion/gambit: a healing/Res or cavalry battalion; Blessing gambit (party-wide regen) is ideal on a healer; Stride for mobility.
  • Key items: Spirit Dust (Mag) and Seraph Robe (low HP); Goddess Icon for survivability.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Holy Knight — magic cavalry healer-nuker; best fit for her Faith + Riding [Game8, Neoseeker].
  2. Gremory — doubled spell uses, pure-caster ceiling [GamerSky CN: "Bishop → Gremory, healer + damage"].
  3. Dark Knight — near-identical to Holy Knight; choose by Reason vs Faith investment [Neoseeker].
  4. Falcon Knight / Bishop — flying dodge-healer or static healer; viable. - Disagreement: Holy Knight vs Dark Knight vs Gremory. Mobile caster crowd (Game8, EN wikis) favors Holy Knight; CN (GamerSky) favors Gremory healer. Both are correct — Holy Knight for an active escort, Gremory for a back-line battery. [note.com JP] rates her C tier (low offense/durability), so invest stat boosters if she's a main. Frozen Lance is the key trick that makes her low Str irrelevant.
