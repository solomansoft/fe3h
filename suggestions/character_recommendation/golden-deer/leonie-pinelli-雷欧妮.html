Leonie Pinelli / 雷欧妮·皮聂利 — Gameplay Recommendation

Strengths: Lance, Bow, Riding. No weaknesses, no budding talent, no Crest. Growths: Spd 60 / Dex 55 / Str 40 / Def 40 — fast, accurate, durable. Rivalry: +2 dmg / -2 taken next to male allies. One of the best non-lord combat units [Reddit].

Class-change path (stack-ranked)

  1. Commoner → Soldier → Cavalier → Paladin → Bow Knight ⭐ best - Rationale: She has natural affinity in all three Bow Knight core stats (Bow + Lance + Riding) — the strongest Bow Knight candidate among cavalry defaults [Fire Emblem Wiki]. Mov 8, Canto, Bowfaire, Bowrange+2 makes her a mobile 1-3 range powerhouse that also wields lances. Master Brigand (Death Blow) and Archer (Hit+20) en route. Caveat: Bow Knight's own growths are mediocre, but her base growths carry it.
  2. Commoner → Soldier → Pegasus Knight → Falcon Knight - Rationale: Her huge Speed makes a flying dodge-tank; Falcon Knight (female-only) gives Spd/Res, Canto, and flight. Master Pegasus for Darting Blow — essential to double the fastest Maddening enemies. [Fire Emblem Wiki, GamerSky CN T2 flier.] Best if you want a flier over a bow cavalry.
  3. Commoner → Fighter → Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord - Rationale: Off-Lance/Axe flying bruiser; her Str/Spd support it and Wyvern Lord is the best physical class. She lacks an Axe boon so Axe A is a grind, but her stats make it work. Strong #3 if you want raw flying damage. [Korean community: "extremely well-suited as a physical dealer" — namu.wiki.]

Alternative: Sniper (foot bow + Hunter's Volley) as a stepping stone or final if you want pure bow [Fire Emblem Wiki: "Sniper until ready for Bow Knight"]. - ⚠️ No weaknesses, so she certifies into nearly anything without remedial tutoring — the most flexible GD unit [namu.wiki: "fill whatever role the team lacks"].

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into only until the class-mastery bar fills, learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave. Not part of her final class path. - Brigand → Death Blow (Str +6 when she initiates combat) — core player-phase damage on every bow/lance build; her zero weaknesses make the Axe cert painless. - Archer → Hit +20 (Hit +20) — accuracy passive, already on the Bow Knight road; master it on the way through. - Pegasus Knight → Darting Blow (Spd +6 when she initiates combat) — the key Maddening enabler to double the fastest enemies; essential for the Falcon Knight build and useful everywhere.

Training focus

  • Prioritize Bow → A and Riding → A (Bow Knight); keep Lance → A (boon) for lance combat arts + Bow Knight's lance access.
  • Master Brigand (Death Blow), Archer (Hit+20), and Pegasus Knight (Darting Blow) — Darting Blow is the key Maddening enabler for doubling.
  • Position her next to male allies to trigger Rivalry (+2/-2).

Supports / pairings

  • Best gameplay supports: Claude, Raphael, Ignatz (male allies trigger Rivalry; she escorts the front). Strong supports with Byleth (Jeralt connection).
  • Romance (S-support): Byleth (either) — thematically central (Jeralt's apprentice).
  • Synergy: keep a male ally adjacent for Rivalry; as Bow Knight she Cantos in, fires 1-3 range, retreats — pairs well with front-line bait.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Bow — Killer Bow (crit, her high Dex/Spd loves it), Brave Bow, Magic Bow; Lance — Killer Lance / Silver Lance for melee and Tempest Lance.
  • Combat arts: Hunter's Volley (if Sniper-mastered), Curved Shot / Encloser (Bow), Tempest Lance / Knightkneeler (Lance).
  • Equipped skills (5): Bowfaire, Death Blow, Darting Blow, Hit+20, and Bowrange+1 / Defiant Crit. (Swap Darting Blow for Quick Riposte if going enemy-phase.)
  • Battalion/gambit: a high-Authority cavalry or ranged battalion; Retribution / Onslaught gambits for value; Stride to extend reach.
  • Key items: she's so well-rounded any booster works — Speedwing/Energy Drop to deepen her carry potential; Goddess Icon for evasion on the Falcon build.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Bow Knight — best Bow Knight candidate in the game; mobile 1-3 range lance+bow cavalry [Fire Emblem Wiki, Game8, fe3h.com].
  2. Falcon Knight — Speed dodge-tank flier; Darting Blow doubler [Fire Emblem Wiki, GamerSky CN].
  3. Wyvern Lord — off-class flying bruiser; needs Axe grind but big payoff [namu.wiki, general Maddening meta].
  4. Sniper — pure bow stepping-stone / final with Hunter's Volley. - Disagreement: bow-cavalry (Bow Knight) vs flier (Falcon/Wyvern). Bow Knight is the textbook fit for her stat affinities; many Maddening players still prefer a flier for Canto+evasion. Either is top-tier — she's rated A tier and "one of the 2 best non-lord combat units" [Reddit, note.com JP]. Her zero weaknesses make her the GD utility carry: build to your team's gap.
