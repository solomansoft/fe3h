Recommendation — Dorothea Arnault / 多洛缇雅·阿尔诺特

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Black Eagles 黑鹫学级 Gameplay build recommendations (English). Grounded in objective data: Mag 40% / Cha 40% / Dex 45% / Spd 40% growths, no Crest, strengths Sword·Reason, weaknesses Faith·Riding·Flying, budding talent Faith (unlocks White Magic Avo +20), personal Songstress (adjacent allies heal 10% each turn). Recruitable (Charm 25 + Authority B).

Class-change path

  1. #1 Noble/Commoner → Monk → Mage → Warlock → Gremory — best offensive caster. Gremory doubles all spell uses (her Reason offense AND her Physic healing) and boosts Mag. She learns Meteor (long-range nuke) and strong Reason spells; her Songstress passive + budding-talent White Magic Avo +20 make her a hybrid attacker/support. ⚠️ Gremory needs Faith A — Faith is both her weakness and her budding talent, so tutor it; the payoff (Physic + Avo +20 + double uses) is worth it. (Gremory is female-only — she qualifies.) [Game8] [GameWith JP] [GamerSky CN]
  2. #2 Dancer (win the White Heron Cup) — arguably her best team role. As Dancer she grants an extra ally turn, keeps her Songstress heal aura, can throw Meteor/gambits, and her weak offense stops mattering since Dancers don't need to fight. GamerSky CN calls her "the strongest support in Black Eagles." [GamerSky CN] [Game8]
  3. #3 Noble → Mage → Warlock (terminal) — pure damage if you skip the Faith A grind for Gremory; loses double uses and Avo +20. [GameFAQs]
  4. Alternative: Dark Flier (Dark Pegasus) for mobility/Canto — ⚠️ blocked by her Flying AND Riding weaknesses; takes far too long to reach. Not recommended. [Game8]

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into ONLY to fill the class-mastery bar and learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave — these are NOT part of her final class path (Mage → Warlock → Gremory, or Dancer). 1. Mage → Fiendish Blow (Mag +6 when she initiates — the universal offensive-caster detour; she passes through Mage on the way to Warlock/Gremory anyway). 2. Monk → Magic +2 (flat +2 Mag — cheap early pickup as she certifies through Monk; stacks with everything). 3. Priest → White Magic Heal +5 (+5 to her Faith healing — fits her Physic/Songstress support role; grab it if she dips into Priest for the white-magic track). Note: Warlock (Bowbreaker) and Gremory are terminal classes here, so their masteries are earned by staying rather than detouring.

Training focus

  • Core: Reason to A (her damage), Faith to A (Physic, Warp, and White Magic Avo +20 budding talent — high value), Authority (recruitment req + better battalions; her Charm makes gambits strong).
  • Budding talent: Faith → White Magic Avo +20 — turns her into a surprisingly evasive healer; raise Faith despite the weakness.
  • For Dancer: just secure the White Heron Cup, then keep building Reason/Faith.
  • Avoid Riding/Flying investment (both weaknesses).

Supports / pairings

  • Ferdinand — popular and effective: her Songstress heals him to keep his Confidence (Hit/Avo +15 at full HP) active. Strong combat synergy + good story.
  • Petra — excellent paired ending and adjacency support.
  • Byleth (S-support) — one of the most-praised romance routes; also good adjacency bonuses. [Reddit: recurring f/f favorite]
  • Synergy: park her next to your dodge-tank/frontliner so the 10% Songstress heal ticks every turn; Dancer build amplifies your ace's turns.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Spells: Reason — Thoron, Meteor (signature long-range), Bolganone; Faith — Physic, Warp, Nosferatu (self-sustain).
  • Combat arts: Sword arts (she has Sword strength) as emergency melee; mostly a caster.
  • Equipped skills (5): Black Tomefaire / Black Magic Range +1 (Warlock) · Fiendish Blow · White Magic Avo +20 (her budding ability) · Songstress (personal) · Authority/Battalion skill. (Dancer build: Sword Avo +20 from Dancer.)
  • Battalion/gambit: a high-Charm battalion (she's a great gambit user); Meteor-linked attack gambits; Stride if Dancer. Her Charm 40% + Authority makes gambits hit hard.
  • Key items: Spirit Dust (Magic), Goddess Ring; an Avo/Charm accessory for the dodge-healer build.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Gremory — #1 offensive build; double uses + Physic + Avo +20. [Game8, GameWith, GamerSky]
  2. Dancer — #1 utility build; extra turns + Songstress; mitigates her weak offense. [GamerSky, Game8]
  3. Warlock (terminal) — #2 if skipping Faith A. [GameFAQs]
  4. Dark Flier — last; blocked by double weakness. [Game8] - Disagreement: Western sources (Game8) lead with Gremory; GamerSky CN leads with Dancer ("strongest support"). Verdict: Gremory if you want a damage mage, Dancer if your team already has magic damage — both are top picks; pick by team needs.
