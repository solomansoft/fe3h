Recommendation — Caspar von Bergliez / 卡斯帕尔·冯·贝尔谷里斯

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Black Eagles 黑鹫学级 Gameplay build recommendations (English). Grounded in objective data: HP 55% / Str 45% / Spd 45% / Lck 40% growths, no Crest, strengths Axe·Brawl, weaknesses Bow·Reason·Authority, no budding talent, personal Born Fighter (adjacent enemies −10 Avoid). Recruitable (Strength 10 + Brawling C).

Class-change path

  1. #1 Noble → Brawler → Grappler → War Master — the definitive build. War Master requires Axe A + Brawl A (both strengths) and grants Crit +20 and Quick Riposte (guaranteed counter). Master Brigand first for Death Blow and Brawler for Unarmed Combat. His Born Fighter lowers adjacent enemy Avoid, helping his attacks land. [GameWith JP] [Game8] [Pegasusknight JP] [GameFAQs]
  2. #2 Noble → Brigand → Grappler (terminal) — if you skip the long Axe+Brawl A grind, Grappler still gives dual unarmed attacks and good Str; a strong Advanced endpoint. [GameFAQs]
  3. #3 Noble → Fighter → Brigand → Warrior — pure axe route. Warrior has Move 5 and high Str; fine but loses War Master's Crit +20 and gauntlet flexibility. [GamerSky CN context]
  4. Alternative niche: Wyvern Rider/Lord for mobility (Axe strength helps), patching his weak Move — but Flying isn't a strength and he loses brawling synergy; lower priority.

⚠️ War Master is male-only — Caspar qualifies. His low Movement is the main weakness; War Master and the Death Blow/Quick Riposte combo make him a brutal player-and-enemy-phase bruiser despite it.

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into ONLY to fill the class-mastery bar and learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave — these are NOT part of his final class path (Brawler → Grappler → War Master). 1. Brigand → Death Blow (Str +6 when he initiates — the single most important detour for him; surfaced from his path's "Master Brigand first for Death Blow"). 2. Brawler → Unarmed Combat (retains the gauntlet-double class skill as an equippable — already noted as "Brawler for Unarmed Combat"; he masters it on the way to Grappler). 3. Fighter → Strength +2 (flat +2 Str — a cheap early detour if he passes through Fighter on the pure-axe alt route). Note: Grappler and War Master are his terminal classes (their masteries — Tomebreaker/Fierce Iron Fist and Quick Riposte/War Master's Strike — are earned by staying, not detouring).

Training focus

  • Core: Brawl to A and Axe to A (both strengths; War Master needs both).
  • Grab Death Blow (Brigand) for +6 Str on initiation — huge for him.
  • Ignore Bow/Reason/Authority (all weaknesses).
  • Manage his low Move: use Stride gambits, pair-up positioning, or a flier escort. His Born Fighter wants him adjacent to enemies anyway.
  • Consider Speed boosters to help him double, since 45% Spd can fall behind on Maddening.

Supports / pairings

  • Linhardt — his best story support (paired ending); Linhardt heals/Warps him into range, fixing his Move issue. Excellent synergy.
  • Mercedes paralogue note: [Reddit] community says recruiting Caspar just for Mercedes' paralogue isn't worth it on Maddening — recruit him for his own combat value instead.
  • Petra / Ferdinand — good combat partners who cover ground he can't.
  • Synergy: Quick Riposte + Born Fighter makes him a strong enemy-phase wall when enemies cluster around him.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Gauntlets — Killer Knuckles / Aura Knuckles (brawling = built-in brave effect, hits 2x; pairs with Death Blow and Crit +20). Axe backup: Killer Axe / Hand Axe for range.
  • Combat arts: Fading Blow / Rushing Blow (gauntlet), War Master's Fierce Iron Fist; Smash (axe).
  • Equipped skills (5): Fistfaire (War Master) · Death Blow · Quick Riposte · Crit-related skill (his Crit +20 stacks) · Defiant Strength or Hit +20.
  • Battalion/gambit: a Str/Crit battalion; Stride is excellent to fix his Move; Retribution for enemy-phase walling.
  • Key items: Speed Ring + Energy Drop (Strength); a Boots if available to fix Move.
