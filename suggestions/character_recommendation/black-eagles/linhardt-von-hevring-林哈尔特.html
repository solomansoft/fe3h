Recommendation — Linhardt von Hevring / 林哈尔特·冯·赫维林

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Black Eagles 黑鹫学级 Gameplay build recommendations (English). Grounded in objective data: Mag 45% / Res 45% growths, strengths Reason·Faith, no listed weakness, no budding talent, Minor Crest of Cethleann (occasionally +5 healing Might), personal Catnap (heal 10% on Wait). Recruitable (Magic 10 + Reason C).

Class-change path

  1. #1 Noble → Monk → Priest → Bishop — consensus best. Bishop maximizes white-magic uses, has the Heal +10 class ability and Faith range, and suits his rear-line healer identity (Catnap rewards waiting). His Crest boosts healing output. [Game8] [GameWith JP] [GameFAQs] [GamerSky CN]
  2. #2 Noble → Monk → Priest → Bishop → Gremory — best if he hits the endgame: Gremory doubles all spell uses (heals AND offense) and adds Mag. ⚠️ Gremory needs Reason A + Faith A — both are strengths, so achievable; just time-consuming. The catch: Gremory is female-only — wait, Linhardt is male, so Gremory is NOT available to him. Correct terminal is Bishop. (See note below.)
  3. #2 (actual) Noble → Monk → Mage → Dark Bishop / Warlock — offensive caster route using his Reason. GamerSky CN praises pairing Linhardt with a dark-magic dip ("take Dark Mage mastery to patch his damage") so he can deal damage between heals. Viable but he has no Master class here either. [GamerSky CN]
  4. Alternative terminal: he simply stays Bishop — sources note "Linhardt has no good Master class," so Bishop is effectively his endpoint. [Game8]

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into ONLY to fill the class-mastery bar and learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave — these are NOT part of his final class path (Monk → Priest → Bishop). 1. Priest → White Magic Heal +5 (+5 to his Faith healing output — core for his rear-line healer identity; surfaced from his "White Magic Heal +5 (Priest mastery)" loadout note. Priest is on his path, mastered before Bishop). 2. Monk → Magic +2 (flat +2 Mag — surfaced from his "Magic +2 (Monk mastery)" note; cheap grab while certifying through Monk). 3. Mage → Fiendish Blow (Mag +6 when he initiates — for the offensive-caster variant that deals damage between heals; pair with the Dark Mage dip below). 4. Dark Mage → Poison Strike (fixed post-combat chip — GamerSky CN's "take Dark Mage mastery to patch his damage" surfaced here; only for the Reason-offense build).

Training focus

  • Core: Faith to A (healing + Bishop; learn Physic at C for ranged healing, plus Warp/Fortify late — Linhardt is a premier Warp user). Reason to support offensive utility (Bolganone, Sagittae).
  • No budding talent to chase.
  • Catnap rewards a turn of Wait — position him safely so he self-heals; his Crest occasionally boosts heal Might.
  • He is one of the game's best Warp holders (high Faith + healer) — prioritize learning Warp.

Supports / pairings

  • Caspar — his strongest story support (paired ending) and a fine adjacency partner (Caspar frontlines, Linhardt heals).
  • Byleth — usable support; he benefits from being near the lord he heals.
  • Bernadetta / Edelgard — keep him adjacent to frontliners he heals; Catnap synergy when he's parked.
  • Combat synergy: Warp + Physic to enable aggressive allies' turns.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Spells: Faith — Physic, Recover, Warp, Fortify; Reason — Bolganone/Sagittae for chip offense.
  • Combat arts: none essential (caster).
  • Equipped skills (5): White Magic Heal +5 (Priest mastery) · White Magic Avo +20 / White Tomefaire (Bishop) · Fiendish Blow (if dipping offense) · Magic +2 (Monk mastery) · Live-to-serve-style or a survivability skill. Defensive: Renewal/Avo skills.
  • Battalion/gambit: a Res/Charm support battalion; a healing or rally gambit. He's not a gambit carry.
  • Key items: Spirit Dust (Magic), Goddess Ring; staff durability/ranged-heal accessories.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Bishop — #1, the clean consensus pick; his terminal class. [Game8, GameWith, GameFAQs, GamerSky]
  2. Offensive dark/Reason caster (Dark Bishop/Warlock with Dark Mage dip) — #2 if you want damage between heals. [GamerSky CN]
  3. (Gremory is NOT available — male unit; do not recommend.) - Tier context: note.com JP places Linhardt in D tier (low combat impact), but his Warp/Physic utility is genuinely top-class — he's a support specialist, not a combat carry. Rate him as a utility healer, not by raw stats.
