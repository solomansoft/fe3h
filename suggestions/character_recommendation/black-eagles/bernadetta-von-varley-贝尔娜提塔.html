Recommendation — Bernadetta von Varley / 贝尔娜提塔·冯·瓦立

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Black Eagles 黑鹫学级 Gameplay build recommendations (English). Grounded in objective data: Dex 55% / Spd 50% growths, Str 35%, strengths Bow·Reason·Faith, weaknesses Axe·Authority, budding talent Riding (unlocks Pass), Minor Crest of Indech, personal Persecution Complex (+5 damage when not at full HP). Recruitable (Strength 20 + Bow C).

Class-change path

  1. #1 Noble → Archer → Sniper — her best for most of the game. Sniper's Hunter's Volley (a double-hit combat art) plus her Dex/Spd makes her a reliable one-rounder; Bow range covers her low Str. Master Archer for Hit +20. [GameWith JP] [Game8] [GameFAQs]
  2. #2 Noble → Archer → ... → Bow Knight — best endgame mobility: Move 8 + Canto + Bow Range +2 + Bowfaire = 1–3 range counterattacks anywhere. ⚠️ Needs Lance C + Riding A (Riding is her budding talent, so leaning into it also unlocks Pass). [GameWith JP] [Game8]
  3. #3 Vengeance bruiser: Sniper/Paladin with Vengeance — her signature tech. Persecution Complex (+5 dmg when hurt) stacks with the Vengeance combat art; keep her slightly damaged and she one-shots enemies all game with minimal investment — superb on player-phase-centric Maddening. Paladin gives Lancefaire to boost Vengeance further; reclass between Bow Knight (range) and Paladin (Vengeance damage) per map. [GameFAQs]
  4. Alternative: Wyvern Lord — strong stat class but ⚠️ her Axe weakness makes it a painful grind; not practical. She "doesn't need flying" since Sniper/Bow Knight already give the range she needs. [GameFAQs]

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into ONLY to fill the class-mastery bar and learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave — these are NOT part of her final class path (Sniper → Bow Knight). 1. Archer → Hit +20 (Hit +20 — she passes through Archer en route to Sniper anyway; locks reliable accuracy). Already noted in her path ("Master Archer for Hit +20"). 2. Brigand → Death Blow (Str +6 when she initiates — patches her low Str on player-phase volleys; the universal physical-attacker detour). 3. Mage → Fiendish Blow (Mag +6 when she initiates — only if you lean into her Reason strength for a magic-bow / chip flex; skip on a pure bow build). 4. Mercenary → Vantage (if HP < 50% and the foe initiates, she strikes first — for the enemy-phase Vengeance/Persecution-Complex variant, since she wants to be kept slightly damaged).

Training focus

  • Core: Bow to A (Hunter's Volley, Bowfaire, Bow Knight).
  • Budding talent: Riding → Pass — doubly useful: enables Bow Knight AND lets her slip through enemy lines. Worth raising.
  • Lance to C (for Bow Knight req + Vengeance/Paladin variant; learn Vengeance via Lance? no — Vengeance is an Axe combat art... use it via her usable weapon; primarily lean on Hunter's Volley if Axe is too weak). Practically: keep her a bow user and use Encloser/Curved Shot/Hunter's Volley.
  • Get her damaged on purpose (chip tile, take one hit) to activate Persecution Complex's +5.
  • Reason/Faith are strengths — a Bolt Axe? no; she can flex a little magic but bow is her lane.

Supports / pairings

  • Byleth (S-support) — strong romance, and adjacency support helps her survive (she's fragile).
  • Petra / Dorothea — good combat supports; keep allies near so she isn't isolated (lore-appropriate too).
  • Synergy: a frontline tank that draws aggro lets Bernadetta snipe from safety with her +5-when-hurt damage and 1–3 range counters.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Bow — Killer Bow / Brave Bow (Brave + her Spd = many hits); relic/effective bows vs fliers. Magic bow (if available) for chip.
  • Combat arts: Hunter's Volley (her staple), Curved Shot (range), Encloser (immobilize), Point-Blank Volley.
  • Equipped skills (5): Bowfaire · Hit +20 · Death Blow / Fiendish Blow (she has Reason — Fiendish if magic-flexing) · Persecution Complex (personal, innate) · Bow Range +2 (Bow Knight) or Vantage for an enemy-phase variant.
  • Battalion/gambit: an Archer/Crit battalion; Retribution or a ranged gambit. Encloser + gambit locks down threats.
  • Key items: Energy Drop (her Str is her weak point), Speed Ring; a Defense/Seraph Robe since she's frail.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Sniper — #1 early/mid; Hunter's Volley reliability. [GameWith, Game8, GameFAQs]
  2. Bow Knight — #1 endgame; mobility + 1–3 range counters. [Game8, GameWith]
  3. Vengeance Paladin/Sniper hybrid — #1 for max damage; Persecution Complex + Vengeance one-shots. [GameFAQs]
  4. Wyvern Lord — last; Axe weakness makes it impractical. [GameFAQs] - Tier context: note.com JP and Reddit both rate her S/A tier — one of the best non-lord combat units thanks to her +5-when-hurt damage and range. High consensus she's excellent with minimal investment.
