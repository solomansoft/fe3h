Recommendation — Petra Macneary / 佩托拉·玛克涅亚

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Black Eagles 黑鹫学级 Gameplay build recommendations (English). Grounded in objective data: Spd 60% (top in house) / Dex 50% / Lck 45% / Str 40% growths, no Crest, strengths Sword·Axe·Bow·Riding, weaknesses Reason·Faith, no budding talent, personal Hunter's Boon (Crit +20 vs foes at ≤50% HP). Recruitable (Dexterity 10 + Riding C).

Class-change path

  1. #1 Commoner → Fighter → Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord — her best class by broad consensus. Axe + Flying are both strengths; Move 8 flying + her elite Speed make her a fearless dodge-flier who dives the enemy army. Master Brigand for Death Blow (patches her modest Str) and Wyvern Rider for Darting Blow. [GameWith JP-context] [GameFAQs] [Game8 lists Assassin] [GamerSky CN]
  2. #2 Commoner → Myrmidon → Mercenary → Assassin — Sword + Bow boons make this trivial to reach; great Move 6, Stealth, and bow access. Best treated as a stepping stone: master Assassin (Sword/Bow flexibility, Vantage from Mercenary) then switch back to Wyvern Lord. Several sources say embrace it short-term but a flier is her true endpoint. [GameFAQs] [Game8 (lists Assassin as her pick)]
  3. #3 Commoner → ... → Falcon Knight — slightly faster than Wyvern Lord and adds Res, but she's already extremely fast and "really appreciates the added Strength Wyvern Lord gives." ⚠️ Needs Lance A + Sword C + Flying B+ (Lance isn't a strength — tutor it). [GameFAQs]
  4. Alternative: Bow Knight / Sniper — uses her Bow strength for ranged crit-finishing (Hunter's Boon = Crit +20 on weakened foes). [GamerSky CN T2 bow-cavalry pick]

Mastery detours / skills to grab

Classes to certify into ONLY to fill the class-mastery bar and learn the permanent mastery ability, then leave — these are NOT part of her final class path (Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord). 1. Brigand → Death Blow (Str +6 when she initiates — fixes her one weak point, Str; surfaced from her path's "Master Brigand for Death Blow." She certifies through Fighter → Brigand anyway). 2. Pegasus Knight → Darting Blow (Spd +5 when she initiates — the flyer doubling detour. ⚠️ Correction: Darting Blow is the Pegasus Knight mastery, not Wyvern Rider's; Wyvern Rider's mastery is Seal Defense. As a female unit Petra can certify Pegasus Knight to grab it). 3. Mercenary → Vantage (strikes first when foe initiates at HP < 50% — picked up on her Myrmidon → Mercenary → Assassin stepping-stone route; surfaced from her path's "Vantage from Mercenary"). 4. Myrmidon → Speed +2 (flat +2 Spd — cheap grab while passing through Myrmidon toward Assassin).

Training focus

  • Core: Axe to A + Flying to A (Wyvern Lord) — Riding is already a strength to ease the path.
  • Death Blow (Brigand) is high priority — fixes her one weak point (Str).
  • Her Hunter's Boon (+20 Crit vs ≤50% HP foes) makes her a natural finisher/boss-killer — GamerSky CN explicitly recommends her for crit-sniping bosses.
  • Ignore Reason/Faith (weaknesses).
  • No budding talent to chase.

Supports / pairings

  • Dorothea — excellent paired ending and adjacency support; Dorothea's Songstress/heals keep Petra topped up.
  • Byleth (S-support) — strong romance option with good adjacency bonuses.
  • Edelgard / Ferdinand — solid combat supports; mobile partners that cover ground with her.
  • Synergy: as a dodge-flier she's a great enemy-phase magnet; pair with a healer (Dorothea/Linhardt) for sustain.

Weapon / item / battalion / skill loadout

  • Weapons: Axe — Killer Axe / Brave Axe (Brave + her Speed = many hits, stacks with Hunter's Boon crit); Sword — Killing Edge for crit boss-killing; Bow for chip/range.
  • Combat arts: Smash / Diamond Axe (axe), Hunter's Volley (if bow-built), Sword arts for crit.
  • Equipped skills (5): Axefaire (Wyvern Lord) · Death Blow · Darting Blow / Alert Stance+ · Hunter's Boon (personal, innate crit) · Defiant Crit or Hit +20. (Dodge-flier: swap in Avo skills.)
  • Battalion/gambit: a Crit or Avo battalion; Retribution for enemy-phase, or a Stride/offensive gambit. Her Speed + a dodge battalion = strong dodge-tank.
  • Key items: Energy Drop (Strength — her one shortfall), then Speed is already excellent; a Goddess/Avo accessory for dodge builds.

Ranked options & disagreements

  1. Wyvern Lord — #1; Axe/Flying strengths, mobility, and added Str. [GameFAQs, GamerSky, fe3h consensus]
  2. Assassin — strong and easy to reach; best as a master-then-leave stepping stone. Game8 lists Assassin as her top pick, but most depth sources prefer Wyvern Lord as the endpoint. [Game8 vs GameFAQs]
  3. Falcon Knight — viable, faster, but loses the Strength she wants. [GameFAQs]
  4. Bow Knight / Sniper — niche crit-finisher build leveraging Hunter's Boon. [GamerSky] - Disagreement summary: Game8 = Assassin, GameFAQs/community + GamerSky = Wyvern Lord. My verdict: Wyvern Lord #1 (added Str + Move beats Assassin's redundant speed); use Assassin only to grab Vantage/flexibility, then fly. Reddit/note.com rate Petra A/B tier — a top non-lord combat unit.
