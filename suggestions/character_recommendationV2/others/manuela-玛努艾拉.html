Manuela / 玛努艾拉 — Build Menu V2

Faculty / Church of Seiros recruit. Recruitable from Ch.8 (Byleth Lv 15) in the Academy Phase on all routes. Primarily a healer/support with unusual Speed (60% growth); respectable Str/Mag. No Crest. Personal Infirmary Master (adjacent allies get Crit Avoid +10).
Proficiencies: Sword / Faith / Riding (boons); Reason / HeavyArmor (bane); Lance/Axe/Bow/Brawl/Authority/Flying neutral.
Community default class is Bishop (Faith-A, lowest friction). Female → Gremory available.

Build 1 — Holy Knight (圣骑士)

Path: Monk/Priest → Mage/Cavalier line → Holy Knight. Cert: Faith B (boon) / Riding A (boon) — both her boons, light tutoring. Note Holy Knight technically lists a Reason requirement variant; her healer-flavored Holy Knight runs on Faith + Riding, keeping her off her Reason bane.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest, NO Fiendish Blow): White Magic Heal+5 [⛏ Priest, Faith — boon] boosts her core healing. Speed +2 [⛏ Myrmidon, Sword — boon] cheap permanent bump leaning on her 60% Spd. Do NOT dip Mage for Fiendish Blow — Reason is her BANE; skip it entirely.

5 skills: Faithfaire · White Magic Heal+5 · Faith Prowess 5 · Speed +2 · Renewal.

  • ✔ Mounted healer — 7 move + Canto fixes the 4-move-Bishop problem her 60% Spd makes wasteful; keeps Fortify/Physic in range.
  • ✔ Built entirely on her Faith + Riding boons — no Reason grind; can hold a Levin Sword off her Sword boon for chip.
  • ✔ Infirmary Master (Crit Avoid +10 aura) follows the front line on a mount.
  • ✘ Less Res than Bishop; not a pure spell-tank.
  • ✘ Healing partly redundant with Divine Pulse; a convenient extra healer, not a carry.

Build 2 — Dancer (舞者)

Path: win the Dance audition → Dancer (special class, no cert line). Her Sword boon + 60% Spd make Sword Avo / Sword Dance flex viable.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest, NO Fiendish Blow): Sword Avo+20 / Speed +2 [⛏ Myrmidon, Sword — boon] off her Sword boon, leaning on her Speed. White Magic Heal+5 [⛏ Priest, Faith — boon] if she keeps a healing kit. No Mage/Fiendish Blow — Reason is her bane.

5 skills: Sword Prowess 5 · Sword Avo+20 · Speed +2 · Faithfaire (keeps Heal) · Special Dance.

  • ✔ Refresh = an extra ally action every turn — best value-per-deployment if she wins the dance-off; her 60% Spd makes a self-defending high-Avo Dancer.
  • ✔ Can still carry a Heal staff for incidental healing while granting Dance.
  • ✘ Pulls her off pure healing; only one unit can be Dancer.
  • ✘ If she loses the audition this build is off the table.
