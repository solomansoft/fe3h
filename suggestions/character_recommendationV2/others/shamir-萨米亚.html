Shamir / 萨米亚 — Build Menu V2

Knights of Seiros recruit. Recruitable from ~Ch.6; auto-joins Silver Snow; recruitable on Azure Moon / Verdant Wind. Joins Lv 11 Sniper, Bow A, personal Survival Instinct (+4 Str/Mag/Dex/Spd for a turn after she initiates a kill). Premier archer the moment she joins.
Proficiencies: Bow / Lance / Reason (boons); Faith (bane); Sword/Axe/Brawl/Authority/Flying neutral. No gender-lock issues.

Build 1 — Bow Knight (弓骑士)

Path: stay Sniper / Cavalier → Bow Knight. Cert: Lance C (boon) / Bow A (boon — met on join) / Riding A (neutral) — only Riding needs training; light tutoring.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] harvest cheap off her Bow boon — Archer is her natural beginner step. Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — neutral] "essentially +12 damage," explicitly recommended for her; off-boon but cheap. (Skip Faith detours — Faith bane.)

5 skills: Bowfaire · Death Blow · Hit+20 · Bow Prowess 5 · Defiant Crit (or Vantage for an enemy-phase setup).

  • ✔ 8 move + Canto + 4-range no-counter bow — kiting nuke. "Generally better than Sniper, as evidenced by the level requirement."
  • ✔ Endgame she "reliably one-rounds all non-armored units" incl. Falcon Knights; a Magic Bow uses her Reason boon vs high-Res targets.
  • ✘ Loses Sniper's Hunter's Volley guaranteed double — burst dips vs the Sniper build.
  • ✘ Needs the Riding A grind (her only neutral gate).

Build 2 — Sniper (狙击手)

Path: stay (joins as Sniper, Bow A) → push Bow S+ for Bowfaire. Cert: Bow A — already met, zero tutoring.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] cheap accuracy harvest off her boon. Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — neutral] the +12-damage dip. (Skip Faith — bane.)

5 skills: Bowfaire · Death Blow · Hit+20 · Bow Prowess 5 · Vantage. (Hunter's Volley is the combat art she leads with.)

  • ✔ Hunter's Volley = guaranteed player-phase double — "overpowered, fantastic especially on Maddening where doubling is hard."
  • ✔ Zero setup, immediately a top archer; Survival Instinct snowballs her after a kill.
  • ✘ Only 5 move, no Canto — falls off on big Maddening maps vs Bow Knight.
  • ✘ No mounted utility; static positioning.
