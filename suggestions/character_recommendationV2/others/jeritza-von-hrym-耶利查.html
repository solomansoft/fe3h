Jeritza von Hrym / 耶利查 — Build Menu V2 🧩 DLC

⚠️ PLAYABLE ONLY ON CRIMSON FLOWER (红花线). On every other route he is the enemy boss "Death Knight (死亡骑士)" and cannot be recruited. Requires Expansion Pass (Wave 3 DLC). Joins automatically ~Ch.13 at high level in his unique Death Knight class, with monstrous Str and the game's near-highest Spd growth (60%).
Proficiencies: Sword (boon), Lance (boon), Brawl (boon), Riding (boon); Faith (bane), Authority (bane); budding Flying. Axe/Bow/Reason/HeavyArmor neutral.
Gender note: Jeritza is male, so Pegasus/Falcon Knight (and their Darting Blow mastery) are impossible — do not chase Darting Blow. Mastermind (his class skill) grants 2× class EXP, so masteries fill fast despite the late join.
Sources: Game8 / GameFAQs / fe3h.com (EN), GameWith / note.com (JP), GamerSky (CN).

Build 1 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主)

Path: unlock Flying budding talent → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Lance C (boon) / Axe A (neutral) / Flying A (budding → trainable). Axe is his one missing physical skill; Mastermind 2× EXP makes the grind fast.

Mastery detours: Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — neutral] — Axe is only neutral for him, but Death Blow stacks onto his monstrous Str for one-round kills, so it's worth the dip. SKIP Darting Blow — Pegasus Knight is female-only and Jeritza is male; he cannot get it. Lean on his native 60% Spd instead. Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — neutral] optional accuracy.

5 skills: Lancefaire · Death Blow · Hit+20 · Defiant Crit · Alert Stance+.

  • ✔ 8 move + flight + Canto + Avo+10 turns his Str/Spd frame into a flying deleter; matches his Flying budding talent.
  • ✔ Carries the Scythe of Sariel (lance relic) on his Lance boon; Killer weapons exploit his crit ceiling.
  • ✔ Mastermind 2× EXP solves the late-join mastery problem; even plain Wyvern Rider is a fine low-effort CF endgame.
  • ✘ Axe A is neutral (not a boon) — the one investment.
  • ✘ No Darting Blow available (male) — relies on natural Spd for doubles.

Build 2 — War Master (战神)

Path: Brawler → Grappler → War Master (Master Seal). Cert: Brawl A (boon — free) + Axe A (neutral). Male-only class — fine for Jeritza.

Mastery detours: Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C] as above; Quick Riposte [⛏ War Master] is the class's own mastery (always follow up on enemy phase at high HP).

5 skills: Axefaire · Fistfaire · Crit+20 · Death Blow · Quick Riposte.

  • ✔ Crit+20 + dual faire + his Spd = he crits and doubles everything; plays straight to his Brawl boon.
  • ✔ No Flying grind needed — cheapest path to a finished build.
  • ✘ Foot (6 move) — loses Wyvern's mobility, Canto, and Avoid on Maddening-style maps.
  • ✘ More exposed front-liner; no air access to back-line targets.
