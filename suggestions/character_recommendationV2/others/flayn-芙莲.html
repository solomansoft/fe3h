Build 1 — Bishop (主教)

Path: Monk/Priest → Bishop. Cert: Faith A (boon) — natural for her Faith focus, light tutoring. No detour required (her role is pure support; she joins as a healer).

Mastery detours (none needed — pure healer): If you want budding payoff, complete Reason (budding talent) for Seal Magic. Avoid all Flying/Pegasus detours (Flying bane) — do NOT chase Darting Blow. Fiendish Blow (Mage) only if you ever build her offensive, otherwise skip.

5 skills: Faithfaire · White Magic Heal+5 · Faith Prowess 5 · Magic +2 · Renewal. (Seal Magic if budding completed.)

  • ✔ Near-unkillable by magic — Bishop +5 Res on top of her 50% Res growth = premier "magic bait" while she heals.
  • ✔ Extra heal uses + full protective Faith list (Fortify/Physic/Warp) + her unique Caduceus range. Personal Lily's Poise = adjacent allies take −3 dmg.
  • ✔ Best as Seteth's attack adjutant — +6 dmg to his Swift Strikes (story-natural pairing).
  • ✘ 4 move, no flight (and flight is her bane — don't fix it that way).
  • ✘ Healing partly redundant once Divine Pulse is online; pure-healer value is real but not a combat carry.
