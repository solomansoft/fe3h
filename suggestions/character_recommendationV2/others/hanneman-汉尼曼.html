Build 1 — Warlock (术师)

Path: Monk → Mage → Warlock. Cert: Reason A (boon) — natural fit, light tutoring (Warlock is Reason-only). The game even has an unused War-Phase Warlock icon for him, confirming intended design.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] top priority — +6 Mag on initiation harvested cheap off his Reason boon; Mage is his natural step. Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] uses his Bow boon to fix mage accuracy/Spd, explicitly suggested. Magic +2 [⛏ Monk] cheap early Mag bump. (Skip HeavyArmor detours — bane.)

5 skills: Black Tomefaire · Fiendish Blow · Hit+20 · Reason Prowess 5 · Defiant Magic.

  • ✔ "Fully utilizes his offensive magic potential" — Black Tomefaire + long-range Meteor; both harvest skills (Fiendish Blow, Hit+20) sit on boons.
  • ✔ Cheapest path (Reason-only gate); combat-ready with light training. Rally Magic +4 buffs a magic carry.
  • ✘ Static 4-move caster, no Canto — positioning is awkward on big maps.
  • ✘ As a male, capped below Gremory mages; a coverage pick, not an endgame carry.

Build 2 — Dark Knight (暗骑士)

Path: Monk → Mage → Dark Mage → Dark Knight. Cert: Reason B (boon) / Lance C (neutral) / Riding A (boon) — Reason & Riding are boons; only Lance C needs a dip. Mid tutoring.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] same cheap Reason-boon harvest. Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] Bow-boon accuracy. (Skip HeavyArmor — bane.)

5 skills: Black Tomefaire · Fiendish Blow · Hit+20 · Reason Prowess 5 · Lifetaker (or Defiant Magic).

  • ✔ 8 move + Canto on a mage — solves the Warlock positioning problem; only class combining big magic with mounted mobility.
  • ✔ Leans on TWO boons (Reason + Riding); Lance also lets him chip in melee. Best pick if you want a mobile nuker.
  • ✘ Loses Black Tomefaire's exclusivity edge and some raw Mag vs Warlock; needs the Lance dip.
  • ✘ Still male-capped (no Gremory); a strong utility mage, not a top-tier carry.
