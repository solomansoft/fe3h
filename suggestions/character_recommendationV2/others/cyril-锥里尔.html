Build 1 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主)

Path: Brigand/Archer-line beginner → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Lance C (boon) / Axe A (boon) / Flying A (boon) — every gate is a boon, so cert is fast and Aptitude makes weapon ranks rise quickly. Light tutoring to accelerate.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] bank it early while Aptitude growths snowball; top priority. Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] his Bow is also a boon, so harvest it cheaply for accuracy. (Skip Reason/Faith detours — both his banes.)

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Hit+20 · Defiant Crit · Axe Prowess 5.

  • ✔ Aptitude turns him into "Leonie/Petra-level growths" — recruited early he becomes an elite flier. 8 move + flight + Canto + Defiant Crit.
  • ✔ Both his offensive boons harvest for free on the path (Axe→Death Blow, Bow→Hit+20); strong Dex/Spd support evasion + reliable doubles.
  • ✘ Value is gated on early recruitment — a late grab wastes the Aptitude compounding and he stays weak.
  • ✘ Low join bases mean an investment window before he carries; needs feeding early.
