Catherine / 卡多莉奴 — Build Menu V2

Knights of Seiros recruit. Auto-joins Silver Snow (Ch.12); recruitable ~Ch.4 on Blue Lions / Golden Deer; enemy on Crimson Flower. Joins Lv 11 Swordmaster, Sword A, Major Crest of Charon, relic Thunderbrand.
Proficiencies: Sword (boon), Brawl (boon — unusable*); Reason (bane); Axe/Bow/Lance/Flying neutral.
* Gender lock: Brawler/Grappler/War Master and Hero are male-only — Catherine is female, so her Brawl boon is dead and Hero is impossible (the old guide listed it in error).

Build 1 — Swordmaster (剑圣)

Path: stay (joins as Swordmaster, Sword A) → push Sword S+ for Swordfaire. Cert: Sword A — already met, zero tutoring.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — neutral] fixes Swordmaster accuracy; Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — neutral] extra player-phase damage. Both off-boon-but-cheap; optional on Normal.

5 skills: Swordfaire · Death Blow · Hit+20 · Battalion Vantage · Sword Crit+10.

  • ✔ Combat-ready on arrival — no investment, maximizes Thunderbrand + Foudroyant Strike.
  • ✔ +4 Spd class bonus = reliable doubles; her personal makes her tanky with a low/no battalion.
  • ✘ Only 5 move — falls off on Maddening maps.
  • ✘ Sword Might < axe; no flight/Canto.

Build 2 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主)

Path: Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Lance C / Axe A / Flying A — Axe & Flying are neutral for her, so this is a real tutoring investment.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C] sits on the path already — free harvest.

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Hit+20 · Defiant Crit · Alert Stance+.

  • ✔ 8 move + flight + Canto — solves the Swordmaster mobility problem the community flags for Maddening.
  • ✔ Axe Might + her high Str = a fearless KO flier.
  • ✘ Heavy tutoring (Axe A + Flying A from nothing); drops Thunderbrand synergy.
  • ✘ Loses the zero-setup advantage that is her whole appeal.

Build 3 — Assassin (刺客)

Path: Myrmidon/Mercenary → Assassin. Cert: Sword C (boon) + Bow C (neutral) — light.

5 skills: Swordfaire · Sword Avo+20 · Hit+20 · Death Blow · Lethality.

  • ✔ Keeps her Sword boon + Thunderbrand; high Avoid, 6 move, stealth, Lethality procs.
  • ✔ Cheap distinct build for a low-stakes route.
  • ✘ Lower raw output than Wyvern Lord; needs the Bow C dip.
  • ✘ Not a Maddening-grade carry.

Not Hero — Hero is male-only and impossible for Catherine.
