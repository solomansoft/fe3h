Build 1 — War Master (战神 · male-only)

Path: joins Warrior → War Master. Cert: Axe A (boon) / Brawl A (boon) — both boons, he joins with high axe, so little to no tutoring; just push both to A.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] top priority, his single best damage skill, harvested cheap off his Axe boon. Fierce Iron Fist [⛏ Grappler, Brawl B] sits on his Brawl-boon path. Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — neutral] accuracy insurance for his mediocre Dex. (Skip Reason — bane.)

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Quick Riposte (post-mastery) · Battalion Wrath · Axe Prowess 5.

  • ✔ Consensus best class — Axefaire + Crit +20, master skill Quick Riposte (guaranteed enemy-phase double) + War Master's Strike. Near-zero cert cost off his two boons.
  • ✔ Top Str growth, guaranteed counter — a well-rounded frontline body when the roster is thin.
  • ✘ Late recruit with mediocre 45/40 growths — feed him kills early or he plateaus; 5 move, no Canto.
  • ✘ "Generally not powerful enough to replace a well-trained student" — a stopgap.

Build 2 — Great Knight (大骑士)

Path: Warrior/Armored Knight → Great Knight. Cert: Axe A (boon) / HeavyArmor A (boon) / Riding A (neutral) — Axe and HeavyArmor are both boons for him, so only Riding A needs the grind. Mid tutoring.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] same cheap Axe-boon harvest, gives his offense bite. Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — neutral] accuracy. (Skip Reason — bane; skip Flying — bane.)

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Hit+20 · Pavise · Axe Prowess 5.

  • ✔ Mounted + armored — keeps his HeavyArmor-boon tankiness but fixes the immobile-armor problem with 7 move + Canto.
  • ✔ HeavyArmor being a boon (unlike Gilbert) makes this a clean fit; high Def/HP soak.
  • ✘ Low Spd gets doubled by fast enemies; play him as a mobile wall, not a doubler.
  • ✘ Riding A grind on top of a late recruit; still a depth pick, not a carry.
