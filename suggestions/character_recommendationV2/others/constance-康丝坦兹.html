Build — Dark Flier (暗天马, female-only)

Path: Monk/Mage → Warlock (or Gremory) to grow Mag first → Dark Flier (Abyssian Master Seal, Lv20+). Cert: Reason B+ (boon) / Flying C (boon) — both boons, cheap.

⚠️ Critical: Dark Flier has NO Tomefaire and NO Mag-growth bonus (it gives Spd/Res growth instead). Level her in Warlock/Gremory first for the +10% Mag growth, THEN switch to Dark Flier late purely for the 8-move flight + Canto. Carry Black Tomefaire over from the Warlock/Mortal-Savant access since Dark Flier lacks it.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] the cornerstone +6 Mag on initiate for her 60% Mag frame — natural beginner step, harvest early. Uncanny Blow [⛏ Valkyrie] fixes her shaky long-range Bolting accuracy; certify, master, move on. Never touch Axe/Brigand — Axe is a bane (so no Death Blow harvest here).

5 skills: Black Tomefaire (carried over) · Fiendish Blow · Uncanny Blow · Hit+20 · Alert Stance+.

  • ✔ Flying (her boon) + 8 move + Canto turns the glass cannon into a hit-and-retreat nuke vs Res-weak targets.
  • ✔ Her 60% Mag carries damage; Circadian Beat +3 Mag indoors makes her terrifying on indoor maps.
  • ✔ Reason + Flying are both boons, so the whole path is cheap tutoring.
  • ✘ Must spend a Warlock/Gremory phase FIRST — Dark Flier adds no Mag and no Tomefaire, so committing early caps her damage.
  • ✘ Extremely frail (flier = bow-weak); keep her behind a body-blocker, out of archer range.

Foot alt Gremory (x2 spell uses, +5 Mag, +Mag growth) wins on raw output; Valkyrie (mounted, +1 range, Uncanny Blow) wins on range/accuracy. Dark Flier wins on mobility — all three want the Warlock Mag phase first.
