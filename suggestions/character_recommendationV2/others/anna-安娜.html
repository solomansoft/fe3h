Anna / 安娜 — Build Menu V2 🧩 DLC

Special recruit (Wave 3 DLC / Expansion Pass). Recruitable into any house/route as early as Ch.3, no requirements. High Luck (personal Business Prosperity = +5 Lck) and high Speed (55%), but mediocre Str (35%). Anna CANNOT build supports with anyone — plan her as a self-sufficient combat unit, not a pair-up / Linked-Attack piece.
Proficiencies: Sword (boon), Axe (boon), Bow (boon), Faith (boon); Reason (bane), Authority (bane); budding Riding. Lance/Brawl/Flying/HeavyArmor neutral.
Intended class: Trickster (Luck/Sword theme — Lucky Seven). We build her Wyvern Lord instead: it fixes her only weakness (Str) and harvests her Axe + Bow boons. Avoid Reason/Authority builds (both banes).
Sources: Game8 / GameFAQs / fe3h.com (EN), GameWith / note.com (JP), GamerSky (CN).

Build — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主)

Path: Myrmidon → Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Lance C / Axe A (boon — cheap) / Flying A (neutral). Only Flying is a real grind; Axe is a boon.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] sits on the path already — free harvest, and the +6 Str on initiate compensates her mediocre Str. Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] her Bow is a boon too, so accuracy insurance is cheap. SKIP Darting Blow — Pegasus Knight is female-only (Anna is female, so it's available, but Lance is only neutral and the Brigand/Archer harvests come first); grab it only if you over-train Flying.

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Hit+20 · Defiant Crit · Alert Stance+.

  • ✔ 8 move + flight + Canto — solves her foot-class mobility ceiling on Maddening-tier maps.
  • ✔ +4 Str class bonus + Death Blow patches her only stat weakness; her 55% Spd + high Luck make her dodge-y and double-happy.
  • ✔ Self-sufficient — Canto + high Avo + a Faith dip for self-Heal means she needs no support partner (she has none).
  • ✘ Flying A from nothing is the one real tutoring cost.
  • ✘ Sword Might < axe; she abandons the "intended" Trickster Luck gimmick.

Intended class Trickster (Lucky Seven + Foul Play) is thematic but lower-output; Wyvern Lord is the meta stat-fix. No supports possible regardless of class.
