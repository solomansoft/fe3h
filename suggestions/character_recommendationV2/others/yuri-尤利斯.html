Build — Dancer (舞者)

Path: win the White Heron Cup with Yuri (high Cha 50% → strong odds) → Dancer. Cert: none beyond the contest; he fights with his Sword boon. Keep Sword climbing for Sword Avo+ on dance turns.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): on his way up, harvest Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — bane] only if you happen through it (Axe is a bane — generally SKIP); his Sword carries combat. Vantage [⛏ Mercenary, Sword/Bow C — Sword boon] pairs with his Spd/Avo for an enemy-phase dodge-and-counter. Unlock budding Bow → Deadeye if you want a safe ranged option (Honorable Spirit loves him detached).

5 skills: Sword Avo+20 · Sword Crit+10 · Vantage · Hit+20 · Alert Stance+.

  • ✔ Arguably the best dancer in the game — high Cha + Sword Avo means he refreshes an ally AND survives the front as a dodge-tank.
  • ✔ Refresh is a force-multiplier the whole team uses; his 65% Spd keeps his own combat relevant between dances.
  • ✔ Zero off-boon tutoring — all on Sword; Fetters of Dromi adds reach to reposition.
  • ✘ Utility role, not a raw-damage carry — his combat ceiling (Trickster) goes unused.
  • ✘ Honorable Spirit rewards being ALONE, which fights the "stand next to an ally to dance" positioning — manage spacing.

Combat-first alternative: Trickster (Sword B + Faith C, both boons) — Foul Play swap, Duelist's Blow Avo+20, Faith self-heal, boosted by Fetters of Dromi. Use it if the team already has a dancer.
