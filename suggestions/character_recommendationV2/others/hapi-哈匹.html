Ashen Wolves (Cindered Shadows DLC). Recruitable into Part 1 after clearing Cindered Shadows Ch.5. Personal Monstrous Appeal (effective vs monsters; monsters more likely to target her). Build 1 — Dark Knight (暗骑士)

Path: Mage → Warlock (grow Mag) → Dark Knight (Master Seal). Cert: Lance C (neutral) / Reason B+ (boon) / Riding A (boon) — Riding is a boon, so the mount req is cheap; only the small Lance C dip is off-profile.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] the cornerstone +6 Mag on initiate — natural beginner step, harvest early. Uncanny Blow [⛏ Valkyrie] optional accuracy fix for long-range tomes. Seal Resistance [⛏ Dark Knight] its own mastery, debuffs enemy Res. Never Brigand/Axe — Axe is only budding and Brawl is a bane.

5 skills: Dark Tomefaire · Black Tomefaire · Fiendish Blow · Hit+20 · Seal Resistance.

  • ✔ The ONLY player class with Dark Tomefaire (+5 to dark magic) — directly boosts her signature Banshee Θ / Death Θ list.
  • ✔ Mounted 7 move + Canto on her Riding boon = hit a Res-weak target and pull back to safety.
  • ✔ High Res; Dark Knight even adds +Mag growth, so it's a fine final shell after a short Warlock phase.
  • ✘ Very low Def/Lck — keep her out of bow range and behind a blocker.
  • ✘ Lance C is a minor off-profile dip.

Build 2 — Valkyrie (圣魔导, DLC, female-only)

Path: Mage → Warlock (grow Mag) → Valkyrie (Abyssian Master Seal). Cert: Reason B+ (boon) / Faith C (neutral) / Riding (boon).

Mastery detours: Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] as above; Uncanny Blow [⛏ Valkyrie] is the class's own mastery — Hit boost on initiate fixes long-range tome accuracy.

5 skills: Black Tomefaire · Fiendish Blow · Uncanny Blow · Hit+20 · Alert Stance+.

  • ✔ Mounted caster with +1 magic range + Canto; Uncanny Blow makes long-range nukes reliable.
  • ✔ Equip Thyrsus for absurd Banshee/Bolting range from total safety.
  • ✘ No Dark Tomefaire — slightly weaker on her dark-heavy spell list than Dark Knight.
  • ✘ Same frailty; lives off range + Canto rather than bulk.

Foot alt Gremory (x2 dark spells, +5 Mag) maximizes spell economy but loses Canto. Dark Knight edges all of them on her dark list thanks to the exclusive Dark Tomefaire.
