Build 1 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主)

Path: joins Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Lance C (boon) / Axe A (boon) / Flying A (neutral) — he is already B Axe / B Lance / C Flying on join, so only Flying needs topping up. Near-zero tutoring.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] harvest cheap off his Axe boon; +6 Str on Swift Strikes is enormous. Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — neutral] accuracy insurance for his mediocre Dex. (No HeavyArmor detours — HeavyArmor is his bane; skip Armored Blow/Weight−.)

5 skills: Lancefaire · Death Blow · Hit+20 · Defiant Crit · Lance Prowess 5. (Swift Strikes is the combat art he leads with.)

  • ✔ Joins in the wyvern line — staying the course wastes none of the Part-1 skill-building he missed; combat-ready immediately.
  • ✔ Swift Strikes (Lance A) 1–2 player-phase double off his high Str; Major Crest of Cichol gives 75% no-counter. 8 move + flight + Canto = best physical class.
  • ✔ Flayn as attack adjutant adds up to +6 dmg to Swift Strikes — equip her on him.
  • ✘ Mediocre Spd — Darting Blow / Death Blow patch it but he won't naturally double everything.
  • ✘ Don't bother with his ~35% Mag / Holy Knight flex — strictly worse for him.
