Gilbert (Gustave) / 吉尔伯特 — Build Menu V2

Knights of Seiros — Azure Moon ONLY. Auto-joins Ch.13 (War Phase) at ~Lv 26 Fortress Knight; no academy-phase access, no other route. High Def/HP, low Spd/Res, mediocre Mag, no Crest. Personal Veteran Knight (−2 dmg while a battalion is equipped).
Proficiencies: Lance / Axe / Authority / Riding (boons); HeavyArmor (bane); Sword/Bow/Brawl/Reason/Faith neutral.
* Key trap: his join class (Fortress Knight) and the "tank" path lean on HeavyArmor, which is his BANE — that grind is the thing to avoid. No gender-lock issues.
Sources: Game8 / GameFAQs / Reddit / Neoseeker / Fandom (EN), note.com (JP), GamerSky (CN).

Build 1 — Fortress Knight (重装骑士)

Path: stay (joins Fortress Knight). Cert: HeavyArmor A (BANE) — he joins already in the class so it's "free" on arrival, but pushing/holding HeavyArmor A is a grind against his bane, and he uses a weaker Fortress variant carried over from his Ch.5 NPC build.

Mastery detours: Pavise [⛏ Fortress Knight — already here] halve physical damage; core wall skill, cheap since he joins armored. Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] harvest off his Axe boon to give his weak offense bite. ⚠ The whole HeavyArmor line is his bane — this is the build you only take if you accept the grind.

5 skills: Lance/Axe Prowess 5 · Pavise · Death Blow · Defense +2 · Veteran Knight / battalion-defense.

  • ✔ #1 in community guides as his simplest role — no reclass, leans on his huge Def/HP for a wall on join.
  • ✔ Pavise + a defense battalion + Veteran Knight make a stubborn damage soak.
  • ✘ ⚠ HeavyArmor is his BANE — holding the rank fights his weakness; weak Res, 4 move, and a downgraded Fortress variant.
  • ✘ Low Spd → doubled by fast enemies; pure wall, near-zero offense. We recommend a non-armor build below instead.

Build 2 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主)

Path: Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Lance C (boon) / Axe A (boon) / Flying A (neutral) — Lance & Axe are boons; only Flying A needs training. Mid tutoring, and it gets him entirely off his HeavyArmor bane.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] cheap off his Axe boon, gives him real offense. Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — neutral] accuracy. (Skip the HeavyArmor line — bane.)

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Hit+20 · Defiant Crit · Axe Prowess 5.

  • ✔ Triangle Attack pick — three fliers around a target force a guaranteed crit-style combo; built on his Axe boon.
  • ✔ 8 move + flight + Canto fixes his Speed/mobility problem (move covers what his low Spd can't), and Death Blow makes his Str finally hit hard.
  • ✘ Flying A grind from scratch on a Ch.13 recruit; loses his armor-tank identity.
  • ✘ Lower Def than the wall builds — he flies, he doesn't soak.

Build 3 — Paladin (圣骑士 · low-effort)

Path: Cavalier → Paladin. Cert: Lance A (boon) / Riding A (boon) — both boons, so the cheapest path, and it completely sidesteps his HeavyArmor bane.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Aegis [⛏ Paladin — already here] halve ranged/magic damage (the other half of the Aegis/Pavise wall). Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] Axe-boon offense. (Skip HeavyArmor — bane.)

5 skills: Lancefaire · Death Blow · Aegis · Defense +2 · Lance Prowess 5.

  • ✔ Lowest-effort optimal build — both cert gates are boons, no grind against any weakness; 7 move + Canto.
  • ✔ Still tanky enough (Aegis + his natural Def) to wall, but mobile; keeps Veteran Knight value with a battalion.
  • ✘ Less raw Def than Fortress; low Spd still means few doubles.
  • ✘ A solid, efficient body — not a damage carry on a late recruit.
