Build — War Master (战神)

Path: Brawler → Grappler → War Master (Master Seal). Cert: Brawl A (boon) + Axe A (boon) — both boons, the cheapest top-tier path in the game. Male-only class — fine for Balthus.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] harvest it cheaply — +6 Str on his already huge Str = one-round KOs. Fierce Iron Fist [⛏ Grappler] the 3-hit combat art sits on his own path. Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — bane] Bow is a bane, but his low Dex/Spd really wants accuracy — take the cheap Archer dip anyway (Hit+20 is the payoff, you never keep the Bow rank).

5 skills: Fistfaire · Axefaire · Death Blow · Crit+20 · Quick Riposte.

  • ✔ Crit+20 + dual faire + Quick Riposte on Brawl/Axe boons — zero off-boon weapon grind.
  • ✔ King of Grappling makes him hit HARDER and tankier as he drops to ≤50% HP — a self-buffing front-line wall.
  • ✔ Grappler alt (Brawl A only) trades Crit+20 for the tripling Fierce Iron Fist with no Mov penalty — also fully on-boon.
  • ✘ Foot (6 move); his low Spd means he can be doubled — lean on Quick Riposte + bulk.
  • ✘ Low Dex needs the Hit+20 detour (through his Bow bane).

War Monk (DLC, canon class) adds Faith self-heal on his Faith boon — a more self-sufficient alt if you want sustain over Crit+20. Avoid Flying (bane).
