Claude von Riegan / 克劳德 — Build Menu V2

Golden Deer house leader — locked lord, Verdant Wind only. Cannot be recruited away; the route's protagonist. Minor Crest of Riegan (combat-art HP drain); growths skew Dex 60 / Spd 55 / Cha 55 / Str 40, +20% personal EXP (Leicester Lineage).
Proficiencies: Sword (boon), Bow (boon), Authority (boon), Flying (boon); Lance (bane), Faith (bane); Axe neutral. Budding talent: Axe → Diamond Axe (turns Axe into an effective boon once popped).
Harvest: Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] on-boon, free; Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C] via his Axe budding (pop Axe first, then the cert is on-strength). Skip Darting Blow — Pegasus Knight is female-only, so Claude cannot get it.
Sources: Game8 / GameFAQs / Fire Emblem Wiki (EN), GameWith / note.com (JP), GamerSky (CN).

Build 1 — Barbarossa (大盗) recommended

Path: Noble → Archer → Wyvern Rider → Barbarossa (his unique Part 2 master class). Cert: Bow A (boon) + Flying A (boon) + Lance C (bane — small dip). A flying bow class tailor-made for him [Game8].

Mastery detours: Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] on-boon, already on the path — free harvest, keeps his bow landing on Maddening. Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C] — pop his Axe budding first, then the Brigand cert is on-strength; gives +6 Str on every initiation. Skip Darting Blow — Pegasus Knight is female-only.

5 skills: Bowfaire · Hit+20 · Death Blow · Defiant Crit · Alert Stance+.

  • ✔ Mov 8 + flight + Canto on a bow platform — fires 1–2 range and repositions; his best mobility-to-damage package.
  • ✔ Keeps bow range AND wields axe (Diamond Axe) — leverages two of his boons plus the budding.
  • ✔ Cheapest flying build: Bow/Flying are both boons, only the Lance C gate touches a bane.
  • ✘ Slightly lower raw axe Mt than Wyvern Lord; no Axefaire.
  • ✘ Requires popping the Axe budding to get Death Blow on-strength.

Build 2 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主)

Path: Noble → Archer → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Lance C (bane — dip) / Axe A (neutral → boon once Axe budding popped) / Flying A (boon).

Mastery detours: Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C] sits on the path once Axe is budded — free harvest. Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] on-boon, keep his bow accurate for the 1–2 range option.

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Hit+20 · Avo+10 · Defiant Crit.

  • ✔ Best physical class in the game — Axefaire + Avo+10 + Mov 8 + Canto; highest raw KO ceiling.
  • ✔ His Crest of Riegan HP-drain rewards diving with combat arts.
  • ✘ Leans on the Axe budding (must pop it) and a bigger Axe A grind than Barbarossa.
  • ✘ Slightly less bow focus — you're committing to axe melee over kiting.

Build 3 — Sniper (狙击手)

Path: Noble → Archer → Sniper (optionally → Bow Knight for Mov 8). Cert: Bow A (boon) only — no flying or Lance gate. GamerSky CN calls him "the best archer in the game."

Mastery detours: Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] mastered on the way through — free, on-boon. Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C] optional after popping Axe budding for extra burst.

5 skills: Bowfaire · Hit+20 · Death Blow · Bowrange+1 · Battalion Vantage.

  • ✔ Bowrange+1 + the broken Hunter's Volley (two hits, +Crit) — zero flying investment.
  • ✔ Pure on-boon build; certifies fastest.
  • ✘ Foot Mov 5 — falls behind on big Maddening maps (Bow Knight fixes it but has weak growths).
  • ✘ Drops the flight + Canto that make him a premier diver.
