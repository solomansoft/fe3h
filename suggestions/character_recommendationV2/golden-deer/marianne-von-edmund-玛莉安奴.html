Marianne von Edmund / 玛莉安奴 — Build Menu V2

Golden Deer house member (auto-joins Verdant Wind; recruitable elsewhere). Minor Crest of the Beast (chance +Mt on weapon attacks); holds Hero's Relic sword Blutgang. Growths Mag 50 / Res 45 / Dex 40 / Spd 40, low Str 20 / Def 15. Animal Friend personal heals 20% near cavalry/fliers.
Proficiencies: Sword (boon), Faith (boon), Riding (boon), Flying (boon); Brawl (bane), Heavy Armor (bane); Lance/Axe/Bow/Reason neutral. Budding talent: Lance → Frozen Lance (magic-scaling lance art — sidesteps her weak Str).
Harvest: Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — neutral] for offensive casting; Darting Blow [⛏ Pegasus Knight, Lance C + Flying D — Flying boon, female-OK] for doubling. Skip Brawler (Brawl bane). Non-DLC keeps her as a healer/Dancer; Holy Knight is the DLC-flavor alt.
Sources: Game8 / Neoseeker / GameFAQs (EN), note.com (JP), GamerSky (CN).

Build 1 — Dancer (舞者) non-DLC pick

Path: Noble → Monk → Priest → Dancer (win the White Heron Cup with her high Cha). Cert: none beyond the dance-off; keeps her Faith Physic/heal kit (Faith boon). A non-DLC role that turns her modest combat into top-tier utility.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — neutral] for any offensive casting; Darting Blow [⛏ Pegasus Knight, Lance C — boon-adjacent, female-OK] if she'll ever melee with Frozen Lance. Never Brawler.

5 skills: Sword Avo+20 (Dancer) · Fiendish Blow · Movement+1 · Defiant Mag · Faith heal passive.

  • ✔ Dance refresh = an extra turn for a carry every round — the single highest-value action in the game.
  • ✔ Retains Faith Physic/Recover healing; her low offense stops being a liability as a support pivot.
  • ✔ +2 Mov, Sword Avo+20, and keeps Blutgang for emergencies.
  • ✘ Only one Dancer slot per team — competes with other dance candidates.
  • ✘ Minimal personal combat; she enables others rather than KOing.

Build 2 — Holy Knight (圣骑士) DLC alt

Path: Noble → Monk → Priest → Bishop → Holy Knight. Cert: Faith A (boon) + Riding A (boon) — both on-strength, no remedial tutoring. Magic cavalry (Mov 8 + Canto) that heals/Physics and nukes then retreats [Game8, Neoseeker].

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — neutral] boosts every Aura/Nosferatu/Frozen-Lance initiation. Darting Blow [⛏ Pegasus Knight, Lance C — Flying boon] for doubling. Skip Brawler/armor (both banes).

5 skills: White Magic Avo+ · Fiendish Blow · Movement+1 · Canto (innate) · Defiant Mag.

  • ✔ Mobile pocket-healer — escorts the front, casts and Cantos out; her Faith + Riding boons make it free.
  • ✔ Frozen Lance (Lance budding) turns her high Mag into melee damage despite low Str.
  • ✘ Low HP/Def — keep her behind the front; she's a healer first.
  • ✘ note.com JP rates her C tier; invest stat boosters if she's a main.

Build 3 — Gremory (女神官)

Path: Noble → Monk → Mage → Warlock → Gremory (female-only). Cert: Faith A (boon) + Reason A (neutral). Pure caster — doubles all spell uses (huge for a healer who also nukes) + Mag+5 [GamerSky CN].

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — neutral] already on the path — master it for +6 Mag on initiation.

5 skills: Black/White Tomefaire · Fiendish Blow · Black & White Magic Uses x2 (innate) · Movement+1 · Defiant Mag.

  • ✔ Doubled heal AND nuke uses — a back-line battery who never runs dry on Physic/Aura.
  • ✔ Highest pure-caster ceiling for her; Mag+5 lifts her best stat.
  • ✘ Loses Holy Knight's Canto/mobility — static back-line.
  • ✘ Reason A is a neutral grind if you want offensive black magic too.
