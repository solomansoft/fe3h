Lorenz Hellman Gloucester / 洛伦兹 — Build Menu V2

Golden Deer house member (auto-joins Verdant Wind; recruitable to other houses via Charm/level). Minor Crest of Gloucester (chance +5 Mt on magic attacks); balanced growths HP 55 / Str 40 / Mag 40 / Dex 45 / Spd 40 — a rare lance+magic hybrid.
Proficiencies: Lance (boon), Reason (boon), Riding (boon); Brawl (bane); Sword/Axe/Bow/Faith neutral. No budding talent.
Harvest: Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] on-boon, free, stacks with his Crest Mt procs. Avoid the Brawl bane — never route through Brawler/Grappler; his Dark Knight path has no Brawl gate.

Build 1 — Dark Knight (暗黑骑士) recommended

Path: Noble → Mage → Warlock → Dark Knight. Cert: Reason A (boon) + Riding A (boon) + Lance C (boon) — all on-strength, no remedial tutoring. Dark Knight keeps Speed (only −5% growth vs Paladin's −10%).

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] already on the path en route to Warlock — master it; +6 Mag on initiation, stacks with his Crest of Gloucester Mt procs. Never touch Brawler (Brawl bane) — no brawl mastery is worth the grind.

5 skills: Black Tomefaire · Fiendish Blow · Movement+1 · Lancefaire · Defiant Mag.

  • ✔ Mov 8 + Canto magic cavalry — casts Ragnarok/Agnea's Arrow then repositions; sturdier than pure mages.
  • ✔ Leverages all three boons (Reason, Riding, Lance) — premium magic-cavalry fit; protects Speed.
  • ✔ Crest of Gloucester adds Mt to magic, stacking with Fiendish Blow.
  • ✘ Hybrid means neither Str nor Mag is elite — output below a dedicated nuker.
  • ✘ note.com JP rates him B tier — solid escort, not a top carry.

Build 2 — Paladin (圣骑士)

Path: Noble → Cavalier → Paladin (→ Great Knight optional). Cert: Lance A (boon) + Riding A (boon) — both on-strength.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — neutral] off-boon but cheap, +6 Str on physical-lance initiation. Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] still worth harvesting on-boon for any hybrid lance/magic hit. Skip Brawler (Brawl bane).

5 skills: Lancefaire · Death Blow · Movement+1 · Hit+20 · Defiant Crit.

  • ✔ Simplest build — uses his Lance boon + Distinguished House personal (+2 dmg with battalion); good Mov.
  • ✔ Zero magic investment if you'd rather a straightforward lance rider.
  • ✘ Ignores his Reason boon + Crest Mt synergy — lower ceiling than Dark Knight.
  • ✘ −10% Spd growth vs Dark Knight's −5%.
