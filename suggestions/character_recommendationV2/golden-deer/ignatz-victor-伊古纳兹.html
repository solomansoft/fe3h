Ignatz Victor / 伊古纳兹 — Build Menu V2

Golden Deer house member (auto-joins Verdant Wind; recruitable elsewhere). No Crest. Growths Dex 50 / Spd 50 / Lck 55 / Mag 30 / Str 35 — accurate, evasive, leans support/debuff. Watchful Eye personal grants Hit+20.
Proficiencies: Sword (boon), Bow (boon), Reason (boon), Authority (boon); Riding (bane); Lance/Axe/Faith/Brawl/Flying neutral. Budding talent: Reason → Seal Strength (−6 Str debuff).
Harvest: Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] on-boon, free — stacks with his personal Watchful Eye Hit+20 for near-100% accuracy. Pop the Reason budding → Seal Strength (equip passively, a must-have debuff). Avoid mounted routes — Bow Knight needs Riding A, his bane.
Sources: Game8 / Fire Emblem Wiki / GameFAQs (EN), note.com (JP), GamerSky (CN).

Build 1 — Sniper (狙击手) recommended

Path: Commoner → Fighter → Archer → Sniper (stay Sniper). Cert: Bow A (boon) only — no Riding gate, no remedial tutoring. Bowrange+1 + the elite Hunter's Volley (two hits, +Crit); with Break Shot (−5 Def) + budding Seal Strength (−6 Str) he is one of the best debuffers in the game [Fire Emblem Wiki, GamerSky CN].

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] already on the path — master it; stacks with his Watchful Eye personal Hit+20 for near-100% accuracy. Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — neutral] off-boon but cheap, compensates his modest Str. Pop Reason budding → Seal Strength (not a class detour — equip the debuff passively).

5 skills: Bowfaire · Hit+20 · Seal Strength · Death Blow · Bowrange+1.

  • ✔ Near-100% Hit (Hit+20 ×2) + Hunter's Volley crit-spam; his Dex/Lck love Killer Bow.
  • ✔ Premier debuffer — Break Shot + Seal Strength soften targets for the team's heavy hitters.
  • ✔ Pure on-boon build (Bow A only); avoids his Riding bane entirely.
  • ✘ Foot Mov 5; modest Str means he sets up kills rather than finishing big targets alone.
  • ✘ Needs the Reason budding popped for Seal Strength.

Build 2 — Assassin (刺客)

Path: Commoner → Myrmidon/Fighter → Thief → Assassin. Cert: Sword C (boon) + Bow C (boon) — both on-strength. Game8's pick: uses both his weapon boons + his high Spd/Lck for a dodgy infiltrator (Mov 6, stealth).

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] free, on-boon. Seal Strength via Reason budding still worth popping.

5 skills: Bowfaire/Swordfaire · Hit+20 · Seal Strength · Sword Avo+20 · Lethality.

  • ✔ High Avoid + Mov 6 + stealth; both cert weapons are boons.
  • ✔ A dodgy skirmisher that still carries his debuff utility.
  • ✘ Less impactful than the Sniper debuffer — loses Bowrange+1 and Hunter's Volley range.
  • ✘ Modest raw output; he's a utility unit either way.

Build 3 — Bow Knight (弓骑兵)

Path: Commoner → Fighter → Archer → Sniper → Bow Knight. Cert: Bow A (boon) + Lance C (neutral) + Riding A (BANE) — requires real Riding tutoring through his weakness. Same debuff kit but Mov 8 + Bowrange+2.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] already mastered en route. Seal Strength via Reason budding.

5 skills: Bowfaire · Hit+20 · Seal Strength · Bowrange+1 · Death Blow.

  • ✔ Mobile debuffer — Cantos in, fires 1–3 range, retreats; best mobility for his utility role.
  • ✘ Riding A is his bane — slow remedial tutoring; only take this if you commit to fixing it.
  • ✘ Bow Knight's weak growths lean entirely on his base stats.
