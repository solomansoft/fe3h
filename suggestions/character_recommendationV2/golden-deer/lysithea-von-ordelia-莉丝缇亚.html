Lysithea von Ordelia / 莉丝缇亚 — Build Menu V2

Golden Deer house member (auto-joins Verdant Wind; recruitable elsewhere — easy via Reason/Faith/Charm). Two Crests (Major Gloucester + Minor Charon, both add Mt). Growths Mag 60 / Dex 60 / Spd 50 — glass-cannon nuker; low HP 20 / Def 10. Mastermind personal doubles skill EXP (certifies fastest in the roster).
Proficiencies: Reason (boon), Faith (boon), Authority (boon); Sword (bane), Lance (bane), Axe (bane), Heavy Armor (bane); Bow/Brawl/Riding/Flying neutral. Budding talent: Sword → Soulblade (optional flavor).
Harvest: Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] on-boon, free; her Mastermind doubles skill EXP so she fills the bar fastest. Death Blow does NOT apply — she's a caster. Avoid Dark Knight — it requires Lance C, her bane; Valkyrie is the magic-cavalry alternative that needs no Lance.
Sources: GamerSky (CN), GameWith / note.com (JP), Reddit / Game8 (EN).

Build 1 — Gremory (女神官) SS pick

Path: Noble → Mage → Warlock → Gremory (female-only). Cert: Reason A (boon) + Faith A (boon) — both on-strength, no remedial tutoring. The consensus best mage in the game: Mag+5 modifier and doubles all spell uses — her biggest limiter is spell quantity (Warp/Dark Spikes T/Luna/Meteor) [GamerSky CN, GameWith JP, Reddit].

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] already on the path — master it for +6 Mag on initiation (Death Blow N/A — she's a caster). Her Mastermind doubles skill EXP, so she clears the mastery bar fastest. No physical detours — Brigand/Archer/Pegasus certs are slow through her Sword/Lance/Axe banes and pointless for a nuker.

5 skills: Fiendish Blow · Black Tomefaire · Black & White Magic Uses x2 (innate) · Movement+1 · Defiant Mag.

  • ✔ Highest raw magic ceiling + Major Crest of Gloucester Mt procs; Dark Spikes T/Luna obliterate armor & cavalry.
  • ✔ Doubled spell uses solve her one weakness (limited casts) — fire Warp/Meteor far more often.
  • ✔ Pure on-boon build (Reason + Faith both boons); certifies fastest via Mastermind.
  • ✘ Foot Mov 5 + fragile (HP 20 / Def 10) — must stay behind a tank.
  • ✘ No Canto — positioning is unforgiving on Maddening.

Build 2 — Valkyrie (圣女) VW pick

Path: Noble → Mage → Warlock → Valkyrie (female-only). Cert: Reason A (boon) + Riding (neutral) — no Lance gate. This is the magic-cavalry option deliberately chosen over Dark Knight, which requires Lance C — her bane. Valkyrie gives Mov 8 + Canto without touching a weakness.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] already on the path — master it. Mastermind fills the bar fastest. No physical detours (Sword/Lance/Axe all banes).

5 skills: Fiendish Blow · Black Tomefaire (Warlock) · Movement+1 · Canto (innate) · Defiant Mag.

  • ✔ Mov 8 + Canto fixes her fragility — nukes then retreats out of enemy range; huge survivability gain for a glass cannon.
  • ✔ Avoids the Dark Knight Lance C bane entirely while keeping magic-cavalry mobility.
  • ✔ Reason boon + Mastermind = cheap, fast setup; still casts her full nuke list.
  • ✘ No Tomefaire on Valkyrie itself — slightly less burst than Gremory (carry Black Tomefaire from Warlock mastery).
  • ✘ Still fragile if caught — use the mobility to stay safe, don't tank.
