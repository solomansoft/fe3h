Raphael Kirsten / 拉斐尔 — Build Menu V2

Golden Deer house member (auto-joins Verdant Wind; recruitable elsewhere). No Crest, no budding talent. Growths HP 65 / Str 50 / Def 45 — a tanky bruiser; very low Mag 15 / Spd 15 / Res 10 (will rarely double). Goody Basket personal self-heals (scales with Luck).
Proficiencies: Axe (boon), Brawl (boon), Heavy Armor (boon); Bow (bane), Riding (bane); Sword/Lance/Reason/Faith neutral.
Harvest: Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] on-boon, free; Fierce Iron Fist (Grappler) + Quick Riposte (War Master) are his class kit, not detours. Skip the Archer detour — Bow is a bane, so Hit+20 is a slow grind for little gain. War Master is male-only — Raphael qualifies.
Sources: Game8 / GameWith (JP), Reddit, GamerSky (CN).

Build 1 — War Master (战神) recommended

Path: Commoner → Fighter → Brigand → Grappler → War Master (male-only). Cert: Axe A (boon) + Brawl A (boon) — both on-strength, fast. Game's premier brawler: Str+5 modifier, Crit+20, and the Quick Riposte mastery gives the enemy-phase follow-up he otherwise lacks (low Spd) [Game8, GameWith JP, Reddit].

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] already on the path — free, on-boon; +6 Str turns his huge Str into massive player-phase burst. Skip Archer — Bow is a bane, Hit+20 would be a slow grind.

5 skills: Fistfaire · Death Blow · Quick Riposte · Crit+20 (innate) · Battalion Wrath.

  • ✔ Quick Riposte fixes his enemy phase — high HP/Def + guaranteed follow-up = self-sufficient bruiser.
  • ✔ Top Str modifier + Crit+20; both cert weapons are boons, so he trains fast.
  • ✔ Battalion Wrath + Vantage combo is brutal on his huge HP frame.
  • ✘ Foot Mov 5, no Canto; low Spd means he relies on Quick Riposte to double.
  • ✘ Fix accuracy with a high-Hit/Crit battalion (modest Dex).

Build 2 — Grappler (格斗家)

Path: Commoner → Fighter → Brigand → Grappler (male-only). Cert: Brawl A (boon). Grappler's Fierce Iron Fist hits three times for big burst, plus Fistfaire; slightly higher Spd modifier than War Master.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] already on the path — harvest before advancing.

5 skills: Fistfaire · Death Blow · Fierce Iron Fist (combat art) · Tomebreaker (mastery) · Battalion Wrath.

  • ✔ Fierce Iron Fist 3-hit burst is one of the highest single-target damage spikes in the game.
  • ✔ Essentially the penultimate stop of War Master — keep it if you've no Master Seal yet.
  • ✘ No Quick Riposte — weaker enemy phase than War Master.
  • ✘ Same Mov 5 / no Canto limitation.

Build 3 — Fortress Knight → Warrior (重装→战士)

Path: Commoner → Armored Knight → Fortress Knight (then → Warrior late). Cert: Heavy Armor (boon) then Axe A (boon). A wall route leveraging his huge Def; switch to Warrior late because Fortress Mov 4 is too low [GameWith JP].

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] on-boon. Avoid Bow/Riding classes — both banes.

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Quick Riposte · Hit+20 · Defiant Str.

  • ✔ Pure tank wall using his Heavy Armor boon + huge Def early.
  • ✔ Warrior pivot restores Mov + axe damage if Fortress feels too slow.
  • ✘ Defensive niche only — his Str/HP are wasted as a passive wall; consensus is to go offensive brawler.
  • ✘ Fortress Mov 4 cripples positioning until the Warrior swap.
