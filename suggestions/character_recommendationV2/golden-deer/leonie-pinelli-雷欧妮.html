Leonie Pinelli / 雷欧妮 — Build Menu V2

Golden Deer house member (auto-joins Verdant Wind; recruitable to other houses — easy via Bow/Lance/Strength). No Crest, no budding talent. Growths Spd 60 / Dex 55 / Str 40 / Def 40 — fast, accurate, durable; Rivalry personal gives +2 dmg / −2 taken next to male allies. "One of the best non-lord combat units" [Reddit].
Proficiencies: Lance (boon), Bow (boon), Riding (boon); no bane — certifies into almost anything without remedial tutoring.
Harvest: Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] on-boon, free; Darting Blow [⛏ Pegasus Knight, Lance C + Flying D — Lance boon, female-OK] the key Maddening doubler. Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — neutral] off-boon but painless given zero banes.
Sources: Fire Emblem Wiki / Game8 / fe3h.com / Reddit (EN), note.com / namu.wiki (JP/KR), GamerSky (CN).

Build 1 — Bow Knight (弓骑兵) VW pick

Path: Commoner → Soldier → Cavalier → Paladin → Bow Knight. Cert: Bow A (boon) + Riding A (boon) + Lance C (boon) — all on-strength. The strongest Bow Knight candidate among cavalry defaults [Fire Emblem Wiki]; her base growths carry the class's mediocre modifiers.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] already on the road — master it. Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — neutral] painless given no banes; +6 Str on every bow/lance initiation. Darting Blow [⛏ Pegasus Knight, Lance C — boon, female-OK] grab it before settling in.

5 skills: Bowfaire · Death Blow · Darting Blow · Hit+20 · Bowrange+1.

  • ✔ Mov 8 + Canto + Bowrange+2 — Cantos in, fires 1–3 range bow or melee lance, retreats; a mobile player-phase powerhouse.
  • ✔ Uses all three boons; her Spd/Dex make her accurate and dodgy.
  • ✘ Bow Knight's own growths are mediocre — relies on her base growths leading in.
  • ✘ No flight/evasion ceiling of the Falcon build.

Build 2 — Falcon Knight (天马骑士) AM pick

Path: Commoner → Soldier → Pegasus Knight → Falcon Knight (female-only). Cert: Lance A (boon) + Flying A (neutral, trains fine). Her huge Speed → a flying dodge-tank with Canto.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Darting Blow [⛏ Pegasus Knight, Lance C — boon] sits on the path — free, essential to double the fastest Maddening enemies. Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] on-boon accuracy passive.

5 skills: Lancefaire · Darting Blow · Hit+20 · Alert Stance+ · Avo+10.

  • ✔ Flight + Canto + dodge — her Speed makes a near-untouchable evasion flier.
  • ✔ Darting Blow lets her double the fastest foes; great low-stakes AM workhorse.
  • ✘ Lower raw output than Bow Knight's 1–3 range carry.
  • ✘ Loses bow range — pure lance melee on the flier frame.

Build 3 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主)

Path: Commoner → Fighter → Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Lance C (boon) / Axe A (neutral grind) / Flying A (neutral). She lacks an Axe boon so Axe A is a real grind, but her Str/Spd carry it [namu.wiki].

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — neutral] on the path already — harvest it. Darting Blow [⛏ Pegasus Knight, Lance C — boon] for the doubling enabler.

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Darting Blow · Avo+10 · Defiant Crit.

  • ✔ Best physical class + her stats = a fearless flying bruiser with Axefaire and Avo+10.
  • ✔ Distinct from her bow/lance builds if a route already has a Bow Knight.
  • ✘ Axe A is a neutral grind (her one non-boon weapon path).
  • ✘ Loses the 1–3 range utility her boons naturally give.
