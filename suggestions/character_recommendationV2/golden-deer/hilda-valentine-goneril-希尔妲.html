Hilda Valentine Goneril / 希尔妲 — Build Menu V2

Golden Deer house member (auto-joins Verdant Wind; recruitable elsewhere via Charm/Bow… but slow due to low motivation). Minor Crest of Goneril (chance to nullify enemy counter on combat arts); growths HP 50 / Str 45 / Spd 50 / Cha 50 — strong physical attacker. Advocate gives adjacent males Atk+3.
Proficiencies: Lance (boon), Axe (boon); Faith (bane), Heavy Armor (bane); Sword/Bow/Flying neutral. Budding talent: Heavy Armor → Seal Speed (converts the Heavy Armor bane into a boon if popped).
Harvest: Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] on-boon, free — pairs with her Crest counter-nullification to one-shot safely. Darting Blow [⛏ Pegasus Knight, Lance C + Flying D — Lance boon, female-OK] to double the fastest foes.
Sources: Game8 / GameWith / note.com (JP), GamerSky (CN), Fire Emblem Wiki (EN).

Build 1 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主) recommended

Path: Noble → Fighter → Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Axe A (boon) + Flying A (neutral, trains fine) + Lance C (boon) — no bane gate. The universal pick [Game8, GameWith JP, GamerSky CN, note.com JP].

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] sits on the path already — free, on-boon harvest; +6 Str lets her one-shot on initiation, and her Crest of Goneril nullifies the counter. Darting Blow [⛏ Pegasus Knight, Lance C — boon, female-OK] to double fast foes. Skip armor/Faith classes (both banes) unless you pop the Heavy Armor budding.

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Darting Blow · Avo+10 · Defiant Crit.

  • ✔ Best physical class — Mov 8 + Canto + Axefaire + Avo+10; her Str/Spd dive and KO.
  • ✔ Crest of Goneril + combat arts = safe counter-proof one-shots.
  • ✔ Both cert weapons are boon/neutral — no remedial tutoring through her banes.
  • ✘ Low motivation = slow training; concentrate tutoring on Axe/Flying only.
  • ✘ Needs Darting Blow to reliably double the fastest Maddening enemies.

Build 2 — Warrior (战士)

Path: Noble → Fighter → Brigand → Warrior. Cert: Axe A (boon) — on-strength, no flying needed. Foot axe powerhouse with high Str modifier + bow access [GameWith JP, GamerSky CN].

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] already on the path — free, on-boon. Darting Blow [⛏ Pegasus Knight, Lance C — boon] if you can spare the detour.

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Darting Blow · Hit+20 · Defiant Crit.

  • ✔ Highest Str modifier of her options + bow access for chip; cheapest build (one boon weapon).
  • ✔ Pick when you lack Flying training or don't want another flier.
  • ✘ Foot Mov 5 — no flight/Canto, falls behind on big Maddening maps.
  • ✘ Lower Avo than the wyvern frame.

Build 3 — Great Knight (重骑士)

Path: Noble → Soldier → Cavalier → Paladin → Great Knight. Cert: Lance/Axe (both boons) + Riding (neutral). A mounted lance/axe bruiser; Heavy Armor is innate to the class so her Heavy Armor bane is a non-issue for certification, though her low Heavy-Armor proficiency caps Def growth.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon] on-boon, free. Optionally pop her Heavy Armor budding → Seal Speed to convert that bane and tank harder.

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Movement+1 · Seal Speed · Defiant Crit.

  • ✔ Mounted bruiser using both her weapon boons; tankier than Wyvern Lord.
  • ✔ Seal Speed budding turns Heavy Armor into a boon if you commit.
  • ✘ Lower ceiling — Wyvern Lord beats it on mobility (flight) and Avo.
  • ✘ Heavy Armor is a base bane; needs the budding to grow Def properly.
