Annette Fantine Dominic / 安妮特 — Build Menu V2

Blue Lions. Auto-joins on Azure Moon; recruitable elsewhere (Magic 10 + Faith B). No budding talent. Minor Crest of Dominic (10% conserve attack-magic use). Personal: Perseverance / Rally Strength (adjacent ally Str+4). Magic growths (50% Mag / 50% Dex) plus a usable 30% Str and her Axe boon make her a unique hybrid mage/axe unit. Owns relic Crusher (大锤) on Azure Moon.
Proficiencies: Axe (boon), Reason (boon), Authority (boon); Bow (bane), HeavyArmor (bane); Sword/Lance/Brawl/Riding/Flying/Faith neutral.
Harvest note: Fiendish Blow (Reason boon) + Death Blow (Axe boon) are free harvests. Bow bane → NO Hit+20 harvest; Crusher's low Hit needs an Accuracy Ring instead.
Sources: fe3h.com / Game8 (EN), note.com / GameWith JP, GamerSky CN.

Build 1 — Warlock → Gremory (黑魔法师→格莫瑞) — female-only, fits

Path: Monk → Mage → Warlock → Gremory. Cert: Reason A (boon) + Faith for staff utility + Authority (boon) for Rally stacking. Highest Magic, doubles spell uses; female-only, fits.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon, on path] Magic+6 core nuke spike. Magic+2 [⛏ Monk] early. SKIP Hit+20 (Archer needs Bow C — Bow is her bane); use an Accuracy Ring for Crusher.

5 skills: Reason Prowess · Fiendish Blow · Magic+2 · Faith Prowess · Perseverance (Rally Str, always-on).

  • ✔ Max Magic + doubled spell uses (Bolganone/Fimbulvetr); Authority boon feeds her Rally Strength buffs.
  • ✔ Lowest investment — all certs are boons; Fiendish Blow harvested free.
  • ✘ Lower mobility than the flying build; no Lightning-Axe burst.
  • ✘ Crusher accuracy needs the Accuracy Ring (Hit+20 is bane-gated).

Build 2 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主, "flying Annette") — community alt

Path: Monk (master) → Mage (master) → Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Axe A (boon) + Flying A (neutral). Dart in, swing Lightning Axe (magic-scaling, one-shots many enemies), retreat with Canto.

Mastery detours (double boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] for magic-axe damage AND Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon, on path] for physical-axe damage — both boons. SKIP Hit+20 (Bow bane) — Accuracy Ring for Crusher/Bolt Axe.

5 skills: Axefaire · Fiendish Blow · Death Blow · Magic+2 · Perseverance (always-on).

  • ✔ 8-move flight + Canto + Lightning Axe (scales on Magic) = a mobile nuke that deletes Maddening enemies; Crusher/Bolt Axe synergy.
  • ✔ Both Reason-boon Fiendish Blow and Axe-boon Death Blow harvested for free.
  • ✘ Needs Flying A (neutral) and a long class path — heavier than Gremory.
  • ✘ Crusher's low accuracy; Hit+20 bane-gated → Accuracy Ring mandatory.

Build 3 — Dark Knight (暗黑骑士)

Path: Monk → Mage → Warlock → Dark Knight. Cert: Reason A (boon) + Riding A (neutral) + Lance C. Mounted mage with mobility + Reason [Game8 alt].

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] on path. SKIP Hit+20 (Bow bane).

5 skills: Reason Prowess · Fiendish Blow · Magic+2 · Lancefaire/Movement · Perseverance.

  • ✔ 7 move + Canto mounted caster — more reach than Gremory, no Flying grind.
  • ✘ Gremory out-magics it (no spell-use doubling) and Wyvern out-mobilizes it.
  • ✘ Riding A is neutral (off-boon); middling pick.
