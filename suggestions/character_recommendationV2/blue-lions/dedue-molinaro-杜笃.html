Dedue Molinaro / 杜笃 — Build Menu V2

Blue Lions — Dimitri's retainer. Locked unit on Azure Moon (leaves and returns in Part 2). No Crest, no budding talent. Personal: Staunch Shield (+4 Def on Wait). Pure-tank growths: 60% HP / 50% Str / 50% Def, very low 20% Spd (he rarely doubles → enemy-phase wall, not chaser).
Proficiencies: Lance (boon), Axe (boon), Brawl (boon), HeavyArmor (boon); Faith (bane), Riding (bane), Flying (bane); Sword/Bow/Reason/Authority neutral.
Sources: Game8 / GameWith JP / fe3h.com (EN), note.com JP, pegasusknight JP, GamerSky CN.

Build 1 — Fortress Knight (装甲骑士/堡垒)

Path: Commoner → Soldier → Armored Knight → Fortress Knight. Cert: HeavyArmor A (boon) + Lance/Axe (boon) — all on-strength, light tutoring.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): HP+5 [⛏ Commoner, his start]; Defense+2 [⛏ Soldier, Lance — boon] stacks his tank identity; Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon harvest] for the off-build offense if he ever swings on player phase. Quick Riposte from War Master is the strongest counter-tank add (see Build 3).

5 skills: Lancefaire/Axefaire · Defense+2 · Weight-3 · Quick Riposte · Armored Effect− (+ Staunch Shield always-on).

  • ✔ Walls physical threats with the game's top Def; HeavyArmor/Lance/Axe all boons → no bane friction.
  • ✔ Commands the unique Duscur Heavy Soldiers battalion (+10 protection) with Authority B.
  • ✘ Only 4–5 move, no Canto — falls behind a fast team.
  • ✘ 20% Spd = never doubles; pure enemy-phase tile-holder.

Build 2 — Warrior (战士)

Path: Fighter → Brigand → Warrior. Cert: Axe A (boon) — cheap. GameWith JP's mobility fix for Fortress's slow move.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon harvest] sits directly on the path → free, and core to this offensive build. Strength+2 [⛏ Fighter] early.

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Quick Riposte · Strength+2 · Crit+10 (+ Staunch Shield).

  • ✔ Death Blow (Axe boon) harvested for free + Axefaire = real player-phase damage; better move than Fortress.
  • ✔ Keeps his bulk respectable while letting him keep pace with the team.
  • ✘ Trades some of his signature Def/identity for offense.
  • ✘ Still low Spd; relies on Death Blow + Quick Riposte rather than doubling.

Build 3 — War Master (战神) — male-only, fits

Path: Brawler → Grappler → War Master. Cert: Axe A + Brawl A (both boons) — light. War Master is male-only, which fits Dedue.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon]; Quick Riposte [⛏ War Master mastery] = guaranteed enemy-phase follow-up at high HP — turns his counters into doubles, excellent even if he later returns to Fortress.

5 skills: Fistfaire · Crit+20 (War Master) · Death Blow · Quick Riposte · Tomebreaker.

  • ✔ Crit+20 + gauntlet multi-hits off his Brawl boon = a counter-attacking bruiser.
  • ✔ All certs are boons; Quick Riposte is the build's best portable skill.
  • ✘ Gives up his pure-tank identity; lower Def than Fortress.
  • ✘ Slower to set up than the lance-armored route on Normal.
