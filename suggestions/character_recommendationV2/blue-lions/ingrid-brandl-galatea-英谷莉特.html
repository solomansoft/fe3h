Ingrid Brandl Galatea / 英谷莉特 — Build Menu V2

Blue Lions. Auto-joins on Azure Moon; recruitable elsewhere (Dex 15 + Flying D). Minor Crest of Daphnel (10% Mt+5 on combat art). Personal: Lady Knight (gambits Mt+3, Hit+5). Best-in-house Speed 60% with high Lck/Res/Cha — a natural flier and dodge tank; modest 35% Str is her one weak stat. Owns relic Lúin (鲁纳) (lance).
Proficiencies: Sword (boon), Lance (boon), Riding (boon), Flying (boon); no bane — low tutoring friction; no budding talent.
Gender note: female → can take Pegasus/Falcon Knight and harvest Darting Blow (Speed+6, female-only). Sources: Game8 / fe3h.com / Reddit (EN), note.com / GameWith JP, GamerSky CN.

Build 1 — Falcon Knight (天马骑士/飞马) — female-only, fits

Path: Soldier → Pegasus Knight → Falcon Knight. Cert: Lance A (boon) + Flying A (boon) — all on-strength, light. "Unquestionably her best choice" [Game8].

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Darting Blow [⛏ Pegasus Knight, Lance C + Flying D — boon, female-only, on path] "vital" — Speed+6 turns her 60% Spd into guaranteed doubles. Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — neutral but cheap] offsets her modest Str so her doubles hit hard. Defense+2 [⛏ Soldier] early.

5 skills: Lancefaire · Darting Blow · Death Blow · Alert Stance+ · Hit+20.

  • ✔ 8-move flight + Canto + huge Spd/Res + Lúin = a doubling, dodging menace; one of the BL's best dodge tanks with Alert Stance+.
  • ✔ Darting Blow harvested on-path (female-only boon); no banes to fix anywhere.
  • ✘ Modest 35% Str — leans on Death Blow + doubles, not raw hits.
  • ✘ Lower Def than the wyvern; weak early bases (scales well, stick with her).

Build 2 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主)

Path: Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Lance C / Axe A (neutral — a real dip for her) / Flying A (boon). More Str + Def, still 8-move flight + Canto.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Darting Blow [⛏ Pegasus Knight, Lance C + Flying D — boon, female-only] harvest before leaving Pegasus; Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — neutral, on path] free on this route.

5 skills: Axefaire (or Lancefaire) · Darting Blow · Death Blow · Defiant Crit · Alert Stance+.

  • ✔ More Str/Def than Falcon → consistent doubling and durability under harder enemy stats.
  • ✔ Distinct class from her AM Falcon build (R3); Death Blow free on the path.
  • ✘ Needs Axe A (neutral, off-boon) — slightly more grind than Falcon.
  • ✘ Trades some of her signature Speed/avoid identity.

Build 3 — Magic Flier (Dark Flier / Valkyrie / Gremory) — niche

Path: Mage → Warlock → Dark Flier (all female-only). Cert: Reason from neutral — a heavy build-around. Uses her decent 35% Mag.

Mastery detours: Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — neutral] for magic damage; Darting Blow still possible via Pegasus dip.

5 skills: Tomefaire-adjacent · Fiendish Blow · Darting Blow · Uncanny Blow · Alert Stance+.

  • ✔ Flying + Canto magic damage; novel hybrid.
  • ✘ Underuses her elite physical/flying kit; Reason is neutral, heavy to raise.
  • ✘ Strictly a novelty vs Falcon/Wyvern.
