Ashe Ubert / 亚修 — Build Menu V2

Blue Lions. Auto-joins on Azure Moon; recruitable elsewhere (Charm 15 + Lance C). No Crest. Personal: Locktouch (free chest/door opening — removes the need for keys/Thief). Growths favor Dex 55% / Spd 50% / Lck 40% with modest Str 35% — a precise, evasive bow/utility unit. ⚠ If not recruited pre-timeskip he is an enemy on Crimson Flower.
Proficiencies: Axe (boon), Bow (boon); Reason (bane); budding Lance → Shatter Slash; Sword/Brawl/Riding/Flying/Faith/Authority/HeavyArmor neutral.
Harvest note: BOTH Death Blow (Axe boon) and Hit+20 (Bow boon) are cheap harvests for Ashe — grab both.
Sources: Game8 / fe3h.com (EN), note.com / pegasusknight JP, GamerSky CN.

Build 1 — Sniper (狙击手)

Path: Fighter → Archer → Sniper. Cert: Bow A (boon) — cheap. Hunter's Volley (Bow A) is a counter-proof 2-hit nuke and his best tool; high Dex = accuracy + crits.

Mastery detours (double boon-harvest): Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon, on path] AND Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon harvest] — both are boons for Ashe, so harvest both early to fix his modest Str. Strength+2 [⛏ Fighter] on the way.

5 skills: Bowfaire · Hit+20 · Death Blow · Bow Crit+10 · Close Counter.

  • ✔ Hunter's Volley + Death Blow + Hit+20 (both boon-harvested) turns his Dex/Spd into a reliable ranged nuke despite low Str.
  • ✔ Keeps Locktouch utility from the back row — safe chip + free treasure.
  • ✘ 5 move, no Canto — repositions slowly.
  • ✘ Low Str base means he leans on Death Blow/crits rather than raw damage.

Build 2 — Bow Knight (弓骑士)

Path: Fighter → Archer → Sniper (master) → Bow Knight. Cert: Bow A (boon) + Riding A (neutral — a real dip). Game8's pick for his Lck/Dex.

Mastery detours (double boon-harvest): Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] + Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon], same as Sniper.

5 skills: Bowfaire · Hit+20 · Death Blow · Bow Crit+10 · Movement (Canto).

  • ✔ 8 move + Canto + 1–3 range fixes Sniper's mobility while keeping the bow focus.
  • ✔ Both mastery boons still harvested.
  • ✘ Needs Riding A (neutral, off-boon) — extra tutoring.
  • ✘ Bow Knight has weaker growths than Sniper [pegasusknight JP]; many keep him a Sniper on Normal.

Build 3 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主)

Path: Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Lance C (budding) / Axe A (boon) / Flying A (neutral). Axefaire uses his Axe boon.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Death Blow [⛏ Brigand, Axe C — boon, on path] free; Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon] grab before switching off bows.

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Hit+20 · Defiant Crit · Alert Stance+.

  • ✔ 8 move + flight + Canto, Axefaire on his Axe boon — the highest-mobility ceiling.
  • ✔ Still harvests both boon masteries.
  • ✘ His low Str growth means less axe payoff than on Sylvain/Felix.
  • ✘ Needs Flying A (neutral) and drops Hunter's Volley/Locktouch positioning.
