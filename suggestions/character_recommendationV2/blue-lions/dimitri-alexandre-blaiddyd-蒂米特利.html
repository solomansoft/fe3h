Dimitri Alexandre Blaiddyd / 蒂米特利 — Build Menu V2

Blue Lions house leader. Locked unit on Azure Moon — you keep him the whole game, worth heavy investment. Major Crest of Blaiddyd (Combat Art crit-double). Personal: Royal Lineage (+20% EXP; +20 Avoid at full HP in Part 2). 60% Str / 50% Spd / 55% HP growth — widely rated the single strongest unit in the game.
Proficiencies: Lance (boon), Sword (boon), Authority (boon); Axe (bane), Reason (bane); budding Riding → Seal Movement; Brawl/Bow/Faith/HeavyArmor/Flying neutral.
Bane note: Axe bane means NO Death Blow harvest and no cheap Wyvern Lord / War Master (both need Axe A). Stay on the Lance/Riding path.
Sources: Game8 / fe3h.com / GameFAQs (EN), note.com / GameWith JP, GamerSky CN.

Build 1 — Great Lord (大領主)

Story class line (auto): Dimitri is story-granted his unique line — Lord (intermediate) → High Lord (advanced, Part 1) → Great Lord (master, auto at the start of Ch.16). No exam, no Lance/Authority gate at any step.

Gameplay path: override the middle of that line with Soldier → Cavalier → Paladin for better stats + Canto before Ch.16 (Lord/High Lord's own class stats lag Paladin), then let Great Lord auto-arrive. Either way you never certify — so raise Lance (combat arts) and Authority C (Battalion Wrath) as skills, not for a class gate. Push Lance S+ for Lancefaire.

Mastery detour: Vantage [⛏ Mercenary, Sword C — boon] — the one detour worth taking, since it powers his signature Battalion Wrath + Vantage + Retribution enemy-phase loop that the community calls "broken." SKIP Death Blow — Brigand needs Axe C and Axe is his bane.

5 skills (matches roster): Battalion Wrath · Battalion Vantage · Vantage · Atrocity (signature art) · Defiant Crit — his corroborated enemy-phase build (Authority boon). (Lancefaire is innate to Great Lord — no slot.) Normal QoL swap: drop a defensive slot for Movement +1 / Seal Movement (cheap via his Riding budding).

  • ✔ Top stat bonuses on his signature class; uses Lance + Authority boons, zero bane grind.
  • ✔ Areadbhar + the Battalion Wrath + Battalion Vantage + Retribution enemy-phase combo is the game's strongest loop.
  • ✘ Great Lord has no Canto; 6 move — slightly stiff repositioning.
  • ✘ Wants Authority + Retribution online (~Ch.8) to fully come alive.

Build 2 — Paladin (圣骑士)

Path: Soldier → Cavalier → Paladin (terminal). Cert: Lance A (boon) + Riding B+ (budding) — cheap, on-boon.

Mastery detours: Defense+2 [⛏ Soldier, Lance — boon]; Vantage [⛏ Mercenary, Sword C — boon]. SKIP Death Blow (Axe bane).

5 skills: Lancefaire · Battalion Wrath · Battalion Vantage · Vantage · Movement (Seal Movement / Canto).

  • ✔ 8 move + Canto fixes Great Lord's mobility on the rare big map; same lance-boon kit.
  • ✔ Lowest-effort strong build; Seal Movement from his Riding budding adds control.
  • ✘ Slightly lower stat bonuses than Great Lord; gives up the unique class.
  • ✘ Same Vantage/Wrath dependency to peak.

Build 3 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主) — community ceiling, bane-gated

Path: Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Lance C / Axe A (BANE) / Flying A (neutral) — Axe A from a bane is a heavy, slow grind.

Mastery detours: SKIP Death Blow — even though Brigand sits on this path, Axe is his bane; the mastery is bane-gated, so don't treat it as a free harvest. Take Defiant Crit from the wyvern line instead.

5 skills: Axefaire · Defiant Crit · Battalion Wrath · Battalion Vantage · Alert Stance+.

  • ✔ 8 move + flight + Canto — the community's highest-mobility pick; his huge Str makes a fearless KO flier.
  • ✘ Axe A is his bane (plus Flying A) — heavy tutoring vs the free Lance route.
  • ✘ Drops Areadbhar/Lancefaire synergy. Not worth it on Normal where the Great Lord already over-performs.
