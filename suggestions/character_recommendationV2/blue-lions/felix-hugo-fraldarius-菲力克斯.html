Felix Hugo Fraldarius / 菲力克斯 — Build Menu V2

Blue Lions. Auto-joins on Azure Moon; recruitable elsewhere (Spd 15 + Sword B+). Major Crest of Fraldarius (40% Mt+5). Personal: Lone Wolf (+5 dmg with no battalion). Elite combat growths: 55% Str / 55% Spd — one of the best player-phase damage units in the game.
Proficiencies: Sword (boon), Bow (boon), Brawl (boon); Faith (bane); budding Reason → Black Magic Crit+10; Lance/Axe/Riding/Flying/Authority/HeavyArmor neutral.
Harvest note: Bow is a boon → Hit+20 is a cheap harvest. Death Blow (Axe C) is neutral, not a boon — grab it only if convenient.
Sources: Game8 / fe3h.com / Reddit (EN), note.com / GameWith / mark-lxiii JP, GamerSky CN.

Build 1 — Swordmaster (剑圣)

Path: Myrmidon → Mercenary → Swordmaster. Cert: Sword A (boon) — cheap; push Sword S for Swordfaire. Sword Crit+10 stacks with his Crest for relentless crits.

Mastery detours: Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon harvest] secures crit-fishing accuracy — free on his Bow boon. Death Blow [Brigand, Axe C — neutral] optional extra player-phase damage (not a boon, but cheap). Speed+2 [⛏ Myrmidon, his start].

5 skills: Swordfaire · Sword Crit+10 · Hit+20 · Death Blow · Sword Avo+20.

  • ✔ His natural class — Sword Aptitude + Sword Crit+10 + 40% Crest = constant crits, almost no investment.
  • ✔ +4 Spd class bonus = reliable doubles; Lone Wolf keeps him strong battalion-less.
  • ✘ Only 5 move, no Canto/flight.
  • ✘ Slightly lower ceiling than War Master's gauntlet quads.

Build 2 — War Master (战神) — male-only, fits

Path: Myrmidon → Mercenary → Brigand/Archer (master) → Grappler → War Master. Cert: Axe A (neutral) + Brawl A (boon). Male-only — fine for Felix.

Mastery detours: Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon harvest]; Death Blow [Brigand, Axe C — neutral, cheap]; Quick Riposte [⛏ War Master mastery]. Gauntlets exploit his 40% Crest.

5 skills: Fistfaire · Crit+20 (War Master) · Death Blow · Hit+20 · Quick Riposte.

  • ✔ Highest ceiling — gauntlet quads + Crit+20 + Crest is monstrous burst.
  • ✔ Brawl boon makes the path cheap; Quick Riposte adds enemy-phase doubles.
  • ✘ Axe A is neutral (not a boon) — a bit more grind than staying Swordmaster.
  • ✘ Overkill on Normal where Swordmaster already shreds.

Build 3 — Sniper / Bow Knight (狙击手/弓骑)

Path: Archer → Sniper → Bow Knight. Cert: Bow A (boon) + Riding A (neutral) for Bow Knight; stop at Sniper if you skip Riding.

Mastery detours: Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon harvest] sits on the path. Hunter's Volley (Bow A) is a counter-proof 2-hit nuke.

5 skills: Bowfaire · Hunter's Volley enabler · Hit+20 · Bow Crit+10 · Close Counter.

  • ✔ Ranged safety on his Bow boon; Hunter's Volley + his Str/Dex = a precise nuke.
  • ✔ Bow Knight adds 1–3 range + mobility + Canto.
  • ✘ Underuses his elite melee Str; raw output below his sword/gauntlet builds.
  • ✘ Bow Knight wants Riding A (neutral) — extra dip; many keep him a Sniper.

Build 4 — Hero (勇者) — male-only, Advanced experience pick

Path: Myrmidon → Mercenary → (Brigand for Axe) → Hero. Cert: Sword A (boon, he has it) + Axe to cert rank (neutral — a small dip). Male-only, so Felix qualifies.

Mastery detours: Vantage [⛏ Hero mastery] — attack first on enemy phase at <50% HP, deadly with his Crit+Crest. Hit+20 [⛏ Archer, Bow C — boon harvest]; Death Blow [Brigand, Axe C — on the way to the Axe cert].

5 skills: Swordfaire · Vantage · Sword Crit+10 · Hit+20 · Death Blow.

  • ✔ Sword + Axe flexibility and more HP/bulk than Swordmaster; Vantage + his crit/Crest make a self-sufficient enemy-phase dueler.
  • ✔ Uses his Sword boon; the Axe dip doubles as the Brigand→Death Blow harvest.
  • ✘ Advanced (not Master) — lower stat caps than War Master; Axe is neutral (small grind).
  • ✘ A side-grade to Swordmaster on raw numbers — chosen here to experience the class, not to min-max.
