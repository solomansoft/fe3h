Mercedes von Martritz / 梅尔赛德丝 — Build Menu V2

Blue Lions. Auto-joins on Azure Moon; recruitable elsewhere (Magic 15 + Bow C). Minor Crest of Lamine (conserves healing uses). Personal: Live to Serve (self-heals equal to HP healed on allies) — one of the best healer personals in the game. Magic-focused growths (50% Mag / 45% Res / 45% Dex), low Spd (rarely doubles → keep her behind the line).
Proficiencies: Reason (boon), Faith (boon); Sword/Lance/Axe/HeavyArmor (all bane); budding Bow → Waning Shot; Brawl/Bow/Riding/Flying/Authority neutral.
Build note: pure caster — every physical-weapon class is gated behind a bane, so never melee her. Fiendish Blow (Reason boon) is her one cheap harvest.
Sources: Game8 / fe3h.com (EN), note.com / GameWith JP, GamerSky CN.

Build 1 — Bishop → Gremory (主教→格莫瑞) — female-only, fits

Path: Monk → Priest → Bishop → Gremory. Cert: Reason A (boon) + Faith A (boon) — both her strengths, easy. Gremory doubles spell/staff uses (huge for Live to Serve + Reason offense); female-only, fits.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon harvest] Magic+6 on initiate, her core nuke spike. Magic+2 [⛏ Monk, her start]; White Magic Heal+5 [⛏ Priest, on path] amplifies Live to Serve. No physical-class detours — all are bane-gated.

5 skills: Reason Prowess · Faith Prowess · Fiendish Blow · Magic+2 · Live to Serve (always-on).

  • ✔ Best ceiling — doubled spell/staff uses means more Physic/Fortify + more Fimbulvetr/Bolganone nukes per map.
  • ✔ All certs are boons; Fiendish Blow harvested free; Live to Serve self-sustains her.
  • ✘ Very low Spd; never doubles, gets doubled — keep her screened.
  • ✘ Needs both Reason A + Faith A (still easy, but two ranks).

Build 2 — Bishop (主教, terminal)

Path: Monk → Priest → Bishop. Cert: Faith A (boon) only — cheapest. The dedicated healer; learns Fortify + long-range Physic.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Magic+2 [⛏ Monk]; White Magic Heal+5 [⛏ Priest, on path] amplifies Live to Serve. Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon] optional if you also want some offense.

5 skills: Faith Prowess · White Magic Heal+5 · Magic+2 · White Magic Avo+30 · Live to Serve (always-on).

  • ✔ Lowest investment medic — Faith A only; Fortify/Physic + Live to Serve sustains the whole team.
  • ✔ Higher White Magic range and Avo than Gremory.
  • ✘ Lower offense than Gremory; no spell-use doubling.
  • ✘ Same low Spd / screen-her caveat.

Build 3 — Warlock → Gremory (黑魔法师→格莫瑞)

Path: Monk → Mage → Warlock → Gremory. Cert: Reason A (boon) leaning; same endpoint as Build 1 but nuke-first.

Mastery detours (boon-harvest): Fiendish Blow [⛏ Mage, Reason C — boon, on path] free; Magic+2 [⛏ Monk].

5 skills: Reason Prowess · Fiendish Blow · Magic+2 · Faith Prowess · Live to Serve.

  • ✔ Emphasizes her good Reason for nuking; ends at the same spell-doubling Gremory.
  • ✘ Slightly less healing-forward than the Bishop route on the way up.
  • ✘ Same low Spd; pure-caster banes still apply.
