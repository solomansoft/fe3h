## Rules digest
- **A2.1 boon/bane + path-harvest:** route the class PATH through mastery classes on the unit's BOON/neutral weapons and HARVEST those masteries (Brigand=Axe C→Death Blow, Archer=Bow C→Hit+20, Mage=Reason C→Fiendish Blow, Pegasus=Lance C+Flying D→Darting Blow [FEMALE-ONLY]). DROP any mastery gated behind a BANE weapon unless that weapon is the unit's budding talent. Harvest early (Lv10–20).
- **Gender locks:** male-only = Hero, Brawler, Grappler, War Master. female-only = Pegasus Knight, Falcon Knight, Valkyrie, Dark Flier, Gremory. (Males cannot get Darting Blow.)
- **R3:** reused units use a DIFFERENT class each route. **R7:** roster must fit ~12 max deployment (train 12–13). **R9:** route pick is team-driven (composition). **R11:** don't grind an off-build skill on Byleth just to recruit; use support/passive stats or skip. **R12:** Normal=lean kit, Hard=fuller, Maddening=full optimal.
