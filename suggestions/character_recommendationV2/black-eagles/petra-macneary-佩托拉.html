Petra Macneary / 佩托拉 — Build Menu V2

Black Eagles student. Stays on Crimson Flower; auto-joins Silver Snow; recruitable elsewhere (Dexterity 10 + Riding C). No Crest, Spd 60% (top in house) / Dex 50% / Lck 45% / Str 40% growths, personal Hunter's Boon (Crit +20 vs foes at ≤50% HP).
Proficiencies: Sword (boon), Axe (boon), Bow (boon), Riding (boon); Reason (BANE), Faith (BANE); no budding talent. Lance/Brawl/Authority/Heavy Armor/Flying neutral.
Detour note: HARVEST Death Blow (Axe boon) + Hit +20 (Bow boon) — both off her boons, cheap, and Death Blow fixes her one weak stat (Str). Darting Blow is Pegasus Knight mastery (female — she can grab it), not Wyvern Rider's.
Sources: Game8 / GameFAQs / fe3h.com (EN), note.com (JP), GamerSky (CN).

Build 1 — Dancer (舞者)

Path: base combat class → win the White Heron Cup → Dancer. Cert: light — the contest only. She is fast/Charming enough to win it and keeps strong personal combat.

Mastery detours: Death Blow [⛏ HARVEST — Brigand, Axe boon] and Hit +20 [⛏ HARVEST — Archer, Bow boon] so her dance-turn attacks still hit hard. Steal [⛏ Thief, Sword C — Sword boon] — with the highest Speed in the cast she steals items from nearly any foe (Steal works on lower-Spd targets); a cheap utility detour and this playthrough's Thief coverage.

5 skills: Sword Avo +20 (Dancer) · Death Blow · Hit +20 · Hunter's Boon (personal) · Sword Crit +10.

  • ✔ Extra ally turn for Edelgard/a carry, while her own Speed + Hunter's Boon keep her a real combat threat.
  • ✔ Best of both worlds: utility body that can still crit-finish.
  • ✘ Move 6, no flight; gives up the Wyvern mobility ceiling.
  • ✘ One Dancer slot — competes with Dorothea on CF.

Build 2 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主)

Path: Commoner → Fighter → Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Axe A (boon) + Flying A (neutral). Move 8 flight + her elite Speed = a fearless dodge-flier — her combat #1 by broad consensus.

Mastery detours: Death Blow [⛏ HARVEST — Brigand, Axe boon] patches her Str, on path. Hit +20 [⛏ HARVEST — Archer, Bow boon]. Darting Blow [⛏ Pegasus Knight, female] if you want extra doubling.

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Hit +20 · Hunter's Boon (personal) · Alert Stance+.

  • ✔ Move 8 + flight + Canto; Spd 60% + Alert Stance+ makes her a premier dodge-flier.
  • ✔ Death Blow + Hunter's Boon crit turns her into a flying boss-killer.
  • ✘ Flying A tutor (neutral); gives up the Dancer team utility.
  • ✘ Dodge reliance — risky on bad RNG.

Build 3 — Assassin (刺客, stepping stone)

Path: Commoner → Myrmidon → Mercenary → Assassin. Cert: Sword + Bow — both boons, trivial to reach. Move 6, Stealth, bow access; best as a master-then-leave step before flying.

Mastery detours: Vantage [⛏ Mercenary, on path]; Speed +2 [⛏ Myrmidon]. (Then transition to Wyvern Lord.)

5 skills: Swordfaire · Sword Avo +20 · Vantage · Hunter's Boon (personal) · Lethality.

  • ✔ Game8's listed pick; Sword/Bow boons make it free, with Stealth + Lethality procs.
  • ✔ Great early flex while you build toward the wyvern.
  • ✘ Redundant Speed (she's already fastest); no flight.
  • ✘ Lower ceiling than Wyvern Lord — use it as a stepping stone.
