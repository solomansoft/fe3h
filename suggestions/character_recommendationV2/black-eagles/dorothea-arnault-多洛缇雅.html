Dorothea Arnault / 多洛缇雅 — Build Menu V2

Black Eagles student. Stays on Crimson Flower; auto-joins Silver Snow; recruitable to other houses (Charm 25 + Authority B). No Crest, Mag 40% / Cha 40% / Dex 45% / Spd 40% growths, personal Songstress (adjacent allies heal 10% each turn).
Proficiencies: Sword (boon), Reason (boon); Faith (BANE), Riding (BANE), Flying (BANE); budding talent Faith (White Magic Avo +20).
Faith bane note: Faith is a BANE for her — but it is also her budding talent, so once it awakens the cost drops and the payoff (Physic + White Magic Avo +20 + Gremory's double uses) justifies the Gremory grind. HARVEST Fiendish Blow (Reason boon) for her offense.
Sources: Game8 / GameFAQs / fe3h.com (EN), GameWith (JP), GamerSky (CN).

Build 1 — Dancer (舞者)

Path: base caster → win the White Heron Cup → Dancer. Cert: light — just the dance contest; her weak offense stops mattering since Dancers don't need to fight.

Mastery detours: Fiendish Blow [⛏ HARVEST — Mage, Reason C boon] so she can still throw Meteor/gambits. Magic +2 [⛏ Monk] cheap.

5 skills: Sword Avo +20 (Dancer) · Fiendish Blow · Songstress (personal) · Magic +2 · Authority/Battalion skill.

  • ✔ Grants an extra ally turn — lets your ace act twice; GamerSky calls her "the strongest support in Black Eagles."
  • ✔ Keeps the Songstress 10% heal aura; can chip with Meteor/gambits.
  • ✘ Personal combat is negligible — purely a support body.
  • ✘ One-per-army slot; competes with other Dancer candidates.

Build 2 — Gremory (女祭司) female-only

Path: Noble → Monk → Mage → Warlock → Gremory. Cert: Reason A (boon) + Faith A (BANE, but her budding talent → awaken to cut the cost). Gremory doubles all spell uses (offense AND Physic) and boosts Mag.

Mastery detours: Fiendish Blow [⛏ HARVEST — Mage, Reason C boon] on her path. Magic +2 [⛏ Monk]. White Magic Heal +5 [Priest] if dipping the white track.

5 skills: Black Tomefaire · Fiendish Blow · White Magic Avo +20 (budding) · Songstress (personal) · Black Magic Range +1.

  • ✔ Double Meteor + double Physic + Mag boost; her budding White Magic Avo +20 makes her an evasive hybrid.
  • ✔ Songstress heal aura on top of a real damage mage.
  • ✘ Faith A is a bane grind — only worth it because Faith is her budding talent (awaken first).
  • ✘ Move 4–5, no Canto; modest Mag growth (40%).

Build 3 — Mortal Savant (魔剑士) experience pick

Path: Monk → Mage → (Myrmidon/Mercenary for Sword) → Mortal Savant. Cert: Sword A + Reason B+ — both her boons, so the class that is a trap on most units is genuinely cheap for Dorothea. Master tier.

Mastery detours: Fiendish Blow [⛏ HARVEST — Mage, Reason C boon] on her path; Magic +2 [⛏ Monk].

5 skills: Black Tomefaire · Fiendish Blow · Sword Avo +20 · White Magic Avo +20 (budding) · Hit +20.

  • ✔ Sword + black magic in one body at 1–2 range, built entirely on her two boons — the rare unit Mortal Savant actually fits.
  • ✔ Fun melee-mage hybrid that still chips with Thoron / Meteor.
  • ✘ Low Spd — rarely doubles (Mortal Savant's signature flaw); no Canto.
  • ✘ Fewer casts and less raw power than Gremory — chosen to experience the class on a unit who can run it well.
