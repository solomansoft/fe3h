Edelgard von Hresvelg / 艾黛尔贾特 — Build Menu V2

Black Eagles lord — locked to the house, never recruitable. Leads Crimson Flower; enemy on the other three routes. Minor Crest of Seiros, growths Str 55% / Mag 45% / Cha 60%, personal Imperial Lineage (1.2× EXP). Unique class line Armored Lord → Emperor; relic axe Aymr (Raging Storm extra action).
Proficiencies: Sword (boon), Axe (boon), Authority (boon), Heavy Armor (boon); Bow (BANE), Faith (BANE); budding talent Reason; Lance/Brawl/Riding/Flying neutral.
Bow bane correction: the old guide casually listed Hit +20 — but Archer/Bow is a BANE for her, so the Archer detour is too expensive. Drop Hit +20; replace with Defiant Crit or Armored Effectiveness − from her own Armor line. HARVEST Death Blow instead — Axe is a boon and Brigand sits on her path.
Sources: Game8 / GameFAQs / fe3h.com (EN), GameWith / note.com (JP), GamerSky (CN).

Build 1 — Emperor (帝王)

Path: Noble → Fighter → Brigand → Armored Lord → Emperor (her exclusive line). Cert: Axe + Heavy Armor + Authority — all boons, so this is a light investment for her signature class.

Mastery detours: Death Blow [⛏ HARVEST — Brigand, Axe C boon] sits directly on the path; +6 Str on initiate stacks with Raging Storm. Strength +2 [⛏ Fighter] cheap early grab. SKIP Hit +20 — Archer/Bow is her BANE; use her own Armored Effectiveness − / Defiant Crit instead.

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Defiant Crit · Armored Effectiveness − · Battalion Wrath.

  • ✔ Aymr + Raging Storm = extra action after a KO; one of the best player-phase combat arts in the game.
  • ✔ Huge Str + Def from the Armor line; her Charm 60% makes Authority gambits hit hard.
  • ✘ Armored line = low Move, no Canto; relies on Aymr to shine (JP note.com only A-tier without it).
  • ✘ Bow/Faith are dead weapons — no Hit +20 fix for armored accuracy.

Build 2 — Wyvern Lord (飞龙领主)

Path: Noble → Fighter → Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord. Cert: Axe A (boon) + Flying A (neutral — tutor) + Lance C. Multiple sources call her "one of the best Wyvern Lord units in the game."

Mastery detours: Death Blow [⛏ HARVEST — Brigand, Axe C boon] is already on the path. SKIP Hit +20 (Bow bane).

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Defiant Crit · Alert Stance+ · Battalion Wrath.

  • ✔ Move 8 + flight + Canto turns her Str growth into a mobile wrecking ball.
  • ✔ Solves the armored mobility problem the community flags for Maddening.
  • ✘ Tutoring Flying A from neutral; drops Aymr/Raging Storm synergy (Armor-line exclusive).
  • ✘ Loses the unique-lord identity that is her whole appeal on CF.

Build 3 — Fortress Knight (铁壁骑士)

Path: Noble → Armored Knight → Fortress Knight. Cert: Heavy Armor — her boon, so a light wall build.

Mastery detours: Death Blow [⛏ HARVEST — Brigand, Axe C boon] for the rare offensive turn. SKIP Hit +20 (Bow bane).

5 skills: Axefaire · Death Blow · Armored Effectiveness − · Defiant Crit · Weight −3.

  • ✔ Leans on her Heavy-Armor boon for a near-unkillable choke-point wall.
  • ✔ Cheap; Altema rates an armored Edelgard a "strong final candidate."
  • ✘ Move 4, no Canto; the worst mobility of her three options.
  • ✘ Gives up Aymr/Raging Storm — strictly a durability pick, not a carry.
