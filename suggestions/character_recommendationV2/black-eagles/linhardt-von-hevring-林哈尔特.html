Linhardt von Hevring / 林哈尔特 — Build Menu V2

Black Eagles student. Stays on Crimson Flower; auto-joins Silver Snow; recruitable elsewhere (Magic 10 + Reason C). Minor Crest of Cethleann (occasional +5 healing Might), Mag 45% / Res 45% growths, personal Catnap (heal 10% HP on Wait). One of the game's best Warp holders.
Proficiencies: Reason (boon), Faith (boon); no bane, no budding talent. All weapons neutral.
Gender lock: Gremory is female-only — Linhardt is male, so it is impossible (the old guide caught this). His male caster terminals are Bishop (white) and Warlock (black).
Sources: Game8 / GameFAQs / fe3h.com (EN), GameWith (JP), GamerSky (CN).

Build 1 — Bishop (主教)

Path: Noble → Monk → Priest → Bishop. Cert: Faith — his boon, so a light path. Bishop maximizes white-magic uses, has Heal +10, and suits his Catnap-rewards-waiting rear-line identity. His Crest boosts healing.

Mastery detours: White Magic Heal +5 [⛏ Priest, Faith — on path] stacks +5 healing. Magic +2 [⛏ Monk] cheap early. (Gremory is female-only — not an option; Bishop is his terminal.)

5 skills: White Magic Heal +5 · White Tomefaire · Magic +2 · Live to Serve · Renewal.

  • ✔ Premier Warp + Physic + Fortify utility — top-class even at "D-tier" combat.
  • ✔ Heal +10 + Crest + Catnap = a self-sufficient back-line support.
  • ✘ Minimal offense; a support specialist, not a carry (note.com D-tier combat).
  • ✘ Move 5, no Canto — needs careful positioning.

Build 2 — Warlock (黑魔导师)

Path: Noble → Monk → Mage → Warlock. Cert: Reason — his boon; a light offensive-caster line that deals damage between heals (he keeps Faith on the side).

Mastery detours: Fiendish Blow [⛏ HARVEST — Mage, Reason C boon] +6 Mag on initiate, on his path. Magic +2 [⛏ Monk]. Poison Strike [⛏ Dark Mage] if dipping for chip.

5 skills: Black Tomefaire · Fiendish Blow · Magic +2 · Bowbreaker · Battalion Vantage.

  • ✔ Black Tomefaire + Fiendish Blow gives him real Bolganone/Sagittae output.
  • ✔ Reason boon means cheap reach; can still throw the occasional Physic.
  • ✘ Trades his best-in-class Warp utility for mediocre raw damage.
  • ✘ Move 5, no Canto; his offense never rivals a dedicated nuke.
