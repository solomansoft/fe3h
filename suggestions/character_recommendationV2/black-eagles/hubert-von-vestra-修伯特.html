Hubert von Vestra / 修伯特 — Build Menu V2

Black Eagles retainer — locked to the house, never recruitable. Auto-stays on Crimson Flower; enemy elsewhere. No Crest, Mag 55% growth (highest in Black Eagles), Spd 45%, personal Officer Duty (gambit Might +5). His spell list is mostly dark magic (Mire, Banshee, Death Γ).
Proficiencies: Bow (boon), Reason (boon), Authority (boon); Axe (BANE), Faith (BANE), Flying (BANE); budding talent Lance (Frozen Lance).
Class correction: the old guide floated Warlock — but Warlock's Black Tomefaire does NOT boost his dark spells, so it is a downgrade. Never Warlock. Build Dark Knight (only class with Dark Tomefaire). HARVEST Fiendish Blow (Reason boon) and Hit +20 (Bow boon — both his strengths, cheap).
Sources: Game8 / GameFAQs / fe3h.com (EN), GameWith / Pegasusknight (JP), GamerSky (CN).

Build 1 — Dark Knight (暗黑骑士)

Path: Noble → Monk → Mage → Dark Bishop → Dark Knight. Cert: Reason A (boon) + Riding A (neutral — his bottleneck, tutor it) + Lance C (budding). Dark Knight is the only class with Dark Tomefaire, which matches his dark spell list.

Mastery detours: Fiendish Blow [⛏ HARVEST — Mage, Reason C boon] +6 Mag on initiate, on his path anyway. Hit +20 [⛏ HARVEST — Archer, Bow C boon] — Bow is a boon, so this is cheap and fixes long-range accuracy on Mire. Poison Strike [⛏ Dark Mage] chip; Lifetaker [Dark Bishop, on path].

5 skills: Dark Tomefaire · Fiendish Blow · Hit +20 · Lifetaker · Poison Strike.

  • ✔ Only class that buffs his dark spells; Move 7 + Canto + Frozen Lance melee option.
  • ✔ Highest BE Mag growth + Officer Duty makes him a top gambit-debuffer.
  • ✘ Riding A grind is his one friction point (neutral weapon, must tutor).
  • ✘ No flight (Flying is a BANE anyway); fragile if caught in melee.

Build 2 — Dark Bishop (暗黑主教, terminal)

Path: Noble → Monk → Mage → Dark Bishop (stay). Cert: Reason — his boon, so a light pure-caster line with no Riding grind.

Mastery detours: Fiendish Blow [⛏ HARVEST — Mage, Reason C boon] on path. Hit +20 [⛏ HARVEST — Archer, Bow C boon] cheap accuracy. Poison Strike [⛏ Dark Mage].

5 skills: Fiendish Blow · Hit +20 · Poison Strike · Lifetaker · Battalion Vantage.

  • ✔ Extra dark-spell uses; no Riding tutoring required.
  • ✔ Keeps his whole dark kit working with cheap boon detours.
  • ✘ Move 5, no Canto — loses Dark Knight's mobility and Frozen Lance.
  • ✘ Strictly a fallback if you refuse the Riding grind.
