Bernadetta von Varley / 贝尔娜提塔 — Build Menu V2

Black Eagles student. Stays on Crimson Flower; auto-joins Silver Snow; recruitable elsewhere (Strength 20 + Bow C). Minor Crest of Indech, Dex 55% / Spd 50% growths, Str 35%, personal Persecution Complex (+5 damage when not at full HP).
Proficiencies: Bow (boon), Reason (boon), Faith (boon); Axe (BANE), Authority (BANE); budding talent Riding (Pass).
Axe bane correction: the old guide listed Death Blow — but Brigand/Axe is a BANE for her, so that detour is too costly. Drop Death Blow. Stay pure bow and patch Str with Hit +20 (Bow boon) + her Vengeance / Persecution Complex tech instead.
Sources: GameWith (JP), Game8 / GameFAQs / fe3h.com (EN), GamerSky (CN).

Build 1 — Sniper (狙击手)

Path: Noble → Archer → Sniper. Cert: Bow — her boon, a light path. Sniper's Hunter's Volley double-hit + her Dex/Spd makes a reliable one-rounder; range covers her low Str.

Mastery detours: Hit +20 [⛏ HARVEST — Archer, Bow C boon] on her path already — locks accuracy. Vantage [⛏ Mercenary] for the enemy-phase variant. SKIP Death Blow (Brigand/Axe is her BANE).

5 skills: Bowfaire · Hit +20 · Hunter's Volley (combat art) · Persecution Complex (personal) · Bowrange +1.

  • ✔ Hunter's Volley + Dex/Spd = a clean, low-investment one-rounder.
  • ✔ Bow range keeps her fragile body safe; pure-boon build, no bane grind.
  • ✘ Move 5, no Canto; her 35% Str leans on range/Persecution rather than raw power.
  • ✘ Frail — needs a tank to draw aggro.

Build 2 — Bow Knight (弓骑兵)

Path: Noble → Archer → … → Bow Knight. Cert: Bow A (boon) + Riding A (budding talent — leaning in also unlocks Pass) + Lance C. Move 8 + Canto + Bow Range +2.

Mastery detours: Hit +20 [⛏ HARVEST — Archer, Bow C boon] on path. SKIP Death Blow (Axe bane).

5 skills: Bowfaire · Hit +20 · Bow Range +2 (class) · Pass (budding) · Persecution Complex (personal).

  • ✔ 1–3 range counterattacks anywhere, Move 8 + Canto, and Pass to slip lines.
  • ✔ Best endgame mobility for her; Riding doubles as her budding talent.
  • ✘ Riding A is a real time cost (budding, not a boon).
  • ✘ Still frail; positioning-dependent.

Build 3 — Vengeance bruiser (报仇 流派)

Path: Sniper / Bow Knight base, kept slightly damaged. Cert: Bow (boon); the build is about skills, not a new class.

Mastery detours: Hit +20 [⛏ HARVEST — Archer, Bow boon]; Vantage [⛏ Mercenary] for enemy phase. SKIP Death Blow (Axe bane).

5 skills: Bowfaire · Hit +20 · Vantage · Persecution Complex (personal) · Vengeance (combat art).

  • ✔ Persecution Complex (+5 when hurt) + Vengeance one-shots all game with minimal investment.
  • ✔ Vantage lets her strike first on enemy phase while kept low.
  • ✘ Requires babysitting her HP at a damaged-but-safe threshold.
  • ✘ Risky against burst damage that overshoots the window.
