Caspar von Bergliez / 卡斯帕尔 — Build Menu V2

Black Eagles student. Stays on Crimson Flower; auto-joins Silver Snow; recruitable elsewhere (Strength 10 + Brawling C). No Crest, HP 55% / Str 45% / Spd 45% / Lck 40% growths, personal Born Fighter (adjacent enemies −10 Avoid).
Proficiencies: Axe (boon), Brawl (boon); Bow (BANE), Reason (BANE), Authority (BANE); no budding talent. Sword/Lance/Faith/Heavy Armor/Riding/Flying neutral.
Gender note: War Master is male-only — Caspar qualifies, and both its requirements (Axe + Brawl) are his boons, so it is the ideal terminal.

Build 1 — War Master (战神) male-only

Path: Noble → Brawler → Grappler → War Master. Cert: Axe A + Brawl A — both boons, so a light grind for a Crit +20 + Quick Riposte class.

Mastery detours: Death Blow [⛏ HARVEST — Brigand, Axe C boon] +6 Str on initiate — his single most important detour. Fierce Iron Fist [⛏ HARVEST — Grappler, Brawl boon, on path]. Unarmed Combat [Brawler, on path]. SKIP Hit +20 (Bow bane) — Born Fighter already lowers enemy Avoid.

5 skills: Fistfaire · Death Blow · Quick Riposte · Crit +20 (class) · Born Fighter (personal).

  • ✔ Crit +20 + gauntlet brave (2 hits) + Death Blow = brutal burst; Quick Riposte walls enemy phase.
  • ✔ Born Fighter shreds adjacent enemy Avoid so his crits land.
  • ✘ Move 5, no Canto — fix with Stride / Linhardt Warp / pair-up.
  • ✘ 45% Spd can fall behind on Maddening — feed Speed boosters.

Build 2 — Grappler (格斗家, terminal)

Path: Noble → Brawler → Grappler (stay). Cert: Brawl — his boon; an Advanced endpoint that skips the long Axe+Brawl A double grind.

Mastery detours: Death Blow [⛏ HARVEST — Brigand, Axe C boon]. Fierce Iron Fist [⛏ Grappler, on path].

5 skills: Fistfaire · Death Blow · Fierce Iron Fist · Tomebreaker · Born Fighter (personal).

  • ✔ Dual unarmed attacks + good Str at a fraction of War Master's investment.
  • ✔ Solid Advanced-tier endpoint if you don't reach endgame.
  • ✘ No Crit +20 or Quick Riposte — strictly weaker than War Master.
  • ✘ Same Move-5 problem without War Master's payoff.
