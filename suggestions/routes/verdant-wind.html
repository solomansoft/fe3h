Route 4 — Verdant Wind (Golden Deer) — Maddening

Run 4 of 4. The finale and hardest run, on full Maddening. The most lore-complete route. Golden Deer is flier-light, so this plan covers the air gap with native Wyvern users. DLC is OPTIONAL. The recommended default (you do not own the DLC) is the all-Golden-Deer non-DLC roster in the next section — it un-reserves Lysithea (native GD) and uses Marianne as Dancer. The DLC Ashen Wolves roster that follows is kept as an optional path for owners. House: Golden Deer · Difficulty: Maddening Byleth class this run (R3 — distinct): Enlightened One (神祖) — Byleth's unique class, auto-unlocked at end of Ch.10, zero certification cost (Sword + Gauntlets + magic, Swordfaire, Sacred Power mastery buffs adjacent allies). A low-effort but genuinely strong sword/support seat on Maddening, and distinct from R1 (Mortal Savant), R2 (Wyvern Lord), R3 (War Master). All four Byleth classes differ.

Byleth gender/birthday: either gender (Enlightened One is gender-neutral; the planned Byleth × Claude S-support works for M and F). Birthday is a free calendar pick — choose an empty week.

🔒 Route-exclusive highlight (verified)

  • Claude — playable only on Verdant Wind (NPC/enemy elsewhere, never controllable). Edelgard is again an enemy/major boss here.

Maddening tip: GD's three opening bow users (Claude, Leonie, Ignatz) give ranged safety from turn 1 — lean on it.

Byleth — sword/support carry

  • Gender & birthday (recommended): Female · Harpstring Moon (M5), wk 1. Female is required for the Byleth × Claude S-support (Claude romances female Byleth only). Early M5 birthday → Golden Deer Bracelet (Charm +2) in both parts — equip on your Dancer / gambit user.

Claude 🔒 ⭐ — flying bow lord

  • Class: Noble → Archer → Wyvern Rider → Barbarossa (unique flying-bow, his signature) — or generic Wyvern Lord (#1) / Sniper→Bow Knight (#2).
  • Training: Bow A + Flying A (both boons), tutor Axe to pop budding (Diamond Axe), Lance D/C for Wyvern gate, Authority.
  • Loadout: Brave/Killer Bow; Hunter's Volley / Curved Shot; Bowfaire, Death Blow, Hit+20, Avo+10; Leicester Knights/flying battalion + Retribution.
  • ⚠️ Flags: none — +20% EXP personal levels him fast.

Lorenz — mounted mage-cavalry

  • Class: Noble → Mage → Warlock → Dark Knight (#1, magic cavalry — keeps Speed, Crest adds Mt). Paladin (#2) for a physical lance route.
  • Training: Reason A + Riding A; keep Lance (boon); Authority.
  • Loadout: Thoron/Ragnarok + Tempest Lance; Black Tomefaire, Lancefaire, Movement+1, Hit+20, Defiant Mag; cavalry/Reason battalion + Stride.

Leonie ⭐ — bow cavalry raider

  • Class: Commoner → Soldier → Cavalier → Paladin → Bow Knight (#1, best Bow Knight candidate — Move 8, 1–3 range). Falcon Knight (#2) / Wyvern Lord (#3) for flying.
  • Training: Bow A + Riding A + Lance A; master Brigand (Death Blow), Archer (Hit+20), Pegasus (Darting Blow).
  • Loadout: Killer/Brave Bow; Hunter's Volley / Tempest Lance; Bowfaire, Death Blow, Darting Blow, Hit+20; cavalry battalion + Stride/Retribution.

Hilda ⚠️ — flying axe carry

  • Class: Noble → Fighter → Brigand → Wyvern Rider → Wyvern Lord (#1, best physical class, Axefaire + Avo+10).

  • Loadout: Killer/Brave Axe; Smash / Helm Splitter; Axefaire, Death Blow, Avo+10, Darting Blow; flying/axe (Goneril) battalion + Retribution.
  • ⚠️ Flags: lazy-base — concentrate her limited instruction on Axe/Flying only.

Marianne ⚠️ — mounted healer-nuker

  • Loadout: Physic/Aura/Nosferatu + Blutgang/Levin Sword/Frozen Lance; Fiendish Blow, Movement+1, Defiant Mag; healing/cavalry battalion + Blessing gambit. Spirit Dust + Seraph Robe.

Raphael ⚠️ — brawler tank-DPS

  • Class: Commoner → Fighter → Brigand → Grappler → War Master (#1, Crit+20 + Quick Riposte).

  • Loadout: Killer/Aura Knuckles; War Master's Strike / Fierce Iron Fist; Fistfaire, Death Blow, Quick Riposte, Crit+20; high-Hit battalion + Battalion Wrath+Vantage.
  • ⚠️ Flags: very low Spd — feed Speedwings, lean on Quick Riposte; keep away from mages. Doubles as a frail-caster bodyguard.

Ignatz — mobile archer / debuffer

  • Class: Commoner → Fighter → Archer → Sniper (#1, debuff bow + Hunter's Volley). Bow Knight (#2) needs Riding tutoring.
  • Training: Bow A + keep Sword (boon); tutor Reason to pop Seal Strength (must-have debuff); Authority; avoid Riding unless Bow Knight.
  • Loadout: Killer/Brave Bow; Hunter's Volley / Break Shot; Bowfaire, Hit+20, Seal Strength, Death Blow; archer battalion + Onslaught/Stride.
  • ⚠️ Flags: modest offense — a setup/utility unit.

🪙 Stealer this route: Leonie (non-DLC) / Yuri 🧩 (DLC)

Run one Thief-master to Steal rare items off enemies — Dark Seal, stat boosters, consumables — the only repeatable way to farm them (Steal is Speed-gated: only foes with lower Spd). Leonie's Spd 60 out-speeds almost any foe — detour her through Thief (Sword C, Lv10), master it, and Steal stays equippable in her Bow Knight build. With DLC, Yuri natively runs Thief → Trickster — the ideal built-in stealer. See team overview · facts: Thief.

Recruitment plan & timing

Maddening rewards early flier/mobility coverage; GD is flier-light, so: - Native GD (Claude, Lorenz, Leonie, Hilda, Marianne, Raphael, Ignatz, Lysithea) — no recruiting; push Hilda + Leonie toward flying/mounted and Lorenz + Lysithea toward mounted magic for mobility. - Non-DLC cross-house recruits (Shamir, Catherine, Alois) — recruit early in Part 1 to fill the 12-slot roster; combat-ready Catherine/Shamir need almost no tutoring. - Ashen Wolves DLC (Yuri, Balthus, Constance, Hapi) — recruitable into Part 1 only, each after the corresponding Cindered Shadows chapter (Constance Ch.2, Balthus Ch.4, Hapi Ch.5, Yuri Ch.6). They join ~Lv3 and become instant top-tier — recruit early on Maddening.
