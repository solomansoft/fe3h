Route 1 — Azure Moon (Blue Lions) — Normal

Run 1 of 4. The warm-up: Blue Lions is the strongest house and the best place to learn the systems on Normal. Rules: ../team-rules.md · Overview: ../team_recommendation_overview.md Tags: 🔒 route-exclusive · ⚠️ tutor/special-care · 💃 dancer · ⭐ top carry · (core)/(flex) roster role Per-unit deep detail: ../../character_recommendation/

House: Blue Lions · Difficulty: Normal Byleth class this run (R3 — distinct): Mortal Savant (魔剑士) — a deliberately fun/unconventional sword+magic build. Per R3, the easy Normal run is exactly where Byleth's quirky class belongs (the build files rate Mortal Savant inefficient — low Spd, "investment rarely justifies returns" — which is why it goes on Normal, never Maddening). Byleth's optimized classes are saved for R2 (Wyvern Lord), R3 (War Master), R4 (Enlightened One). All four Byleth classes differ. Byleth gender/birthday: either gender (Mortal Savant is gender-neutral; the planned Byleth × Dimitri S-support works for M and F). Birthday is a free calendar pick — choose an empty week. Roster size: 13 (10 core + 3 flex).

🔒 Route-exclusive highlights (verified)

  • Dimitri — playable only on Azure Moon (enemy / final boss on the other three routes).
  • Dedue — Blue-Lions-only. Leaves end of Ch.12, returns Ch.16 only if you finished his paralogue "War for the Weak" before Ch.12 ends.
  • Gilbert (Gustave) — Blue-Lions-only; auto-joins Ch.13 (no recruitment).

Roster composition (R9 — built as a team)

  • Damage split: Physical-leaning house, deliberately balanced. Magic: Mercedes, Annette (+ Byleth's Mortal Savant black magic) punish armor/low-Res. Physical: Dimitri, Felix, Sylvain, Ingrid, Ashe, Dedue, Catherine. Two dedicated mages is enough for Normal.
  • Healing/staff: Mercedes (Gremory, primary) + Annette (Faith chip) cover healing; Mercedes carries Physic/Warp.
  • Fliers / mobility: Ingrid (Falcon Knight) + Catherine (Wyvern Lord, flex) + Leonie (Falcon, flex) give air; Sylvain (Paladin) + Dimitri (cavalry line) give Canto. Strong Stride/Rally enabling from Annette's Rally set.
  • Ranged / effective coverage: Ashe (Sniper, anti-flier bows) + Mercedes/Annette magic (anti-armor) + Byleth's Levin Sword/Thoron (1–2 range). Bow coverage vs fliers is thin → Ashe is the anchor; Leonie's flex bow line backs him up.
  • Gambits/Authority: Byleth + Dimitri carry the best battalions; Annette's Rallies amplify the line. Normal difficulty means gambits are insurance, not a crutch.
  • Composition picks flagged below: Felix → Swordmaster (not his #1 War Master), Sylvain → Paladin (#2), Annette → Gremory (#2), plus the two flex reuse-variants (Catherine → Wyvern, Leonie → Falcon).

Budding talents (hidden ★) — what to train to unlock

Each unit has at most one hidden talent. How it blooms: there is no fixed letter rank — just keep training that skill until its three ★ fill (in practice ~C–B rank); the game pops a notification when it awakens, the skill becomes a strength, and you get a free ability/combat art. Most of this roster's budding talents are off their planned build, so only a couple are worth chasing.

Battalions for this roster

Battalion eligibility is gated by each unit's Authority rank (the battalion's "Lvl") + movement type (only fliers take flying battalions). Availability is route-specific (Kingdom stock on this route) and refreshes at the Ch.8 and Ch.14 guild restocks. Objective data (Lvl, bonuses, gambit, unlock) → facts/battalions; full progression walkthrough → Azure Moon guide. Repair endurance at the guild before every mission.

💡 Equip an early battalion on EVERY deployed unit from Ch.1 — even a cheap Lvl E one. Battalions don't change growth rates, but their flat +Atk/+Prot/+Res/+Hit/+Avo make a unit hit harder and survive, so it can stay on the field, take more kills, and level up faster — that's the real "stat-growth" lever. Using the Gambit also trains the unit's Authority (raising the rank gate for better battalions later). Lvl-E options anyone can equip turn 1: Jeralt's Mercenaries (free, +75 End — give it to whoever needs to survive), Seiros Mercenaries / Holy Monks / Church of Seiros Soldiers, Kingdom Infantry / Lance Co. Buy a handful from the guild the moment it opens and slot them broadly; upgrade to the table below as Authority climbs.

💃 Dancer this route: Dorothea

🪙 Stealer this route: Dimitri (Felix = alt)

Run one Thief-master to Steal rare items off enemies — Dark Seal (Dark Mage/Bishop cert), stat boosters, consumables — the only repeatable way to farm them. Dimitri is the best-value pick: his endgame Great Lord is auto-granted (Ch.16, no cert), so he has the most spare mastery bandwidth of the carries; Sword is a boon → trivial Thief cert (Sword C, Lv10); Spd 50% clears the Speed-gate vs most foes; and he's deployed every map, so Steal is always on the field. Do the Thief mastery in Part 1, then just equip Steal in Great Lord. Felix is the alternative (slightly higher Spd 55%) if you'd rather keep the lord on pure offense. ⚠️ Ashe's Locktouch is not Steal — it only opens chests/doors. See team overview · facts: Thief.

Recruitment plan & timing

Blue Lions lacks magic and fliers, so recruit to fill those gaps. Master rule for this run: Byleth's only scarce resource is Faculty Training, and it is reserved 100% for Reason (the off-boon Mortal Savant gate). So recruit by building support to lower each gate (~20%/level) and by meeting stat gates from free/passive sources (Choir = Byleth Authority, gardening = Str, deploying = Authority/Charm) — never by diverting Faculty Training onto a recruit's gate. ⚠️ No tea parties before Ch.4 (they unlock via Ferdinand's "Tea for Two" quest, ~Blue Sea Moon/July; else auto Ch.13) — so early support (Ch.1–3) runs on greetings, gifts, lost items, shared meals, and group tasks.
