Route 1 — Azure Moon (Blue Lions) — Normal

Run 1 of 4. The warm-up: Blue Lions is the strongest house and the best place to learn the systems on Normal. Rules: ../team-rules.md · Overview: ../team_recommendation_overview.md Tags: 🔒 route-exclusive · ⚠️ tutor/special-care · 💃 dancer · ⭐ top carry · (core)/(flex) roster role Per-unit deep detail: ../../character_recommendation/

House: Blue Lions · Difficulty: Normal Byleth class this run (R3 — distinct): Mortal Savant (魔剑士) — a deliberately fun/unconventional sword+magic build. Per R3, the easy Normal run is exactly where Byleth's quirky class belongs (the build files rate Mortal Savant inefficient — low Spd, "investment rarely justifies returns" — which is why it goes on Normal, never Maddening). Byleth's optimized classes are saved for R2 (Wyvern Lord), R3 (War Master), R4 (Enlightened One). All four Byleth classes differ. Byleth gender/birthday: either gender (Mortal Savant is gender-neutral; the planned Byleth × Dimitri S-support works for M and F). Birthday is a free calendar pick — choose an empty week. Roster size: 13 (10 core + 3 flex).

🔒 Route-exclusive highlights (verified)

  • Dimitri — playable only on Azure Moon (enemy / final boss on the other three routes).
  • Dedue — Blue-Lions-only. Leaves end of Ch.12, returns Ch.16 only if you finished his paralogue "War for the Weak" before Ch.12 ends.
  • Gilbert (Gustave) — Blue-Lions-only; auto-joins Ch.13 (no recruitment).

Roster composition (R9 — built as a team)

  • Damage split: Physical-leaning house, deliberately balanced. Magic: Mercedes, Annette (+ Byleth's Mortal Savant black magic) punish armor/low-Res. Physical: Dimitri, Felix, Sylvain, Ingrid, Ashe, Dedue, Catherine. Two dedicated mages is enough for Normal.
  • Healing/staff: Mercedes (Gremory, primary) + Annette (Faith chip) cover healing; Mercedes carries Physic/Warp.
  • Fliers / mobility: Ingrid (Falcon Knight) + Catherine (Wyvern Lord, flex) + Leonie (Falcon, flex) give air; Sylvain (Paladin) + Dimitri (cavalry line) give Canto. Strong Stride/Rally enabling from Annette's Rally set.
  • Ranged / effective coverage: Ashe (Sniper, anti-flier bows) + Mercedes/Annette magic (anti-armor) + Byleth's Levin Sword/Thoron (1–2 range). Bow coverage vs fliers is thin → Ashe is the anchor; Leonie's flex bow line backs him up.
  • Gambits/Authority: Byleth + Dimitri carry the best battalions; Annette's Rallies amplify the line. Normal difficulty means gambits are insurance, not a crutch.
  • Composition picks flagged below: Felix → Swordmaster (not his #1 War Master), Sylvain → Paladin (#2), Annette → Gremory (#2), plus the two flex reuse-variants (Catherine → Wyvern, Leonie → Falcon).

Deployment roster (13)

Byleth — sword/magic hybrid (core)

  • Gender & birthday (recommended): Female · Harpstring Moon (M5), wk 1. Female is required for the Byleth × Dimitri S-support (Dimitri romances female Byleth only). Early M5 birthday → Blue Lion Brooch (Charm +2) in Part 1 and again in Part 2 — equip on your Dancer / gambit user.
  • This run's class (R3 — distinct): Mortal Savant (#fun pick). Myrmidon → Mage/Monk (bank Reason) → Mortal Savant (Sword A + Reason B+). Sword + Black magic, Swordfaire, 6 Mov foot. On Normal the low Spd doesn't matter — enjoy ranged Thoron + Sword of the Creator melee.
  • Mastery detour: Brigand → Death Blow; Mage → Fiendish Blow (suits the magic side).
  • Training: Sword (free) + Reason; Authority to A.
  • Loadout: Sword of the Creator + Levin Sword + Thoron/Bolganone; Swordfaire, Black Tomefaire, Death Blow, Hit+20, White Magic Avo+20; Authority battalion + Retribution.
  • ⚠️ Flags: low Spd in Mortal Savant (won't double) — irrelevant on Normal. Fallback Enlightened One auto at Ch.10 if you want zero effort, but keep Mortal Savant so it stays distinct from R2–R4.

Dimitri 🔒 ⭐ — lance lord / front carry (core)

  • Class: Soldier → Cavalier → Paladin → Great Lord (unique, #1).
  • Loadout: Areadbhar + Killer Lance; Battalion Wrath + Battalion Vantage + Death Blow + Lancefaire; battalion + Retribution (the famous enemy-phase combo — overkill on Normal but teaches it).
  • See character file.

Dedue 🔒 — armor wall (core)

  • Class: Commoner → Soldier → Armored Knight → Fortress Knight (#1, his identity).
  • ⚠️ Flags: very low Spd (enemy-phase tank). Keep adjacent to Dimitri (story Linked Attack).

Gilbert 🔒 ⚠️ — tank / adjutant (flex)

  • Class: Fortress Knight (#2, cheapest) or → Great Knight (#1, Heavy Armor A + Riding) for mobility.
  • ⚠️ Flags: late-joiner, under-leveled (Ch.13 ~Lv26). Lowest investment priority — emergency wall / adjutant.

Felix ⭐ — sword DPS (core) (composition pick)

  • Class: Myrmidon → Mercenary → Swordmaster (#2 — his low-effort sword build; save the War Master identity for the axe units this run). Master Brigand (Death Blow) + Archer (Hit+20).
  • ⚠️ Flags: Authority bane (cheap battalion); Faith bane (never a healer).

Sylvain — lance cavalry DPS (core) (composition pick)

  • Class: Cavalier → Paladin (#2 — Swift Strikes + Move 8 + Canto, no banes). (#1 Wyvern Lord reserved as the air slot here goes to Ingrid/Catherine.)
  • ⚠️ Flags: Bow bane.

Ingrid — flying lance / magic-tank (core)

  • Class: Soldier → Pegasus Knight → Falcon Knight (#1, "unquestionably best").
  • Mastery detour: Pegasus → Darting Blow.
  • ⚠️ Flags: weak early bases — feed kills early; a project, not a tutor-emergency.

Mercedes — healer (core)

  • Class: Monk → Priest → Bishop → Gremory (#1, doubles staff + spell uses).
  • ⚠️ Flags: frail, very low Spd — never melee; feed Spd boosters. A-support with Annette = mutual +3 Mt (build them together).

Annette — black mage + rally (core) (composition pick)

  • Class: Monk → Mage → Warlock → Gremory (#2, low-investment pure mage; keeps her Rally utility). (#1 Wyvern Lord exists but the magic seat is more valuable to this roster.)
  • ⚠️ Flags: none. Build alongside Mercedes for the mutual +3 Mt.

Ashe — mobile archer (core)

  • Class: Fighter → Archer → Sniper (#1, Hunter's Volley). Personal Locktouch = free chests/doors.
  • Mastery detour: Brigand → Death Blow.
  • ⚠️ Flags: none (native).

Dorothea 💃 (recruit) — dancer (core) (reuse-variant)

  • This run's class (R3): Dancer (win the White Heron Cup) — her #2 build. (Her #1 Gremory is reserved for R2.) Songstress aura heals adjacent allies ~10%/turn.
  • ⚠️ Flags: none (weak offense irrelevant in the Dancer role).

Catherine (recruit) ⭐ — sword/air bruiser (flex) (reuse-variant)

  • This run's class (R3): Wyvern Lord (#2 — her flying reuse-variant). She is featured natively in R3 as Swordmaster (#1), so she rides a wyvern here to stay distinct (R3). On Normal you can skip Thunderbrand and just enjoy a flying axe/sword bruiser. (Alternative: bench her — she is flex, so dropping her costs nothing and preserves R3 diversity.)
  • Recruit: trivial on Blue Lions (~Ch.4, no stat gate beyond meeting her). Joins Lv11 Swordmaster, combat-ready.
  • See character file.

Leonie (recruit) — flying lance/bow (flex) (reuse-variant)

  • This run's class (R3): Soldier → Pegasus Knight → Falcon Knight (#2 flying line) — her reuse-variant. She is featured in R4 as Bow Knight (#1), so she flies here instead. A second mobile flier rounds out the Normal roster.
  • Recruit: Strength 13 + Lance/Bow proficiency raised; easy on Blue Lions by ~Ch.6.
  • See character file.

💃 Dancer this route: Dorothea

Recruited cross-house (Charm 25 + Authority B), slotted straight into the White Heron Cup. High Charm + Sword competence makes her a strong dance/dodge unit, and her Songstress aura heals adjacent allies. This is her reuse-variant build (#2 Dancer); in R2 she switches to her #1 Gremory. Distinct from R2 (Petra), R3 (Manuela), R4 (Yuri / Marianne).

Recruitment plan & timing

Blue Lions lacks magic and fliers, so recruit to fill those gaps: - Dorothea — meet Charm 25 + Authority B, recruit by ~Ch.4–6, then win the White Heron Cup. (F!Byleth can free-recruit her via support.) - Catherine — Knights of Seiros, recruitable ~Ch.4 on BL; joins combat-ready (flex sword/wyvern body). - Leonie — recruit ~Ch.6 (Strength 15 + Lance C, per her character file); flex flier. - Everyone else is native Blue Lions.

Azure Moon is the first of the two easy routes, so per R5 it carries the one allowed diversity swap (Dedue × Mercedes). The paired-ending count is still maximized with real support chains; everything else is best-by-default.

  • Ashe × Catherine — real support chain (Catherine is in Ashe's support list, with a paired ending); used to maximize the count. Ashe's best (Byleth) and Catherine's signature (Shamir, absent here) are both unavailable, so this is the best available for both.
