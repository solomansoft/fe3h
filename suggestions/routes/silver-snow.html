Route 3 — Silver Snow (Black Eagles → Church) — Hard → Maddening

Run 3 of 4. The Church route: Black Eagles students without Edelgard, fighting for the Church. Difficulty ramps from Hard into Maddening — treat stat boosters as scarce and "rich-train" ~6 carries. Rules: ../team-rules.md · Overview: ../team_recommendation_overview.md Tags: 🔒 route-exclusive · ⚠️ tutor/special-care · 💃 dancer · ⭐ top carry · (core)/(flex) Per-unit deep detail: ../../character_recommendation/

House: Black Eagles (Church-aligned) · Difficulty: Hard → Maddening Byleth class this run (R3 — distinct): War Master (战神, M Byleth) — optimized raw-damage build (Axefaire + Fistfaire + Crit+20 + Quick Riposte), leaning on Byleth's Brawl strength. (F!Byleth → use Falcon Knight instead — still distinct from the other three routes.) Distinct from R1 (Mortal Savant), R2 (Wyvern Lord), R4 (Enlightened One). All four Byleth classes differ. Byleth gender/birthday: Male = War Master (default plan) — the one route where gender drives the class: War Master is Male-only, Falcon Knight is Female-only (pick Female only if you'd rather fly). The Byleth × Seteth S-support works for both. Birthday is a free calendar pick — choose an empty week. Roster size: 13 (8 core + 5 flex).

🔒 Route highlights (verified) — "impossible to obtain on Crimson Flower"

  • Seteth, Catherine, Cyril — auto-join at the start of Ch.12 ("To War").
  • Flayn — recruited earlier in Part 1 and stays.
  • These four are exactly the units you cannot get on Crimson Flower, so this is the run that features them (🔒 = central + CF-locked).
  • Edelgard and Hubert are NOT playable here. Other faculty (Shamir, Hanneman, Manuela, Alois) are available.

Roster composition (R9 — built as a team, Maddening discipline)

  • Damage split: Balanced for Maddening. Magic: Lysithea (Gremory, premier nuke), Hanneman (Dark Knight, flex), Flayn (Bishop chip) handle armor/low-Res. Physical: Byleth (Falcon Knight, flying lance), Seteth (Wyvern), Catherine (Swordmaster), Shamir (Bow Knight), Cyril (Wyvern), Ferdinand (Wyvern), Alois (War Master).
  • Healing/staff: Flayn (Bishop) + Manuela (faculty healer, now Dancer but keeps Faith for emergency Physic) + Linhardt (flex, Warlock can be swapped to Bishop). On Maddening this is the thinnest area → keep Flayn alive and parked.
  • Fliers / mobility: Byleth (Falcon Knight) + Seteth + Cyril + Ferdinand give a strong air corps; Shamir/Hanneman (mounted) give Canto; Manuela's Dance is the tempo enabler. Excellent Maddening mobility.
  • Ranged / effective coverage: Shamir (Bow Knight, anti-flier, 1–3 range) + Lysithea/Hanneman magic (anti-armor) + Cyril (bow option). Strong effective coverage in every direction.
  • Gambits/Authority: Byleth + Catherine + Seteth carry the strongest battalions; gambits matter more here than R1/R2 — bring rattle/Stride. Manuela's Dance is the key action-economy lever for the Maddening spike.
  • Composition picks flagged below: Ferdinand → Wyvern Lord (#2, chosen over his #1 Paladin to add a third Maddening flier AND keep his R2 Paladin distinct); Shamir → Bow Knight (#1, also her distinct build vs R4 Sniper); Hanneman → Dark Knight (#2, mounted-mage composition pick).

💃 Dancer this route: Manuela

A minority-but-sound pick: ~60% Speed and a Sword boon (Dancer's Sword Avo+20 makes her a fine dodge unit), and because this roster is otherwise healer-light, her ability to Physic-heal between refreshes is a real bonus the other dancers can't match. Converting her redundant combat slot into the refresher is the highest-value use of her seat. Distinct from R1/R2/R4 dancers (Dorothea / Petra / Yuri or Marianne).
