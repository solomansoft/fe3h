Recruitment / 招募

  • Requires the Expansion Pass (Wave 3 DLC). / 需要购买 扩展通票（第三波 DLC）。
  • Recruitable into any house / any route, as early as Chapter 3, by selecting the recruit option during the exploration phase — no stat/skill requirements for Byleth. / 在 探索阶段 选择招募选项即可加入 任意班级／任意路线，最早可于 第 3 章 招募，对贝雷特 无属性／技能要求。
  • On Black Eagles (Crimson Flower), her paralogue becomes available only post-timeskip in the war phase. / 若选黑鹫（红花线），其外传需到战争篇（时间跳跃后）才开放。
  • Paralogue / 外传：The Secret Merchant（秘密商人 / 安娜与耶利查外传）。
  • Note: Anna cannot build support with any character (except basic interactions). / 注：安娜 无法与任何角色建立支援。
