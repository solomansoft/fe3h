Flame Emperor / 烈焰皇帝

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Alias of a playable house leader 火焰纹章：风花雪月 — 可操作学级领袖的别名

Alias relationship / 别名关系

The Flame Emperor is the masked alias used by Edelgard von Hresvelg, leader of the Black Eagles and princess of the Adrestian Empire. The armored Flame Emperor persona uses Agarthan technology to distort her voice and conceal her identity while she works against the Church of Seiros. 烈焰皇帝是艾黛尔贾特·冯·弗雷斯贝尔古所使用的蒙面别名；她是黑鹫学级的领袖、阿德剌斯忒亚帝国的皇女。烈焰皇帝这一披甲身份利用阿加尔塔（Agartha）的技术扭曲她的声音、隐藏其真实身份，以暗中对抗赛罗司教团。
