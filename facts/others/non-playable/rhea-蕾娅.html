Rhea / 蕾娅 (Rhea)

A reference entry for the non-playable character Rhea in Fire Emblem: Three Houses (Nintendo Switch, 2019).

《火焰之纹章 风花雪月》（Nintendo Switch，2019 年）中的非玩家角色蕾娅参考条目。

Role / 职责

  • EN: Archbishop and supreme leader of the Church of Seiros. She also serves as the head (principal) of the Officers Academy at Garreg Mach Monastery. She invites the protagonist to teach at the academy.
  • 中文： 赛罗司圣教会的大主教（大司教）兼最高领袖，同时担任加尔古·玛库大修道院士官学校的校长。她邀请主角到士官学校任教。

Affiliation / 所属

  • EN: Church of Seiros; Garreg Mach Monastery. As archbishop she is also recognized as the foremost authority on the continent of Fódlan.
  • 中文： 赛罗司圣教会；加尔古·玛库大修道院。作为大主教，她也被视为芙朵拉大陆上的最高权威。

Title / 称号

  • EN: Archbishop (of the Church of Seiros).
  • 中文： （赛罗司圣教会的）大主教 / 大司教。

Identity & Aliases / 身份与别名

  • EN: Rhea's true identity is widely documented as Saint Seiros, the founder of the Church of Seiros, and a Nabatean (one of the children of the goddess Sothis). This is a central revelation of the game's story.
  • 中文： 蕾娅的真实身份在资料中被广泛记载为赛罗司圣教会的创始者——圣赛罗司（Saint Seiros），并且是纳巴提（女神索希丝的眷属/子嗣）之一。这是游戏剧情的核心揭示之一。

Voice Actors / 配音

  • EN: English – Cherami Leigh; Japanese – Kikuko Inoue.
  • 中文： 英文配音 — Cherami Leigh；日文配音 — 井上喜久子（Kikuko Inoue）。

Notes / 备注

  • EN: Rhea is portrayed as kind and motherly toward the academy's staff and students, but unforgiving toward those who turn against the Church.
  • 中文： 蕾娅对士官学校的教职员与学生如慈母般和善，但对与教会为敌之人则有着严厉无情的一面。
