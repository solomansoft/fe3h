Death Knight / 死亡骑士

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Alias of a playable unit (boss appearance) 火焰纹章：风花雪月 — 可操作角色的别名（首领登场）

Alias relationship / 别名关系

The Death Knight is the masked alias used by Jeritza (Jeritza von Hrym / Emile von Bartels). He serves the Empire and appears as a recurring boss before he can be obtained as a playable unit. 死亡骑士是耶里查（Jeritza von Hrym / Emile von Bartels）所使用的蒙面别名。他效力于帝国，在能够成为可操作角色之前，作为多次出现的首领登场。

This page is a short cross-reference only. For full stats, growths, personal skill, proficiencies and recruitment, see Jeritza's own unit page (Jeritza is a DLC / special character available on certain routes). 本页仅为简短的交叉引用。完整属性、成长率、个人技能、擅长项与招募条件，请参阅耶里查的角色页面（耶里查为DLC / 特殊角色，在特定路线可加入）。

Objective boss combat facts / 首领战斗客观信息

These are the documented, confirmed traits of the Death Knight when fought as a boss. 以下为作为首领出现时已确认记录的特征。

Field 项目 English 简体中文
Class 兵种 Death Knight (personal class) 死亡骑士（个人兵种）
Equipped weapon 装备武器 Scythe of Sariel (Lance; a regalia, lower weight and higher Hit / Crit than a Steel Lance) 萨列尔之镰（枪；圣遗物，重量低于钢枪、命中与暴击更高）

Note 备注: Because of Counterattack, the Death Knight can strike back at any range, including against ranged attackers. The Scythe of Sariel can be obtained by defeating him in the paralogue "The Face Beneath" with Caspar. 由于拥有反击，死亡骑士可在任意距离反击，包括对远程攻击者。在外传「面具之下」中由卡斯帕尔击败他可获得萨列尔之镰。
