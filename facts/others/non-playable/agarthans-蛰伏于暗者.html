The Agarthans / Those Who Slither in the Dark / 蛰伏于暗者

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Antagonist faction 火焰纹章：风花雪月 — 反派势力

Naming / 译名

EN: This faction is known in English as "Those Who Slither in the Dark", and its members are called Agarthans (descendants of the ancient civilization of Agartha). The commonly used Simplified Chinese fan name is 蛰伏于暗者. Alternate Chinese renderings seen in Chinese guides include 黑暗中蠢动者 / 黑暗蠢动者 (e.g. GamerSky); members are also informally called 地底人 ("subterranean people"). 中文： 本势力的英文名为 "Those Who Slither in the Dark"，其成员被称为 Agarthans（古代文明亚加迪亚的后裔）。常用的简体中文译名为 蛰伏于暗者。中文攻略中可见的其他译法包括 黑暗中蠢动者 / 黑暗蠢动者（如游民星空）；成员亦被俗称为 地底人。

Who they are / 他们是谁

EN: - The Agarthans are the descendants of an ancient, technologically advanced civilization (Agartha) that once flourished. They possess advanced technology far beyond the rest of Fódlan. - In the distant past their civilization was nearly destroyed in conflict with the goddess Sothis and the Nabateans (the goddess's children). The survivors were driven underground. - Their members live among the surface population in secret, often by assuming the identities of (and posing as) other people, and operate from hidden bases. - Their overarching goal is revenge against the goddess and her children and the reclamation of the surface world.

中文： - 蛰伏于暗者是一个曾经繁荣的远古高度发达文明（亚加迪亚）的后裔，掌握着远超芙朵拉其他地区的先进科技。 - 在遥远的过去，他们的文明在与女神苏谛斯及纳巴泰人（女神眷属）的冲突中几近毁灭，幸存者被驱逐至地下。 - 其成员潜藏于地表人群之中，常冒用 / 伪装成他人身份，并以隐秘据点为基地行动。 - 他们的总体目标是向女神及其眷属复仇，并夺回地表世界。

Historical involvement / 历史介入

EN: - The Agarthans aided Nemesis (the King of Liberation): they helped him raid the goddess's resting place and plunder the body of Sothis, from which the Sword of the Creator was forged. They are also tied to the creation/distribution of the power behind the Ten Elites' Crests and Relics. - In the game's present day they manipulate events in Fódlan from the shadows and are connected to the Adrestian Empire during its war.

中文： - 蛰伏于暗者曾协助涅梅西斯（解放王）：帮助他袭击女神安息之地、掠夺苏谛斯的遗体，创造之剑即由此锻造而成。他们也与十杰之纹章与圣遗物背后力量的产生 / 流布有关。 - 在游戏的当代剧情中，他们于暗中操弄芙朵拉的局势，并在战争期间与阿德剌斯忒亚帝国有所关联。
