Lord Arundel / Volkhard von Arundel / 亚伦德尔

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Non-Playable Character 《火焰纹章：风花雪月》— 非可操作角色

English

Identity

  • Name: Volkhard von Arundel, holder of the title Lord Arundel.
  • Status: A noble of the Adrestian Empire. He was granted the title of Lord Arundel after his sister Anselma became a consort of Emperor Ionius IX.
  • Family relationship: Through his sister Anselma, he is the maternal uncle (by blood) of Edelgard von Hresvelg.

Identity relationship with Thales

  • The original Volkhard von Arundel was killed and subsequently impersonated by Thales, the leader of the Agarthans / "Those Who Slither in the Dark." Thales uses the identity of Lord Arundel as his disguise within the Adrestian Empire.
  • For Thales himself (the Agarthan leader, his role, and his boss/combat data), see thales-塔列斯.md. This file intentionally does not duplicate Thales's content.

Background (documented facts only)

  • After the Insurrection of the Seven, Arundel and his niece Edelgard fled to the Kingdom of Faerghus.
  • Later he returned to the Adrestian Empire with Edelgard.

Cross-references

简体中文

身份

  • 姓名： 沃尔卡·冯·亚伦德尔（Volkhard von Arundel），拥有亚伦德尔公（Lord Arundel）头衔。
  • 身份： 阿德剌斯提亚帝国的贵族。在其妹（姊）安塞尔玛成为皇帝伊欧尼乌斯九世的妃子后，他被授予亚伦德尔公的头衔。
  • 亲属关系： 通过其妹（姊）安塞尔玛，他是埃黛尔贾特·冯·弗雷斯贝尔古的血缘上的舅父。

与塔列斯的身份关系

  • 原本的沃尔卡·冯·亚伦德尔已被杀害，其后由阿加尔塔人 / "蛰伏于暗者"（剥削黑暗者 / Those Who Slither in the Dark）的首领塔列斯（Thales）所假冒。塔列斯以"亚伦德尔公"的身份作为其在阿德剌斯提亚帝国内的伪装。
  • 关于塔列斯本人（阿加尔塔首领、其职责及其 Boss / 战斗数据），请见 thales-塔列斯.md。本文件刻意不重复塔列斯的内容。

背景（仅列已确认事实）

  • "七贵族之乱"（Insurrection of the Seven）之后，亚伦德尔与其外甥女埃黛尔贾特一同逃往法嘉斯王国。
  • 此后他又带着埃黛尔贾特返回阿德剌斯提亚帝国。
