Kronya / 科罗妮娅

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Non-Playable Character (Antagonist / Boss) 《火焰纹章：风花雪月》— 非可操作角色（反派 / Boss）

English

Identity

  • Name: Kronya
  • Alias: Monica von Ochs ("Monica") — she impersonates a Black Eagles student named Monica von Ochs who had gone missing.
  • Affiliation: Agarthans / "Those Who Slither in the Dark."
  • Origin: Agartha.

Role

  • A member of Those Who Slither in the Dark who infiltrates Garreg Mach Monastery's Officers Academy disguised as the student Monica von Ochs.
  • Appears as an enemy boss in the main story.

Combat (documented facts only)

  • Class: Assassin.
  • Class skills (Assassin):
  • Locktouch — allows the user to open doors and chests without keys.
  • Stealth — makes it more difficult for foes to target the unit.
  • Keen Intuition — grants Avoid +30 when in combat with a foe 2 or more spaces away.
  • Personal Ability: Agarthan Technology — reduces damage caused by adjacent foes by 3.
