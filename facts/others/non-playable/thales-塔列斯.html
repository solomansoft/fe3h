Thales / 塔列斯

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Non-Playable Character (Antagonist / Boss) 《火焰纹章：风花雪月》— 非可操作角色（反派 / Boss）

English

Identity

  • Name: Thales
  • Role/Title: Leader of the Agarthans / "Those Who Slither in the Dark."
  • Disguise: Assumes the identity of Lord Arundel (Volkhard von Arundel), a noble of the Adrestian Empire. (For the original Volkhard and the identity relationship, see arundel-亚伦德尔.md.)

Role

  • The leader of Those Who Slither in the Dark, commanding members such as Solon and Kronya.
  • Appears as an enemy boss in the penultimate chapter of the main story.

Combat (documented facts only)

  • Class: Agastya — an enemy-exclusive class that is Thales's personal class. It is a customized variant of the Dark Bishop class, specializing in black magic, dark magic, and authority.

身份
