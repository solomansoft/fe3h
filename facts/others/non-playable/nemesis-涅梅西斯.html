Nemesis / 涅梅西斯

Fire Emblem: Three Houses — Non-playable character (lore figure / route final boss) 火焰纹章：风花雪月 — 非可操作角色（背景人物 / 路线最终首领）

Overview / 概要

EN: Nemesis is a major lore figure and one of the overarching antagonists of Fire Emblem: Three Houses. He is known as the King of Liberation and is revered in Fódlan's official history as the hero who saved the continent. He was the first wielder of the Sword of the Creator and bore the Crest of Flames. In the game's present day he is resurrected and appears as the final boss of the Verdant Wind route (Golden Deer). 中文： 涅梅西斯是《风花雪月》中的重要背景人物，也是贯穿全作的主要反派之一。他被称为解放王，在芙朵拉的官方历史中被尊为拯救大陆的英雄。他是创造之剑的初代持有者，身负炎之纹章。在游戏的当代剧情中，他被复活，并作为金鹿线（翠风之章）的最终首领登场。

Background / 背景

EN: - In Fódlan's official, widely taught history, Nemesis is celebrated as the King of Liberation: a hero who, wielding the Sword of the Creator, protected the continent. - The true account revealed in-game is darker: Nemesis was the leader of a band of thieves/an army who, with the help of the Agarthans ("Those Who Slither in the Dark"), raided the Holy Tomb / Zanado and plundered the body of the goddess Sothis. From her remains the Sword of the Creator was forged, and through her blood he gained the Crest of Flames. - He led an army against Seiros (Rhea) during the War of Heroes. - He was accompanied by the Ten Elites, warriors who likewise received the power of the Nabateans' blood (Crests) and wielded Hero's Relics made from the bodies of the goddess's children.

中文： - 在芙朵拉广为流传的官方历史中，涅梅西斯被颂扬为解放王：一位手持创造之剑、守护大陆的英雄。 - 游戏中揭示的真相更为阴暗：涅梅西斯本是一支盗贼团 / 军队的首领，在亚加迪亚人（"蛰伏于暗者"）的协助下袭击了圣墓 / 扎纳德，掠夺了女神苏谛斯的遗体。创造之剑由她的遗骸锻造而成，他也借由她的血脉获得了炎之纹章。 - 在英雄战争中，他率军与塞罗斯（雷亚）为敌。 - 与他并肩作战的是十杰——这些战士同样获得了纳巴泰人之血的力量（纹章），并使用以女神眷属遗体制成的英雄遗产（圣遗物）。
