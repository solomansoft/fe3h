Recruitment / 招募条件

  • Recruitable during the Academy Phase by talking to him at the monastery from Chapter 5 onward once conditions are met.
  • Requirement: Byleth Level 10 (no weapon-skill threshold). Building support rank lowers the threshold.
  • Auto-joins in Chapter 12 on Church-aligned paths (Silver Snow / Azure Moon / Verdant Wind). Cannot be recruited / does not join on the Crimson Flower route.
  • 第5章起，满足条件后可在修道院与其对话「劝诱」招募。
  • 条件：主角等级达到 10 级（无武器技能要求）。提升支援等级可降低要求。
  • 在教团方路线（银雪 / 苍月 / 翠风）第12章自动加入；「红花之章」无法招募。
