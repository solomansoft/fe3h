Recruitment / 招募条件

  • Recruitable during the Academy Phase by talking to her at the monastery from Chapter 6 onward.
  • Requirement: Byleth Level 15 (no stat/skill prerequisite). The standard support discount applies.
  • Auto-joins on the Silver Snow route in Part 2 (around Chapter 12) without manual recruitment.
  • 第6章起，在学院篇可于修道院与其对话「劝诱」招募。
  • 条件：主角等级达到 15 级（无属性/技能前置要求）。可享标准支援折扣。
  • 在「银雪之章」后篇（约第12章）会自动加入，无需手动招募。
