Recruitment / 招募条件

  • Recruitable from Chapter 8 at the monastery by selecting "Recruit."
  • Requirement: Byleth Level 15 (no specific skill threshold). The 20%-per-support-rank discount applies.
  • Available on all routes during the Academy Phase.
  • 第8章起可在修道院通过「劝诱」招募。
  • 条件：主角等级达到 15 级（无特定技能要求）。每提升一个支援等级可享约 20% 的等级折扣。
  • 学院篇所有路线均可招募。
