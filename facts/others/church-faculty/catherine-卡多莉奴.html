Personal Skill / 个人技能

  • Fighting Spirit / 斗志
  • Effect: The unit takes 5 less damage when no battalion is assigned, or when the battalion's endurance is 0.
  • 效果：未配备兵团或兵团耐久为 0 时，自身受到的伤害 -5。

Strengths / Weaknesses / Budding Talent / 擅长 · 不擅长 · 隐藏才能

  • Strengths / 擅长: Sword, Brawling (highest: Sword A) / 剑、格斗（最高：剑 A）
  • Weaknesses / 不擅长: Reason / 理性魔法
  • Budding Talent / 隐藏才能: None / 无
  • Note: Wields the Hero's Relic Thunderbrand; with her Major Crest she can use the combat art Foudroyant Strike. / 持有英雄遗产「雷霆破灭剑」，凭借大纹章可使用战技「雷光一闪」。

Recruitment / 招募条件

  • Church recruit (not a student): no Charm/skill threshold.
  • Requirement: Byleth Level 15. High support rank can lower the requirement.
  • Blue Lions & Golden Deer: recruitable manually at the monastery from around Chapter 4 onward during the Academy Phase once the level condition is met.
  • Black Eagles: auto-recruited in Chapter 12 if you side with the Church (Silver Snow). Cannot be recruited on the Crimson Flower route (she is an enemy).
  • 教团成员（非学生）：无魅力/技能门槛。
  • 条件：主角等级达到 15 级。高支援等级可降低要求。
  • 蓝狮 / 金鹿：约第4章起在学院篇可于修道院手动「劝诱」招募。
  • 黑鹰：若与教团结盟（银雪），第12章自动加入；「红花之章」无法招募（成为敌人）。
