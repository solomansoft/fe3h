Battalions / 营队（兵团）

Objective reference for battalions: the Authority rank gate, movement type, stat bonuses, Gambit, and when/where each unlocks. / 营队客观资料：权威等级门槛、移动类型、属性加成、立体战技（Gambit）以及解锁时机与来源。

Core mechanics / 核心机制

  • A battalion is assigned to one unit and grants stat bonuses + a Gambit. / 每支营队装备给一名单位，提供属性加成 + 一个立体战技（Gambit）。
  • A battalion's "Lvl" = the minimum Authority rank the unit needs to equip it (unit's Authority must be ≥ the battalion's Lvl). Movement type must also match — infantry / armored / cavalry / flying. / 营队的 “Lvl” = 装备所需的最低权威等级（单位权威须 ≥ 营队 Lvl）；移动类型也必须匹配 —— 步行 / 重装 / 骑兵 / 飞行。
  • Using a Gambit trains the unit's Authority (like a weapon trains its skill) and levels the battalion (up to Lv5), improving its bonuses. / 使用立体战技会训练该单位的权威（如同武器训练对应技能），并提升营队等级（至 Lv5），强化加成。
  • Endurance falls as the commanding unit takes damage; at 0 the battalion (and its Gambit) drops for that battle. Repair with gold at the Battalion Guild before each fight. / 耐久随指挥单位受伤下降，归零则该战中营队（及其战技）退场。战前在营队公会用金币修复。
  • Carries over to New Game+. All hired battalions transfer to NG+ at their current level (with built-up battalion EXP) — unlike gold and items, which do not carry over. ⚠️ Units reset to Lv1 and Authority ranks reset, so a high-Lvl battalion can't be re-equipped until you rebuild that unit's Authority to its gate. Tip: before finishing a run, buy up battalions — they carry forward (and surplus ones can be sold for gold in NG+). (Verified: Game8, GameFAQs.) / 可继承至「新游戏+」：所有已雇佣营队按当前等级（含已积累的营队经验）带入 NG+；而金币与道具不继承。⚠️ NG+ 中单位重置为 1 级、权威等级清零，高 Lvl 营队须重新练满权威才能装备。建议：通关前多买营队，可带入下一周目（多余的还能卖钱）。

Acquisition & unlock timing / 取得与解锁时机

How a battalion becomes available is gated by three verifiable anchors (exact per-battalion appearance weeks are not cleanly documented — treat as "available by/around"). / 营队是否可用由三个可核实的锚点决定（具体每营队的出现周次无明确记载，按“大致可用”理解）。

  1. Battalion Guild unlock / 营队公会解锁 — complete Leading the Charge → Bolstering Forces (early Part 1). Bolstering Forces (Jeralt) grants 3 free battalions. / 完成《率先出击》→《增援部队》（第一部前期）即解锁公会；《增援部队》（杰拉尔特）赠送 3 支免费营队。
  2. Route-specific stock / 路线限定库存 — the guild stocks battalions tied to the route's homeland (Kingdom battalions on Azure Moon, etc.). / 公会库存与路线本土势力绑定（苍月之路出王国营队，等等）。
  3. Restocks / 补货 — the guild restocks during Chapter 8, and again near the timeskip: Chapter 14 (Azure Moon / Silver Snow / Verdant Wind) or Chapter 12 (Crimson Flower). / 公会在第 8 章补货，并在时间跳跃前再次补货：第 14 章（苍月 / 苍焰之纪 / 翠风之路）或第 12 章（红花之路）。
  • Authority rank gates equipping, not stock — a battalion can sit in the guild before a unit can use it. Professor Level raises Funds/Activity Points (how much you can buy/repair), but does not itself expand the guild roster. / 权威等级限制的是“能否装备”，而非库存 —— 营队可能先在公会出现、之后单位才够权威装备。教授等级提升资金/活动点（决定能买/修多少），但不直接扩充公会名录。
  • Lord / house battalions are auto-granted — after the timeskip each route's lord receives a unique Lord battalion automatically (e.g. Dimitri → King of Lions Corps). Don't buy one for the lord. / 领主 / 家系营队自动获得 —— 时间跳跃后各路线领主自动获得专属营队（如蒂米特利 → 雄狮之王军团）。无需为领主购买。

⚠️ Verify / 待核实: exact in-game month/week each specific hireable battalion first appears is not reliably documented. The dependable unlock signals are route + the Ch.8 / Ch.14(12) restock + the unit's Authority rank. / 具体营队首次出现的精确月/周无可靠记载；可靠信号为路线 + 第8/14(12)章补货 + 单位权威等级。

Battalion data / 营队数据

Unlock column / “解锁”列 = the verifiable availability anchor: source (route/free) + earliest restock window. Authority Lvl is the equip gate. / “解锁”列 = 可核实的可用锚点：来源（路线/免费）+ 最早补货窗口；权威 Lvl 为装备门槛。

Bonuses use the game's shorthand: Phys = physical Atk, Mag = magic Atk, Prot = physical Def, Res = magic Def, Cha = Charm. / 加成缩写：Phys 物理攻击、Mag 魔法攻击、Prot 物防、Res 魔防、Cha 魅力。

Move-type rule: flying battalions only fit flying units; cavalry battalions only fit mounted units; armored only fit armored classes; everything marked any fits foot/most units. / 移动规则：飞行营队仅限飞行单位；骑兵营队仅限骑乘单位；重装仅限重装职业；标 any 者步行／多数单位通用。

Gambit glossary / 立体战技说明

  • Stride / 突进 — target-area allies gain Movement +5 for the rest of the turn (~2 uses, 1-tile range). The premier repositioning/rush tool. / 范围内友军本回合移动 +5（约 2 次，1 格范围）。最强的重新部署/突进手段。
  • Retribution / 反击 — allies in area can counter at any range for 5 turns. Great on an enemy-phase tank or a defensive carrier. / 范围内友军 5 回合内可无视距离反击。适合敌方回合坦克或防御载体。
  • Wave Attack / 波动攻击 (King of Lions Corps only) — repeatable lance wave, strong vs armor. / （仅雄狮之王军团）可重复的枪击波，克制重甲。
  • Assault Troop / 突击部队 — solid all-purpose offensive gambit. / 全能型进攻战技。
  • Disturbance / 扰乱 — debuffs enemies in the area (lowers their Avo, etc.); common on cheap E-rank battalions. / 削弱范围内敌人；常见于廉价 E 级营队。
  • Onslaught / 强攻 — area attack gambit, no damage falloff; reliable offense. / 范围攻击战技，输出稳定。
  • Line of Lances / 枪阵 & Group Lance Attack / 集团枪击 — AoE physical strikes (lance-flavored). / 范围物理打击。
  • Group Flames·Ice·Lightning / 集团炎·冰·雷 & Resonant Flames·Ice·Lightning / 共鸣炎·冰·雷 — magic AoE gambits (Resonant lines hit a wider area). / 魔法范围战技（共鸣系范围更大）。
  • Fusillade / 齐射 & Flash-Fire Arrows / 速射 — ranged bow AoE gambits. / 弓系范围战技。
  • Blessing / 祝福 & Resonant White Magic / 共鸣白魔 — heal/support gambits for faith carriers. / 治疗辅助战技。
  • Impregnable Wall / 铁壁 — big defensive buff to allies in area; pairs with armored tanks. / 范围防御强化，配合重装。
  • Blaze / 烈焰, Recovery Roar / 恢复怒吼, Lure / 诱敌, Assembly / 集结, Linked Horses / 连环骑, Dance of the Goddess / 女神之舞: situational utility/AoE gambits. / 情境型辅助 / 范围战技。
