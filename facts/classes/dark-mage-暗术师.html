Notes / 备注

  • IMPORTANT / 重要: In-game, Dark Mage is classified as an Intermediate (中级) class, not Advanced (上级). It is grouped with the dark-magic line because it shares the male-only restriction and the Dark Seal (暗魔法考试准考证) certification path that leads to Dark Bishop. / 游戏内暗术师实为「中级」职业而非「上级」。因其同样仅限男性、并使用暗魔法考试准考证转职、且为暗主教的前置职业，故与暗魔法系列归类在一起。
  • Male-only foot dark-magic class. / 仅限男性的步行暗魔法职业。
