Weapons that benefit (best first) / 受益武器（按收益降序）

In Three Houses any weapon can be equipped regardless of class; below are the weapons that gain this class's bonuses (proficiency skill-XP + faire / stat synergy), in descending order of overall benefit. / 在《风花雪月》中任何职业都可装备任意武器；下列为能获得本职业加成（熟练度经验＋武器强化/属性契合）的武器，按总体收益降序排列。

  1. Axe / 斧 & Gauntlets (Brawling) / 拳套 — both faired (Axefaire + Fistfaire, +5) + Crit +20 / 双强化(+5)＋必杀+20

Mastery / 精通奖励

  • Mastery Ability / 精通技能: Quick Riposte / 迅捷反击（HP 较高时反击必定追击）

Notes / 备注

  • 步行职业，专精斧与格斗。 Foot class specializing in axes and brawling.
  • 男性专属。 Male-only.
  • 拥有大师职业中突出的成长率加成与必杀能力。 Notable growth bonuses and high critical capability among Master classes.
