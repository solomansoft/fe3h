Notes / 备注

  • A foot (non-mounted) hybrid class combining sword combat with black magic.
  • 步行型混合职业，兼具剑术近战与黑魔法。
  • Master tier class; requires Master Seal and is generally taken at Level 30+.
  • Mortal Savant 的精通技能在不同资料站有分歧（Game8 误记为 Seal Resistance），经 Serenes Forest 与社区交叉核对确认为 Warding Blow。
